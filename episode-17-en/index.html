    AI-WAR: Comic Episode 17 - Nightmare Nine-Headed Dragon

    AI-WAR: Comic Episode 17 - Nightmare Nine-Headed Dragon

    【Scene: Dragon Kingdom’s Magnificent and Solemn Temple】

    Inside the temple, there is a multi-headed dragon anomaly about 5 stories high. Beside it are several strong monks, each 2 meters tall. Some hold chains to bind the Nine-Headed Dragon, while others wield various weapons, showing a high degree of coordination in their advanced kung fu attacks, alternating between offense and defense.

    (Visual: The main hall of the Great Thunder Temple in the Dragon Kingdom. A massive five-story tall Nine-Headed Dragon is bound by multiple heavy chains. Several two-meter tall, muscular monks are engaged in a fierce battle using advanced kung fu. The scene is heroic.)

    Nine-Headed Dragon: Great Thunder Temple! Why do you bald monks keep giving me trouble? I’m just hungry and eat a few children every day. Is it necessary to fight me for 81 days and nights without rest?

    An old Abbot speaks up. Abbot Wukong: Vile beast! Good and evil are irreconcilable. Not to mention you’ve eaten thousands of children; your very existence is something we must eliminate.

    (Visual: Abbot Wukong, holding a fly-whisk, stands before the massive shadow of the Nine-Headed Dragon, confronting it with a dignified expression.)

    Nine-Headed Dragon: Tiny humans, I’m willing to talk to you not because I’m afraid of you. It’s the law of the jungle—the strong eat the weak. Since I’m strong and you’re weak, it means you should be eaten by me. I can fight you thousand bald monks for another 81 days. Do you still have that combat power? Let me go now, and I can act as if nothing happened. I’ll keep looking for food, and you keep chanting sutras and worshipping this useless stone every day. We can live in peace.

    Wu Nan: Master, we can’t hold on much longer. Why don’t we settle with him? It’s better to resolve an enmity than to keep it. After all, the ones being eaten aren’t our people. Just drive him away. Humans have their limits. If this goes wrong, we might all die here.

    Suddenly, Wu Qiang’s voice comes from outside the temple.

    (Visual: At the temple entrance, Wu Qiang appears heroically against a backdrop of war and dust.)

    Wu Qiang: Master, I’m back. Talking to this kind of beast is a mistake. Only a dead beast is a good beast.

    Wu Qiang (Shouting): Great Majestic Heavenly Dragon! World-Honored Ksitigarbha! Prajna Buddhas! Prajna-paramita-hum!

    Wu Qiang was originally at the temple entrance, 2 meters tall. He suddenly grows to over 4 meters. His muscles explode with a metallic steel luster, and he holds a specially made “Data Zen Staff.” The staff grows larger as Wu Qiang transforms. With a thunderous roar, he leaps into the air and accurately strikes one of the dragon’s heads with a single blow.

    (Visual: Wu Qiang’s body expands and erupts. His muscles show a metallic steel luster. The Data Zen Staff in his hand grows larger. He leaps high and strikes a dragon head heavily.)

    Nine-Headed Dragon (Roaring): Where did you come from, you bald monk? Aren’t you afraid of me? Behold my Giant Dragon Roar, Dragon Punch Burst—the strongest strike!

    The head hit by Wu Qiang instantly explodes into a mass of filth. The remaining 8 heads spit out a massive energy wave at A-Qiang and immediately follow up with a punch, sending Wu Qiang flying into a wall, creating a huge hole. The other monks are all shocked.

    (Visual: A dragon head explodes. The remaining eight heads simultaneously spit out a huge energy wave hitting Wu Qiang, blowing him into a wall and creating a large crater.)

    Abbot Wukong (Shouting): My disciple A-Qiang! Why did you jump straight into battle? Even if you are strong, humans and anomalies are on different levels. Why are you so impulsive?

    Wu Nan (Shouting): Senior Brother Wu Qiang, let’s stop fighting, okay? The opponent is too strong. Let’s give up.

    The neck where the dragon head was blown off twitches, and a new head regenerates instantly.

    Nine-Headed Dragon: You bald monk, you have some strength. It hurt a bit, and it did some damage to me, but it’s still useless.

    (Visual: The flesh at the Nine-Headed Dragon’s injured neck wriggles, instantly regenerating a new, hideous dragon head.)

    In the crater, Wu Qiang’s steel muscles have fine cracks. There is some damage, but it’s not serious.

    Wu Qiang (Shouting): Oh, so it hurts, does it? Then I’m going to use my “Serious Punch”!

    Wu Qiang shouts: “Great Majestic Heavenly Dragon plus the blessing of the Wrathful Vajra! Can you withstand such a Data Zen Staff attack?”

    (Visual: Wu Qiang walks out of the rubble, his entire body’s muscles covered in fine cracks, his eyes blessed by the Wrathful Vajra. The Data Zen Staff is surrounded by light shadows of advanced code as precise as the Rust language.)

    (Wu Qiang has already touched the highest level of human wisdom, and his physical reinforcement has reached the level of steel. This time, through daily meditation, he connects to the wisdom of the Buddha Realm, bringing in the blessing of the Wrathful Vajra. His power upgrade is like evolving from the Basic language to Rust—both power and speed have evolved ten-thousand-fold.)

    (Visual: Wu Qiang turns into an uncatchable stream of light. The Nine-Headed Dragon explodes continuously in an instant and finally dissipates into a cloud of black, foul gas.)

    Wu Qiang stands alone where the Hydra was, panting heavily. His fellow disciples are too stunned to speak. When they realize what happened, they erupt into cheers.

    However, Wu Qiang cannot respond. His whole body is covered in blood, the price of overusing power that exceeds his own.

    After the Nine-Headed Dragon dies, a girl named Lu Qing arrives. She says she comes from a Buddhist and Taoist lineage in Wanwan. Recently, a “Mo-Shin-A” (forest spirit) seal is about to break. she asks Wukong to strengthen the seal or kill the Mo-Shin-A. Her lineage is dying because everyone in Wanwan longs for AI, neglecting their original heritage. But the seal is failing. After defeating the dragon, Wu Qiang doesn’t rest for a moment. Hearing about the dangerous Mo-Shin-A, he immediately leaves a letter and boards a plane to the small island of Wanwan to eliminate the spirit.

    (Visual: Inside the airplane cabin, Wu Qiang is covered in bandages like a mummy, sleeping and recovering from his injuries in his seat.)

    Wu Qiang is wrapped like a mummy on the plane, sleeping to rest and heal.

    【Scene: On the Plane to Wanwan】

    Wu Qiang: Zzz… Being able to eat is a blessing, and being able to sleep is also a blessing.

    Li Xiaolong: Hello, Senior Brother Wu Qiang.

    (Visual: Wu Qiang looks on in surprise as Li Xiaolong crawls out of the overweight suitcase he brought.)

    Wu Qiang: Hey, why are you here?

    Li Xiaolong: You thought you could just leave a letter and run away? Do you think Abbot Wukong knows nothing? He told me to hide in your luggage and come along. I was wondering if I’d be discovered, but I didn’t expect my weight to be nothing to you, Senior Brother.

    Wu Qiang: How can you be so light? Wasn’t my luggage filled with large rocks for body training?!! Don’t tell me you moved the rocks!

    Li Xiaolong: Yes, I moved them all.

    Wu Qiang: What are you here for? I can handle this alone. You’ll only hold me back. I don’t need anyone’s help.

    (Visual: Wu Qiang is moved to tears, crying loudly while discussing the Master’s wisdom with Li Xiaolong on the plane, with a sea of clouds outside the cabin window.)

    Li Xiaolong: Well, Abbot Wukong knew everything. The Abbot sent me precisely to “hold you back.” The Abbot said that rocks are dead things—how can dead things hold you back? If Wu Qiang wants to become stronger, then Xiao Li, go quickly, use any method to follow him, and treat it as training with your Senior Brother. Although you will certainly hold him back, your role is to hold him back to help him become stronger.

    Wu Qiang (Crying): Master is truly so wise. He realized that weighted training no longer worked for me and arranged for you to help. I’m so touched.

    Li Xiaolong: Master’s wisdom is truly unfathomable.

    ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 17 Addendum

    🐉 Nine-Headed Dragon

    • Attributes: A giant dragon with nine heads.
    • Food: Loves to eat children.

    Great Thunder Temple of Dragon Kingdom

    • Wukong: The “Abbot Wukong” of the ancient temple. Rumor has it that in his early years, he obtained an embodied AI data fragment of the legendary “Monkey King”.
    • Wu Qiang: After fighting continuously for 81 days, he boarded a plane to Taiwan wrapped like a mummy to guard the seal.
    • Wu Nan: Wu Qiang’s junior brother. He is incredibly strong but extremely pessimistic. His catchphrase is “It’s no use,” “Give up,” “Let’s settle,” earning him the title of the “Emperor of Giving Up” on the battlefield.
    • Li Xiaolong: A lay disciple of the Great Thunder Temple, determined to become stronger to prove himself.

    Wanwan Temple

    • Lu Qing: A girl from a Buddhist and Taoist lineage (Guandu Temple) in Wanwan. Guandu Temple mainly worships Mazu (Heavenly Holy Mother).

    🧘 Source of Buddhist Wisdom (Buddha-Source/Yuan)

    • Worldview: There exist high-dimensional energies such as Gods, Buddhas, and Demons, the most powerful of which is called the “Buddha-Source.”
    • Characteristics: Buddhists call it “Yuan” (affinity/fate). This is a data frequency that cannot be forced; only those who “enlighten” themselves to specific logic can receive it. The saying “Only save those with whom there is an affinity” refers to the successful pairing of code interfaces.

When the terrifying dual-bodied anomaly “Little Apple” sneaks into Pang Hu and Glasses’ apartment, space begins to collapse… Facing the absolute darkness of a door that won’t open, who can escape alive?
