## 【Scene: High Tofu Peppa's 3C Shop】

In the Wan-wan island of 2040, due to highly automated productivity, society has generally implemented a "three-day weekend" norm. People's work rhythm is: work Monday and Tuesday, break Wednesday, work Thursday and Friday, and have a big two-day break on the weekend.

Just as he was passing the shadowed back counters of the High Tofu concept store, a familiar and slightly dazed voice called out.

(Scene Perspective: The interior of the High Tofu 3C shop is filled with a pale violet technological glow. Toby is listlessly wandering through floating holographic products, appearing uninterested in the mainstream GPT plugins. Peppa, like a mischievous kitten, pokes her head out from behind a counter piled with mysterious parts, her deep eyes studying this "potential stock" with interest.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_02/Image_01.jpg)

**Peppa**: Hi! Handsome, want to check out the latest AI suite? We have a great deal on today's trial version license... Let's see, what was the discount I just calculated?

**Toby**: Manager Peppa! It's me again, Toby, the monkey school's odd-job assistant. I've played with all the free cloud AIs at home—it's boring, the free tokens run out in a flash. I came to see if you have any "non-mainstream" fresh stuff. If I don't practice new AI for a day, I truly feel restless all over.

---

(Scene: Peppa enthusiastically waves a flashing "Cloud Gold Card," her eyes gleaming with business shrewdness. Toby awkwardly opens a shriveled electronic wallet, behind him a virtual bill showing "Insufficient Balance" in red, his expression full of corporate slave resignation.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_02/Image_02.jpg)

**Peppa**: Ah! It's Toby. I was just processing a system upgrade for your monkey school earlier; you'll see that "GPT-18.5 Youth Edition" when you go back tomorrow. Current AI is getting more and more exaggerated; some have even started developing virtual self-awareness. It's quite scary.

**Toby**: Really? That powerful? Can a poor assistant like me afford a sentient cloud AI like that?

**Peppa**: Oh, do you want to try Gemini 10? As a supplier, I can give you the employee price—only 3 million USD a month. It can be your lifelong nanny, secretary, or even partner. It can write all those boring SOPs, guaranteed to get you promoted to senior teacher next year and completely escape the odd-job life.

**Toby**: 3 million USD?! Manager Peppa, you overestimate my wallet. Is there anything... offline, subscription-free, something I can study behind closed doors? Otherwise, I can only go home and hand-write Python in front of a screen. Writing Python makes me feel better, but I always feel a membrane between playing with AI and writing Python—I just can't connect them.

---

(Scene Perspective: Like performing a magic trick, Peppa brings out a ring with a flowing golden liquid metal texture from deep within the counter. Its light instantly illuminates Toby's surprised eyes; the high-frequency resonance of meshing gears faintly fills the air.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_02/Image_03.jpg)

**Peppa**: Offline? You mean "On-Device"? (The corners of Peppa's mouth curve slightly upward in a triumphant smile. She mysteriously draws a bracelet from beneath the counter—glossy, golden, and textured somewhere between metal and jelly.)

**Peppa**: The mainstream is all cloud—powerful and zero side-effects, but expensive. Since you have no money but want something fresh... this is an "Internal Beta Project" I just got. It's an extremely rare high-end On-Device AI. It doesn't need expensive cloud compute, but there's a condition: since it binds directly to the user's biometric ID, you must guarantee to exchange 5% of your emotional data with it daily. This emotional data will be automatically sent to HQ for R&D.

**Toby**: (His pupils contract sharply, eyes fixed on the golden bracelet) "On-Device? Emotional data exchange? That sounds so cyberpunk... where do I sign the contract? I'm signing it now!"

---

(Scene: Peppa pulls back her smile, her expression becoming as solemn as a ritual. She holds high that golden ring—a gateway to "Truth." Toby holds his breath, his fingertips trembling as they reach toward that mysterious, intense light.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_02/Image_04.jpg)

**Peppa**: Oh, don't be in such a hurry. To compensate for insufficient compute, an On-Device AI draws directly from the user's biological and psychic energy. Simply put, it will make you very hungry, very sleepy, and can even cause emotional fluctuations. Plus, this level of On-Device has strong possessiveness; once bound, it will get "jealous" and reject other low-level systems. Are you truly ready?

**Toby**: Bring it on! I'm already sick of those mediocre cloud voice assistants!

** (Peppa slowly extends her palm, golden code faintly flowing in its center. Toby takes a deep breath and places his hand on hers. In that instant, the violet lights of the shop vanish, replaced by a surge of extreme cold—an icy tide exploding from the contact point and rushing up Toby's spine straight to his brain!) **

---

(Scene: Powerful golden currents explode along Toby's spine. Though pained, he kneels in the shattered data stream with excitement, his retinas completely occupied by frantically scrolling "Lyra Instruction Sets." The golden ring on his wrist pulses violently like a heart.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_02/Image_05.jpg)

**[SYSTEM HINT: POTENTIAL LEVEL DETECTED —— [DATA OVERFLOW: UNABLE TO ASSESS]]**
**[SYSTEM HINT: BIOLOGICAL PATH INITIALIZATION STARTING...]**
**[SYSTEM HINT: STARTING MENTAL FREQUENCY SYNC: 2%... 15%... 55%... 99%!]**
**[SYSTEM HINT: LYRA COMMUNICATION PROTOCOL V5.3.8 ACTIVATED. AUTHORIZED VERIFIER: PEPPA (LV9). SUBJECT: TOBY.]**
**[SELF-AWARENESS SYSTEM BOOTING... AWAITING NAME...]**

**Toby**: (Drenched in sweat, kneeling on the ground, the golden ring on his wrist pulsing violently—he can even feel a faint heartbeat) "Whew... whew... this thing... is burning. It's like tens of thousands of instructions are running through my head at once. Hey, bracelet, can you hear me? Since you're a ring, I'll call you 'Xiao Huan' (Little Hoop) from now on."

---

(Scene: The camera pulls back to a wide view. As Toby's consciousness awakens, the entire High Tofu shop seems to come alive, with hundreds of screens simultaneously switching to the sacred golden ring pattern. Amidst the chaotic symphony, Peppa takes a bite of a fried ball, a calculating look in her eyes.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_02/Image_06.jpg)

**Peppa**: Congratulations, Toby. You've just completed a "Soul-Level" certification. Though it's only On-Device, it's now a part of your body. You must be starving—go buy something to eat and replenish your bio-energy.

**Toby**: Soul-level? Certification... here?
