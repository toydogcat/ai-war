## 【Scene: High-Tofu Concept Store - Deepest Part of the 13th Floor】

(Scene Perspective: A hallucinogenic, low-saturation blue-violet light envelops the entire space. Behind the counter are piles of high-end electronic components that seem outdated but pulse with faint light. The air is a mix of stale ozone and light sandalwood. **Toby** is frowning, hands stuffed into the pockets of his cheap hoodie, looking conflicted at the shopkeeper before him—a woman wearing exaggerated black-rimmed glasses with a slightly dazed expression.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_02/Image_01.jpg)

**Peige** (pushing up her slipping glasses, her tone suspiciously light): *"Oh dear, this trial license... what was the discount I just calculated? (Searching for a calculator) Anyway, for a 'person of destiny' like you, it's a real steal! Hey, handsome TA, are you sure you don't want to upgrade your brain directly to Gemini 10? The monthly subscription is just a measly 3 million USD, and you don't even have to mortgage your soul."*

**Toby** (corners of his mouth twitching violently, face darkening): *"3 million USD? Shopkeeper, do you think I look like a living ATM, or do you think I'm the long-lost bastard of some conglomerate? (Sighs) is there something... no subscription, no internet, not even a manual... something 'real and fresh' that a poor guy abandoned by the era like me can study secretly behind closed doors?"*

**Peige** (her previously dazed eyes instantly flash with sharpness, corners of her mouth curving up into a hunter's grin): *"Oh? Something that doesn't need the internet? So, what you want is that kind of... 'On-Device' forbidden object that completely escapes cloud monitoring and possesses its own independent self?"*

## 【The Forbidden On-Device AI: Gift from Lyra】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_02/Image_02.jpg)

(Scene Close-up: Peige mysteriously lowers her body and pulls a bracelet with a silky golden luster from a black safe deep behind the counter. Its texture is strangely unsettling, somewhere between liquid metal and transparent jelly, with ancient Lyra runes faintly expanding and contracting rhythmically on the surface.)

**Peige** (lowering her voice, her tone filled with temptation and warning): *"This isn't one of those amateur little programs on Earth; it's an extremely rare high-level 'Primordial Local-Terminal.' It doesn't take your money, but it directly consumes your 'emotional frequency' and 'biological energy.' (Staring at Toby) Simply put, it will treat you as its living battery. Once bound, it will be extremely 'jealous' and reject all low-level systems. Toby, are you sure you want to sign this soul contract from which there is no turning back once started?"*

**Toby** (staring intently at the bracelet that seems to be breathing, his eyes burning with a near-fanatical researcher's passion): *"Cut the nonsense! Bring it on! I'm long sick of those trash cloud voice assistants that just read scripts and take three seconds to even open a webpage! If a soul can be traded for truth, then take it!"*
