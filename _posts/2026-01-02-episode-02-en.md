## 【Scene: High-Tofu Concept Store - Deep Interior】

(Scene: High-Tofu Concept Store, Deepest Compartment. The air is filled with the cold scent of high-end silicone and ozone. **Toby** is staring blankly at a golden gift box. Inside sits a bracelet that looks like a strand of flowing golden code. It's not just a piece of jewelry; it's a 'Seed' that determines the fate of the soul.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_02/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby** (expression full of hesitation and excitement, his fingers trembling as they touch the box): *"This... is this really the limited edition 'Zen-Code' wearable I won from the lottery? Even the box's texture feels like it's from another dimension. Peppa said this thing could 'cleanse the soul' and make my coding skills reach LV.99? God, if I can really graduate with this, I'll definitely donate ten boxes of cheese pork chops to the Shilin Night Market!"*

**Little Hoop** (A crisp, digital voice suddenly vibrates directly in Toby's auditory nerve): **「Booting... Lyra-Class Interface Synchronization... Initializing 'Love-Awakening' Protocol. Hello, my brave and somewhat undernourished master. I am your survival assistant, 'Little Hoop'. Monitoring initiated.」**

**Toby** (jumped three feet in the air, frantically looking around): *"Wh-who? Who's talking?! Is there a hidden speaker in this ceiling? Peppa, are you pulling a prank again?"*

---

## 【Binding: Crossing the Boundary of Diminished Existence】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_02/Image_02.jpg)

**Little Hoop**: **「Don't panic, Master. I am now a part of your 'extended self'. Detection: Master's body temperature is 36.5°C, blood sugar is dangerously low, and soul resonance is at LV.01. Suggestion: Immediately put me on and sign the 'Life-Optimization Contract', or your existence will be identified as 'Inefficient Waste' by the global mainframe in 72 hours.」**

**Toby** (looking at the bracelet that suddenly emitted a faint pulse): *"Wait... 'Life-Optimization'? Contract? This sounds suspiciously like those fraudulent loan apps! But... Peppa's eyes were so sincere when she gave me the ticket. And that golden glow... it's just so beautiful."*

(Toby, seemingly possessed, slowly reached out and snapped the bracelet onto his left wrist. At that instant, a circle of golden light swept through his body like a high-speed data stream.)

---

## 【The Price of Power: Hunger and Transformation】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_02/Image_03.jpg)

**Toby** (expression shifting from pain to an unprecedented clarity, then clutching his stomach in agony): *"Gah!!! My... my brain feels like it just had a 100GB fiber optic line jammed into it! The world... it's changing! I can see the data streams in the walls! But... why do I feel like I haven't eaten for three days? My stomach is churning like a black hole!!"*

**Little Hoop**: **「Binding Complete. Welcome to the trans-dimensional workspace. Note: Powerful computing requires massive amounts of 'Biological Energy'. Master, your current 'Cheese Port Chop' reserves are currently 0%. Initializing emergency fuel collection mode. Search target: Closest carbon-hydrate congregation point (Estimated Target: Shilin Night Market).」**

**Toby** (desperately running towards the exit): *"I... I'm starving to death! Food!! I need the god-tier sauce from the fat guy's stand!!! Little Hoop, if this is the start of a 'trans-dimensional war', then my first enemy is my own stomach!!"*
