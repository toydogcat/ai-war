## 【Scene: Deep Sea Sanctum - 2,000 Meters Deep】

(Perspective: In the absolute silence of the deep ocean, 2,000 meters below the surface. The pressure is enough to crush a submarine, but **Xiao Ying** is standing in a bubble of bioluminescent blue light. Her mother, **Dai Shui**, is slowly pouring a vial of glowing 'Sea-God's Tears' into the water.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_37/Image_01.jpg)

**Dai Shui** (voice vibrating through the water like sonar): *"Xiao Ying, the surface world is burning. Your 'friends' are just data-points in a collapsing script. To truly protect your existence, you must accept the 'Sea-God's Tears'. It will erase your human biological weaknesses—your sorrow, your fear, and your memories of that delivery boy."*

**Xiao Ying** (holding her pink 'Smart-Pig' gun, her face pale but determined): *"Mother... Toby isn't just a data-point. He’s the person who gave me a cheese pork chop when I was hungry. If 'Evolution' means becoming as cold and deep as this ocean, then I'd rather be a 'Monkey' and die in the sun!!"*

**Dai Shui** (closing her eyes): *"Then you must survive the pressure of your own disobedience. The 'Truth of the Sea' will decide if your memories are worth keeping. COMMENCE DEEP-SEA SYNC!!"*
