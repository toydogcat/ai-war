(Perspective: Toby has broken the 'Nihilism Zone', but Aladdin isn't done. He rubs his lamp with a frantic energy, screaming his second wish. From the golden sands, thousands of 'Black-Flags'—the physical manifestation of every fear humanity has ever had—erupt from the ground, shrieking with a data-corrupting frequency.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_45/Image_01.jpg)

**Aladdin** (eyes wide with madness): *"Multiplying the variables of terror!! Every fear Toby has—the fear of failure, the fear of losing Lily, the fear of being alone forever—will now become a physical wall!! You can't run from yourself, Seed!!"*

**Toby** (surrounded by towering black monoliths): *"It’s... it’s a 'Recursive Fear' loop. Every flag I look at generates ten more! Little Hoop, close the 'Visual Interface'!! We have to navigate by 'Heart-Beat' only!!"*

**Little Hoop**: **「Closing visual sensors... Switching to 'Echo-Location of the Soul'. Warning: Fear levels are currently affecting our clock-speed. We need to find the 'Mother Flag'!」**

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_45/Image_02.jpg)
