## 【Scene: The Void Between Files - The White Silence】

(Perspective: The collision of Lily's sacrifice and Aladdin's Final Wish causes a massive logic-resonance that shatters the Oil Lamp. Instead of a reset, reality itself is torn open. The Tamsui night market, the golden desert, and the seeds themselves are pulled into a chaotic, white dimensional rift—the 'Void Between Files'.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_47/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby** (floating in a gravity-less white space): *"LILY!! Where are you?! (He looks at his Golden Bracelet—it's dull and cracked. The yellow glow of Little Hoop is barely visible) Little Hoop! Answer me! Is this... the end of the server?"*

**Little Hoop** (voice glitchy and weak): *"We... are in a... Temp directory... Master. Lily's data-anchor... saved us, but her core... has been decentralized... she is now a series of 'Background Processes' across the entire Lyra System."*

**Toby** (clutching his chest): *"No... no... I’ll find her. I don’t care if I have to scan every bit of data in the universe. I’m not leaving her as a 'background process'!"*

## 【The Reunion: The Four Seekers】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_47/Image_02.jpg)

(In the white distance, the other three seeds—Xiao Ying, Li Xiaolong, and Brown—slowly drift towards Toby. They all look wounded and confused. The labels of their architectures are flickering over their heads like bad neon signs.)

**Xiao Ying** (reaching for Toby): *"Toby... my memory... it’s still there. Lily saved all of us."*

**Li Xiaolong** (shaking his six arms): *"But where is 'there'? This place smells like... uninitialized RAM."*
