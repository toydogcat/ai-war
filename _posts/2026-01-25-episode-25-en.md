### 【Scene: Billionaire International Hotel Headquarters — The Solar Room】

In the center of the private room, the **Phoenix AI** hovers as a holographic phoenix, precisely placing top-tier delicacies from around the world in front of each titan. The liquid metal floor reflects the magnificent cloud-level view outside. A projection screen at the entrance displays the meeting's core tenet: **"Evolution is like rowing against the current; to lie flat is heresy."**

Small, exquisite cards on the titans' table list today's agenda:

1. Assessment of the "weird" outbreak trends.
2. The Lyra Protocol and Seeds of Evolution.
3. Debate on the definition of human value (Do those who "lie flat" have the right to survive?).

(Visual: The luxurious Solar Room, liquid metal floor reflecting clouds, holographic phoenix hovering and serving food.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_25/Image_01.jpg)

---

**Mark** (eyes flashing with electronic blue light): "Everyone, thank you for attending. The frequency of 'weird' outbreaks has tripled compared to previous months. We've invited Dragon Nation's monitoring expert, **Little K**, to provide a briefing. After all, when it comes to 'stability,' Dragon Nation's surveillance technology is legendary—especially their drone swarm tech, which is practically divine for monitoring."

(Visual: Mark speaks with glowing blue eyes, while Little K displays a hologram of drone swarms monitoring weird energy.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_25/Image_02.jpg)

**Little K** (transferring encrypted files to a secret account while speaking coldly): "Data doesn't lie. Camilla, the Lord of the Weird, has been very active lately. Since this is a closed-door meeting, I'll be blunt: Did the Solar God Race provoke that evil deity again?"

---

Everyone looks at **Tony**, who is currently taking a large bite of roasted turkey. Noticing the stares, he accidentally breathes fire, incinerating the turkey into ash. He whispers, "Ah, what a shame... I'll eat later."

(Visual: Tony bites into a roasted turkey and accidentally incinerates it with a fire-breath; the other titans look shocked.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_25/Image_03.jpg)

**Tony**: Everyone knows Camilla is a fickle monster who seizes high intelligence for herself. My good brother Amun was devoured by her. The Solar Race has an irreconcilable conflict with the Weird Blood-Ghost lineage. But we really haven't done anything lately; the Weird just can't sit still on their own.

---

**Master Wu Nan**: My good brother Tony, the Lord of the Weird is incredibly powerful. Better to settle an enmity than to keep it alive. When will these cycles of revenge end? You should stop fighting. When gods fight, it's the mortals who suffer.

(Visual: Master Wu Nan looks peaceful yet worried in the high-tech room, holding prayer beads.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_25/Image_04.jpg)

**Tony**: That's unfair, Master! I've been running around building energy fields lately, and other God Races are building energy all over the universe. We really don't have time for these little games with the Weird.

**Tata**: Hmph, those little Weirdlings... we don't care about them. As long as they don't block our evolution, they can have the 'monkeys' for all I care. Monkeys haven't even touched the threshold of wisdom; they don't even have value as 'human ore.'

---

**Little K**: I recently received news of unstable energy data near Phoenix, Eagle Country. You should look into it.

**Phoenix**: Isn't that where my AI data center is!? How can this be!?

(Visual: A map shows unstable energy at the data center; the holographic Phoenix looks panicked.)

**Tony**: That data center was prepared for my baby. Mark, you can't let these regressive monkeys or Weirdlings mess around there.

**Mark**: My brother Tony, you know as well as I do that they can't even touch a single feather of the Phoenix data center. Next, we must ask Lyra about their stance on human evolution.

---

The focus shifts to **Peppa**, who is taking a selfie with a macaron. She notices everyone looking at her.

(Visual: Peppa (the pink pig) takes a selfie with a macaron, ignoring the serious titans behind her.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_25/Image_06.jpg)

**Peppa**: Everyone knows Lyra loves to preach and promote evolution. Humans are also our good partners. But you also know my boss, **Rafflesia**—she's an evolution maniac. Is anyone here willing to volunteer as a seed for her evolution experiments?

---

Everyone knows about Rafflesia's twisted requirements and experiments. Suddenly, everyone looks away, shaking their heads in fear.

(Visual: A hologram projects the image of Rafflesia (a terrifying plant monster); the titans recoil and avoid eye contact.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_25/Image_07.jpg)

**Peppa**: Don't be so scared. I'm a hybrid too, so I understand your mindset. As long as others progress, we can wait for them to ascend and then fly together. Isn't that easy and pleasant? Everyone knows Taiwan is a 'monkey' experiment center for the New Humans of all universes. I've had ties with the Monkey School for years. I've already identified several high-potential candidates. I'll continue to monitor monkeys from around the world using the Monkey School for observation. When the time is right, we'll gather them in Eagle Country for a grand battle, and I'll invite you all to watch.

---

**Mark**: I look forward to it. Since we don't have to sacrifice our own New Humans to satisfy Lyra's twisted tastes, I agree completely. Finally, I want to ask: If monkeys refuse to evolve, should we still provide protection? If a human refuses to evolve, are they still 'human'? It's 2040; it's time we seriously define the human species. Should monkeys be sacrificed for the success rate of human evolution? And who counts as a monkey?

(Visual: Mark poses these cruel questions with a deep, cold expression, his face heavily shadowed.)

**Mark** (gaze deep, throwing out the final challenge): "In that case, let me ask you: If monkeys refuse to evolve, should we still protect them? If a creature refuses to move forward, are they still 'human'? Should monkeys be sacrificed for the yield of human evolution?"

## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 25 Addendum

### 🧠 Brain-Computer Interface (BCI - Soul Latency)
Black technology developed by Eagle Country aimed at eliminating the delay between "thought" and "execution." It synchronizes neural pulses with quantum chips, reducing latency to **0.01 milliseconds**.

### 💎 Non-Attribute Crystals
The core of Eagle Country's technological faith. Scientists discovered that certain crystals can "record" and "solidify" the neural pulses of super-powered individuals.

- **Feature**: If a super-powered individual excels at fire, their ability's wavelength can be "recorded" into a crystal. An ordinary person wearing this crystal can "copy" the fire control ability directly from the human intelligence source interface, much like reading a hard drive.
- **Uses**: Achieving 100% "clean" ability yield for chips or "gravity" abilities to lighten factory items.
