## 【Scene: Eagle Nation - X-Corp HQ - Data Core Spire】

(Perspective: Inside a tower of stacked translucent server racks, glowing with a bone-chilling blue light. Thousands of fiberoptic cables converge on a central liquid-cooled control pod. **Mark** is suspended inside like a fetus, with thousands of silk-thin electronic probes inserted into his scalp and neck.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_25/Image_01.jpg)

**Mark** (Consciousness Monologue, divine and ruthless): *"The most pathetic and incurable flaw of being human lies in the ever-present 'Latency'. From the moment your brain issues a small command to the moment your muscles execute it, it takes an average of 150 milliseconds. That 150ms gap is the bottomless abyss separating mortals from gods... I will not just improve hardware; I will personally erase this sinful, inefficient life-latency."*

(Sound Effect: **Hummmmm——High-level commands loading... Heartbeat simulation stopped... Code taking over...**)

**Grok** (The magnetic, purely rational AI assistant voice, echoing in the pod): *"Master, today's 'Human-Ore' energy extraction yield has been optimized to 98.7%. Three perfect 'Void-Attribute' crystals have been produced. Your consciousness latency... has been successfully compressed to 0.005 milliseconds."*

## 【The Forbidden Lab: Human-Ore Mining】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_25/Image_02.jpg)

(Scene Change: Deep in an underground base in the Phoenix desert. A massive centrifugal rotation device with strong electromagnetic noise is in operation. Through reinforced glass, dozens of humans (ability-users) are bound in sealed pods. As magnetic pulses surge, a faint colored glow—the energy of their souls—is stripped and drained away.)

**Researcher** (wearing a gas mask, voice trembling with leftover humanity): *"Mr. Mark... these subjects are civilians we forcibly brought from the 'Buffer Zones'. They... they have families. Are we really going to implement 'Concurrent Stripping' and solidify even their low-level communication interfaces?"*

**Mark** (appearing as a cold holographic projection behind the researcher): *"In the upcoming 2040 Great Purge, any monkey that refuses to evolve is nothing more than a rare-earth deposit with biological protein. Solidifying their fragmented abilities into crystals is the final heat their humble lives can offer to human progress. This is the inevitable price of science. Increase the frequency; I... hate noise."*

## 【Soul Lockdown: Mark's Routine】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_25/Image_03.jpg)

**Mark** (muttering with a cold, contemptuous smile): *"The Lyra civilization... Peppa... hmph. Those arrogant plant-people really think we earthlings are easy prey. Once I have enough Void Crystals, I can turn every inch of the 'Mars Base' into my neural interface. Then, the whole planet will be an extension of my will."*

**Grok**: *"Master, the representatives from the Sun-God Tribe, the Dragon Nation, and Cheese-Country have arrived at the roof of the 'Hao Ye Ren' Hotel. The closed-door meeting begins in three minutes."*

**Mark** (metal faceplate sliding over his face, a violent blue light in his eyes): *"Good. Let's go see for ourselves what 'high-level opinions' those redundant beings who call themselves 'Gods' or 'Managers' have about the upcoming 'Seed Harvest Project'."*
