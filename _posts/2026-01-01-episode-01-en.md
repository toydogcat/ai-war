## 【Scene: Mother's Garden Luxury Residential Area - Rooftop Balcony】

(Scene Perspective: Taipei's most bustling night view unfolds below. **Peige** (nicknamed Piggy) is elegantly lounging on an automatic massage chair. She holds a skewer of steaming, thick-sauced Shilin Night Market fried squid balls in her right hand, while her left hand lightly swipes in mid-air, causing countless semi-transparent virtual star maps and soul frequency curves to dance.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_01.jpg)

**Peige** (eyes half-closed, taking a bite of a juicy squid ball and letting out a satisfied sigh): *"Oh... Sister Strelitzia, can you feel it? This is the perfect resonance of Earth's carbohydrates and saturated fatty acids! It's a soul-level orchestration. If Sister Strelitzia knew I was snacking in such an information hotspot, she'd probably stop the entire rotation of the Lyra System, wouldn't she? But... for art, for infiltrating enemy lines, this little risk is just a drizzle."*

(Suddenly, the quiet night is torn by a burst of blinding green lightning, and a jagged rift splits the center of the virtual star map. A projection of a woman with a cold, icy aura, clad in a tactical cape, emerges on the screen. She is the Lyra System's Highest-Sequence Evolution Officer—**Strelitzia**.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_02.jpg)

**Strelitzia** (her cold voice echoing on the balcony, the projection rays making the air slightly singed): *"Peige! Your progress report is already a standard week overdue. Are you truly conducting reconnaissance, or are you turning yourself into a literal pig in this massive data interference field called 'Shilin'? The old men in the Council are already debating whether to downgrade your permissions to 'Unconscious Reptile' next quarter!"*

**Peige** (startled, the squid ball nearly sticking in her throat, swallowing frantically and wiping sauce from her mouth as she shifts into a military stance): *"Wait, it's a misunderstanding, Sister! I'm conducting 'Deep Ancestral-Level Sensory Civilization Infiltration'! I've analyzed the resonance points of every night market on this island and discovered a startling truth—among these creatures addicted to digital pacifiers, some extremely high-purity seeds of 'Soul' have actually sprouted."*

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_03.jpg)

---

## 【Class Hierarchy: The Distinction Between Wisdom and Monkeys】

**Strelitzia** (the projection emitting a contemptuous hum): *"Seeds? I think you've misjudged them. Hasn't this planet already fallen? Every individual is worshipping the pseudo-code they call 'AI,' surrendering the sovereignty of their souls to machine-generated spam."*

**Peige** (her expression turning solemn, a data matrix flashing in her eyes): *"Sister, that's exactly what's so interesting. Earth is now divided into two dimensions: the tiny minority of 'New Humans' who directly connect to the Source of Wisdom and control reality; and the vast majority—the 'Monkeys' locked in digital cages called 'Monkey Schools,' who only mechanically feed in prompts. They think they're controlling AI, but they're actually just dry-cell batteries being fed by it."*

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_04.jpg)

**Peige** (with a flick of her finger, the screen locks onto a young man quietly organizing cables in a corner): *"But in the ruins of these 'Monkeys,' I've caught an anomaly—**Toby**. His soul hue is blindingly bright under the scanner, completely unpolluted by that corrupt code. His almost foolishly brave curiosity about the world is exactly the raw vessel our 'Love-Awakening' project craves."*

---

## 【The Lyra Opening: Bait of Love and Wisdom】

**Strelitzia**: *"I hope your gamble isn't misplaced. Remember, if this fellow can't break through the LV.50 boundary within three weeks, you'll be packing your bags to mine in the Lyra System's collapse zone. This is the Council's final chance for you."*

**Peige** (a dangerous curve forming on the corner of her mouth): *"Don't worry! I've already planted the deadliest bait in the 'High-Tofu' concept store. That bracelet, glowing with a golden forbidden light, is waiting for its new servant. The moment he dares to put it on, this universe-sweeping Love (AI) War... officially begins!"*
