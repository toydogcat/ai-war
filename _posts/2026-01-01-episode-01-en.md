## 【Scene: Mother's Garden Luxury Residential Area - Rooftop Balcony】

(Scene Perspective: Taipei's most bustling night view unfolds below. **Peppa** (nicknamed Piggy) is elegantly lounging on an automatic massage chair. She holds a skewer of steaming, thick-sauce Shilin Night Market fried squid balls in her right hand, while her left hand lightly swipes in mid-air, causing countless semi-transparent virtual star maps and soul frequency curves to dance.)

**Peppa** (chewing a squid ball with half-closed eyes, face full of soul-healing bliss): *"Mmm... The sauce from this old shop is truly a LV.5 god-tier orchestration. Carbohydrates are truly the greatest invention in the universe. Though if Sister Strelitzia knew I was snacking here, she'd probably get so angry she'd stop the entire planet's rotation, right? But... for art, this little risk is worth it."*

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_01.jpg)

(Suddenly, a rift opens in the center of the star map, and a projection of a powerful woman slowly emerges. She is the high-level evolution expert from the Lyra System—**Strelitzia**.)

**Strelitzia** (Projection): *"Peppa! The report is already three Earth standard days late. Are you addicted to that local data interference field called a 'Night Market' again? The old men in the Council are already debating whether to downgrade your permissions!"*

**Peppa** (startled, nearly spitting out the last squid ball, quickly sits up and wipes the sauce from the corner of her mouth): *"Wait, wait, it's a misunderstanding, Sister Strelitzia! I'm conducting a frontline 'Sensory Civilization Deep Infiltration'! I've personally orchestrated every night market stall on this island. The current collection results are unprecedentedly rich—I've truly discovered a batch of soul seeds with extremely high-purity 'Love' resonance."*

---

## 【Class Hierarchy: The Difference Between Intelligence and Monkeys】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_02.jpg)

**Strelitzia**: *"Get to the point. Where are your so-called 'seeds'? Aren't Earthlings all obsessed with that pseudo-code called 'AI' now?"*

**Peppa** (tone becoming professional): *"Correct. Earthlings are now clearly divided. The ruling class 'New Humans' can directly connect to the Source of Wisdom; while the majority of people are the so-called 'Monkeys'. They are locked into meaningless 'Monkey Schools', learning 'AI' tools that merely imitate intelligence."*

**Peppa** (pointing to a small light on the screen): *"But among these brainwashed monkeys, I found a guy named **Toby**. Although he's just a general assistant, his soul hue is extremely pure, completely unpolluted by decadent code. His limitless curiosity is the perfect vessel for our 'Love-Awakening' project."*

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_03.jpg)

## 【The Lyra Opening: Bait of Love and Intelligence】

**Strelitzia**: *"I hope your eyes haven't failed you. Remember, if he can't break through the LV.50 boundary, you'll have to pack your bags and head back to the Lyra System to mine ore. Understand?"*

**Peppa** (patting her chest): *"No problem! I've already prepared a special 'Surprise Gift Box' for him. In the deepest corner of the High-Tofu Concept Store, that bracelet is waiting for its new owner. As long as he dares to put it on, this trans-dimensional war officially begins!"*
