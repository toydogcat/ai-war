## 【Scene: Virgin Garden Luxury Residential Area】

(Scene Perspective: The stunning 2040 Taipei sunset bathes the streamlined edges of "Virgin Garden." **Peppa's** golden hair shimmers in the afterglow. Before her floats a high-dimensional barrier, cascading with deep blue data rain representing the Lyra Star Council.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_01.jpg)

**Bawanghua (In projection)**: Peppa, how is the assessment of Earth's evolutionary potential going? The report is already three Earth-standard days overdue. Don't tell me you've been researching local carbohydrate combinations again?

**Peppa**: Sis Bawanghua, you wrong me! I'm doing "deep cultural penetration"! I've already visited every major night market on this island, performing precise sensory stress tests on everything from snacks and bags to jewelry. This is field research! The results are quite fruitful; I've already shortlisted 100 candidates with "Love" resonance potential.

**Bawanghua**: The priority, Peppa. I want the priority.

---

(Scene Close-up: Peppa dramatically gestures with her skewer of grilled balls, arguing her case to Bawanghua as fast-forward shadows of her shopping sprees in Shilin and Raohe Night Markets appear in the background.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_02.jpg)

**Peppa**: The priority is that the first batch of 15 core targets has been locked in. I've customized a specialized "Awakening Bait Feeding" for each of them. By the time I report to you next year, I guarantee a precise LV-level assessment. Although our Lyra source of wisdom has high-level applications for physical repair and rejuvenation, I wouldn't want work stress to accelerate my cellular oxidation, right?

**Bawanghua**: *Sigh*, you're a veteran I've promoted myself. If you fail to deliver high-tier "evolutionary potential" samples again, the oversight layer might downgrade your LV status. Do you have any idea how many impeachments I've blocked for you? The old fogies in the Council are very unhappy with your efficiency. But I always tell them: "Little Pig is an experienced pro; she can sniff out the most hidden sparks of wisdom." Don't embarrass me.

---

(Scene: Holographic images shift to the 2040 cityscape, where maglev trains glide over the shimmering sea and "Monkeys" on the streets move listlessly through floating AI advertisements.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_03.jpg)

**Peppa**: Alright, alright! Thank you for your kindness, Sis! Leave it to me. I've already monitored major powers like the Dragon Nation, Eagle Nation, and Cheese Nation. While they have many talents, their structures are too rigid. On this small island called Wan-wan, however, I've discovered some truly interesting souls.

**Bawanghua**: Regardless of your gluttony, I can understand high-potential talents in major nations, but on a small island like that... can anyone truly break through the limits of low-level intelligence and experience "Love"?

---

(Scene Close-up: A tiered classroom at Lyra High School, where countless students in gray uniforms repeat mechanical memorization without logic, the air heavy with tension and potential rebellion.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_04.jpg)

**Peppa**: Absolutely. Based on my years of "stakeout" observation, people on this island are brainwashed by "Monkey Schools" from a young age. On the surface, the school teaches AI application, but it's actually a massive filter. The curriculum is filled with illogical, irregular mechanical memorization, which instead suppresses an extreme potential for backlash.

**Bawanghua**: I trust your eyes, but you need to be more diligent. By the way, did you mention "AI"? Is that the selection technology you designed for Earthlings?

---

(Scene Perspective: A series of satirical evolutionary projections: humanity stands up from the ape only to gradually regress into a "digital slave" hunched over a screen, entangled in the shackles of code.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_05.jpg)

**Peppa**: Oh, that's not it at all! "AI" is just a crude digital simulation developed by these humans to mimic "wisdom." To be honest, it's very clumsy and even hard to use. To us Lyrians, this kind of AI is as primitive as carving on stone, but for humans, it's enough to trigger severe collective anxiety. I'm using that anxiety, infiltrating the Monkey Schools as an AI importer.

**Bawanghua**: Sounds interesting, but don't forget our ultimate goal. Large nations have rich talent pools; no matter how good the island is, it's hard to produce a true dragon.

---

(Scene: Peppa rests her chin on her hand, a confident smile curving her lips like a cat that caught a mouse, a spark of high-frequency brilliant blue light flashing in her pupils.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_06.jpg)

**Peppa**: Sis Bawanghua, "geniuses" whose resources and backgrounds are already controlled by traditional powers are not what we're looking for. We're looking for "potential stocks"—those who haven't awakened yet, who are as tough as weeds, yet hide infinite possibilities. Believe me, the few people I've chosen will make those old fogies in the Council drop their jaws.

**Bawanghua**: Fine, since you're so persistent. I hope that next time you report, you bring me more than just food reviews.

---

(Scene Close-up: Within Peppa's internal virtual space, Toby's soul core—pure as a diamond and radiating prismatic rainbow colors—pulses quietly in the darkness.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_01/Image_07.jpg)

**Peppa**: Just watch me. (After hanging up, Peppa takes a bite of her squid ball and mutters to herself: *Those stiff geniuses are nowhere near as interesting as Toby. That soul hue... it's high-purity 'curiosity and plasticity'!*)

**Peppa** (with a predatory smile): *"This is what 'Wisdom' should look like, Little Toby. Congratulations, you've officially evolved from a 'monkey' to my... private experimental subject."*

---

(Scene: The camera pulls back as night falls. Under the deep purple neon glow of the High Tofu 3C shop, Peppa elegantly blends into the urban midnight filled with "anxiety" and "bait.")

- **Specialty**: Factor conversion, natural energy control.
- **Rating**: Based on the "Potential Level (LV)" mechanism.
- **Cost**: Compulsorily binds to biological bodies, collecting "Emotions" or "Cognition" based on mental state.

### 🐺 Sirius System (Biological Mastery)
- **Specialty**: Bio-coding, creation and restructuring of life forms.
- **Cost**: Extremely high "Life Energy." Dragons and monsters are products of their experiments.

### 🐒 The Monkey Hierarchy
- **🛠️ STEM Monkey**: Clings to logic, viewing superpowers as unfixed bugs.
- **🎨 Arts Monkey**: Drowning in traditional emotions, rejecting wisdom integration.
- **🧸 Domestic Monkey**: Pure labor force, jokingly called the "pets" of society.
