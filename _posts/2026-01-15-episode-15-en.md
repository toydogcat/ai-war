- **Glasses**: A law monkey. He constantly mentions the 'AI Ethics Regulations' and 'Algorithm Accountability Act,' responsible for writing those disclaimers that nobody reads.
- **Scooter**: A transportation monkey, has a crush on Xiao Mei. He also loves to run around and listens to Teacher Lily.

### 💶 Tip-based Repair Protocol

- **Principle**: Targeted at specific commercial cloud AIs. By injecting large amounts of virtual currency, the system is induced to allocate 'Top-tier Redundant Compute' to forcibly overwrite the fault area.
- **Side Effect**: Leads to severe system inflation and cannot resolve non-code threats (like 'Eerie').

- **Executor**: Master Drill.
- **Power**: Ignores high-level frameworks and interfaces, directly 'writing/deleting' at the bottom-level hardware addresses.
- **Outcome**: Prone to physical hardware damage. When fighting 'Eerie', it's easily infected by resentful data, causing mechanical arms to lose control.

- **Position**: Publicity Department teacher, a hidden **extreme rich second generation**.
- **Background**: Her parents are high-level executives in multinational energy companies, controlling various scales of 'nuclear fusion technology' worldwide.
- **Equipment**: Wears a specially made **Nano-Power Suit** (disguised as normal clothes), allowing her to easily lift 200kg weights.
- **Personality**: Usually keeps a low profile under LuLu. Entrusted by LuLu to take special care of Toby, she supports whatever Toby wants to do unconditionally.
