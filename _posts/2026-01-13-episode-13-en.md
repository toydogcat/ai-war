## 【Scene: Monkey School D-7 Building - Director's Office】

(Perspective: Blinding afternoon sunlight filters through dusty floor-to-ceiling windows, illuminating a cluttered desk filled with expired figurines and obsolete consoles. **Director Fat Dragon** is squeezed into his nearly-collapsing leather executive chair, eyes shining with a neurotic, obsessed glow. He is frantically kneading a pinkish-purple, crystal-shelled but cheap-looking 'Smart Pig' toy. The pig emits a sharp, low-fidelity 'squeak' with every squeeze.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_13/Image_01.jpg)

**Fat Dragon** (breathing heavily, a trace of suspicious drool at the corner of his mouth): *"Oh... this texture, this random flickering... this is exactly the 'Wisdom Resonance' Goddess Peppa spoke of! As long as I hold this pig every day, my brain waves will sync across dimensions to the Lyra System. Oh... I feel it, my intelligence is evolving at sub-second speeds..."*

**Xia Wei Xian** (A lobster-type AI service robot wearing an outdated apron, floating in mid-air, expertly rolling its eyes): *"Director, even though my logic core is old, I must remind you—that is just a blow-molded plastic toy with three pre-recorded sounds and some basic flickering LEDs. Also, the 'Tiger System' reported 400 bad sector errors this morning. There are even reports of a girl crying and mastiffs barking in the pantry. Do you really plan to keep playing dead?"*

## 【The Scorn of the High-End Technician: Drill】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_13/Image_02.jpg)

(Scene: The sensor door to the office is shoved open violently. A man with wild hair, deep dark circles, and a mechanical keyboard modified from a large electric drill—**Drill**—walks in, carrying enough rage to start a server room fire.)

**Drill** (slamming his tablet in front of Fat Dragon): *"Fat Dragon! Have you lost your mind? You took the entire data maintenance budget for this quarter and signed it over to that black-market shop 'High-Tofu' in the Saintly Garden again?! Where's the funding I requested to rewrite the core kernel? The school system is like a terminal tumor of out-of-order code, and you’re spending money on this IF-ELSE plastic garbage?!"*

**Fat Dragon** (nonchalantly picking his ear): *"Drill, don't always obsess over those boring low-level details. We're in the Era of Big Data Traffic now. Who still does that inefficient manual work? Aesthetics without traffic are a sin! Here, practice the pose with me: grip the Smart Pig tightly and pray sincerely to the AI! If you're pious enough, the bugs will automatically become features!"*

## 【The Shadow of the Tiger: Deep in the System】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_13/Image_03.jpg)

(Scene: The camera shifts to the dim, empty hallways of the teaching building. A weathered mirrored pillar suddenly flickers with an eerie, sharp red light. The once-smooth virtual interface becomes distorted and torn, revealing black junk code that looks like beast claw marks.)

**Broadcasting System (Tiger)** (emitting a high-pitched, terrifyingly childish call mixed with electronic interference): **「I'm sorry... hmph... Mom is there... Woof woof! ...It hurts... don't hit me... I'll run the task properly next time... I'm sorry... I'm sorry...」**

(Visual Close-up: In the depths of the pillar's shadow, two pairs of glowing green compound eyes, devoid of any carbon-based emotion, flash for a second, leaving behind a trail of black, gear-marked slime on the floor.)
