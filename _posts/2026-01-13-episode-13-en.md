## 【Scene: Director's Office】

(Scene: Director Fat Dragon embraces a giant pink pig in ecstasy, with promotional posters for Notre Dame Garden in the background.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_13/Image_01.jpg)

Early in the morning, **Fat Dragon** stepped into the office, lovingly clutching a **pinkish-purple pig doll**. With every squeeze, the pig emitted a crisp oink. He was frantically fiddling with it, muttering "Wisdom Resonance" under his breath.

**Xia Wei Xian**: Morning, Director. How much longer will this creepy pig-squeezing behavior last? Are you torturing that poor thing this early in the morning?

---

(Scene: Director Fat Dragon's office is filled with colorful "Rainbow Piglet" dolls. Each one emits cheap LED flashes. Xia Wei Xian stands by, looking at the Director with pure disdain.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_13/Image_02.jpg)

**Fat Dragon** (Retorting): This is "Wisdom Resonance"! These are the **Rainbow LED Smart Sensing Pigs** recommended by Goddess Peggy. Peggy said as long as I touch and squeeze them every day, my cerebral cortex will synchronize with the frequency of Lyra. This isn't just a toy—this is **faith**!

**Xia Wei Xian** (Sneezing): Director, I must remind you, these "piglets" are untrustworthy. And lately, **The Tiger System** has been acting up. We've received notices from the police station about "missing little girl" incidents and multiple "stray dog abuse" cases. It used to be dog barking, but lately, it's turned into human screams, looping announcements like "I'm sorry, I won't do it again"...

---

(Scene: Director Fat Dragon looks terrified, while Xia Wei Xian seems to enjoy telling the story.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_13/Image_03.jpg)

**Fat Dragon**: Stop! Don't disturb my wisdom growth with those horror stories. Handle those weird things yourself. Hurry up and send this batch of piglets to the warehouse. These are our "primary teaching materials" for the next quarter. When **Toby** arrives, have him start developing the curriculum!

---

**Drill Master**: Morning, Director.

(Scene: Fat Dragon spreads his arms wide, his eyes gleaming with fanaticism. Behind him, a holy phantom of Manager Peggy seems to appear. Drill Master stands beside him.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_13/Image_04.jpg)

**Fat Dragon**: Drill Master, look! These are the Rainbow Glowing Piglets specially recommended by Manager Peggy. They are simply too intelligent! When I'm not touching it, it's a subtle pink-purple, but it glows differently based on my mood. This is truly the light of wisdom!

**Drill Master**: Director, what is that thing? What does it do? Can you eat it? Did Peggy give it to you again? Why do you trust her so much?

---

(Scene: Fat Dragon points at Drill Master's nose and shouts, holding a glowing rainbow pig. He believes hand-coding is for primitive people. In the background is The Monkey School's famous "Intelligent Management" slogan.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_13/Image_05.jpg)

**Fat Dragon**: Drill Master, you just don't get it. Do you know about the stars? Do you know Lyra? Ah, forget it, you wouldn't understand. Anyway, if Peggy says it's useful, it's useful. If she says it's good, it's good. If we don't know why, it's just because our wisdom isn't high enough. Manager Peggy's choices are the crystallization of wisdom. I've already signed over the school's entire procurement budget for the next quarter to Peggy's Godiva store in Notre Dame Garden. Do you know Notre Dame Garden? Only the elite live there! Monkeys just need to do whatever the elites do—unconditional acceptance of Goddess Peggy. That's how you'll stop being a monkey!

**Drill Master**: Director, that pig is just LED trash with a few lines of if-else statements and some randomizers. It's not reading your mood; your mood is just random. Instead of spending a fortune on these decorations, why not let me rewrite the lab's kernel core? Xia Wei Xian, don't you agree?

---

(Scene: Drill Master looks entranced by his own code. The screen in the background shows: "Total Errors: 0" (because he turned off all error messages). Xia Wei Xian has a "what nonsense are you talking" expression.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_13/Image_06.jpg)

**Xia Wei Xian**: Drill Master, you're truly impressive. That roll-call plugin you wrote for the students was so good—zero errors! As long as you don't write error messages, there are no errors. Truly a worry-free approach!

**Drill Master**: Hear that, Director? Even Xia Wei Xian admires my zero-error plugin. Unlike these AI-developed systems or plugins that report errors everywhere. Every time there's an update, the messages are overwhelming—massive failures every week. AI writes errors faster than I can solve them. And since you gave the entire budget to the Godiva store in Notre Dame Garden, what about my budget for rewriting the Tiger System's kernel? The current system is like a tumor from all those AI patches. One minute it's a lost girl calling out, the next it's a dog barking. What's next?!

---

(Scene: Director Fat Dragon forcibly shoves a piglet in front of Drill Master, forcing him to "interact with AI." Drill Master's drill arms spin at high speed in rage, sparks flying, as he screams: "My Director is a moron!!")

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_13/Image_07.jpg)

**Fat Dragon**: What do you know? Right now, all I hear is you shouting! Manager Peggy said this is "aesthetic resonance." Those sounds aren't errors; they are art. She told me to make the school's atmosphere more "spiritual." I am executing the will of the Goddess of Wisdom! And how many times have I told you—stop being a monkey! No one hand-codes anymore. It's inefficient and often just dies. You must let AI write the code; you just communicate with the AI. And what did you say you were writing? AI++? Is that something new?

**Drill Master**: It's C++! Can you remember anything else besides AI and Peggy?! Just leave me the money, and I'll upgrade these broken systems myself.

---

**Fat Dragon**: I'm not arguing with you. I have business. Where's Toby? I had Xia Wei Xian put all the new Rainbow Piglet dolls in the warehouse. I've decided the next teaching theme for the students will be the Rainbow Piglets. Have him get over there and play with them. Toby loves new things. We're going to win the Best Teaching Award at The Monkey School with these wise piglets!

**Drill Master**: I don't care about you anymore. You're just giving Toby and the students terrible guidance. What can those pig dolls do? Can students really learn how to communicate with AI through them?

---

(Scene: In the school's dim warehouse, thousands of Rainbow Piglet dolls are stacked like mountains. They move their eyes in unison, emitting strange mechanical sounds.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_13/Image_08.jpg)

**Fat Dragon**: Drill Master, stop being primitive! Who hand-codes anymore? Inefficiency is a sin! Come, practice with me—hold the AI piglet, try to talk to the AI, repeat, cycle... you can do it too. Come on, Drill Master, do it with me!

**Drill Master**: Good grief, my Director is a moron!!
