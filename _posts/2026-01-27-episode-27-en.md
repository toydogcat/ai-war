## 【Scene: Saintly Garden - The Baptism】

(Perspective: Inside the 'Fermentation Chamber' of the Saintly Garden. The air is thick with the scent of stinky tofu, which Master Tofu claims is the smell of 'Compressed Wisdom'. **Toby** is sitting cross-legged, a small bowl of pitch-black tofu in front of him. **Lily** is watching nervously.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_27/Image_01.jpg)

**Master Tofu** (pitting his wooden paddle against the side of the vat): *"Boy, your Golden Bracelet is like a supercomputer with no OS. You have the power, but no foundation. This 'Karmic Tofu' is fermented from the ancient 'Dragon-Nation' firewall logic. It’s the ultimate baptism for your soul."*

**Toby** (holding his nose): *"Master... it smells like a server room that’s been on fire for three weeks. Can't I just... download a firmware update instead?"*

**Master Tofu**: *"Firmware updates are for Cowards! This is a physical overwrite! EAT IT!!"*
