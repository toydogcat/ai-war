## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 27 Addendum

### 💎 Non-Attribute Crystals
The core of Eagle Country's technological faith. These crystals can "record" and "solidify" the neural pulses of super-powered individuals.

- **Feature**: Abilities like fire control can be "recorded." An ordinary person can "copy" these abilities directly from the human intelligence source interface.
- **Uses**: 100% chip yield, "clean" technology, and gravitational efficiency.

### ⛏️ Human Mining & Rare Earths
The darkest secret behind the technology.

- **Principle**: Super-powers are "gateways" to the source of intelligence. Eagle Country uses rare earths to maintain signals and cruelly "strips" these gateways from living people, solidifying them into crystals.
- **Consequence**: High-quality crystals come at the cost of draining a "gate opener" completely.

### 👁️ Mark's Ability: Laplace's Demon
Mark's ultimate computational domain. It predicts the atomic momentum of everything nearby with 100% accuracy.

- **Absolute Evasion**: Enemy attacks are predicted 0.01ms before they happen.
- **Atlas Swarm**: Commands a near-infinite number of "Optimus" robots for saturation combat.
- **Satellite Link**: Calls upon thousands of satellites for synchronized locking rays.

### 🚀 Ultimate Move: Ares Atomic Space Cannon
Mark's final trump card—a system located at the Mars base.

- **4D Wormhole**: Opens a wormhole to deliver the most powerful nuclear fusion bomb directly to the target.
- **Zero Ballistics**: No flight process, only the "explosion." Unavoidable.
- **Price of Destruction**: Heavily damages the Mars base hardware, requiring an indefinite cooldown. This is a "mutually assured destruction" move.
