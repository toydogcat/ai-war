## 【Scene: Hao Ye Ren Hotel - Lily's Private Suite】

(Perspective: Inside the 'Hao Ye Ren' (Good-Wealthy) 5-Star International Hotel. The air is cool and smells of high-end lavender and silent server-grade air conditioning. **Toby** is sitting on a velvet sofa that feels softer than a cloud, staring blankly at a plate of exquisite French macarons and a piece of deep-fried cheese pork chop that looks like a piece of art.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_14/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby** (voice trembling as he picks up a silver fork): *"Lily... Teacher, are you really sure I can stay here? This one night’s room fee is probably more than I’d earn delivering bento for ten years... I feel like even the toilet in this room has more processing power than my school tablet."*

**Lily** (wearing a silk pajama set, her hair down, looking incredibly relaxed as she pours a glass of sparkling water): *"Toby, stop worrying about the bill. The 'Monkey School' is currently a logical disaster zone. If you stay in the dorms, Strelitzia's 'Clean-up Team' will find you in minutes. This hotel is managed by a high-dimensional AI called 'Phoenix'—it’s the only place on this island that is completely 'Zero-Log'. You’re invisible here."*

---

## 【Wisdom, Love, and the Source】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_14/Image_02.jpg)

**Toby** (blushing like a ripe tomato, awkwardly scratching the back of his head): *"Lily Teacher... I have a question. I’m just a delivery boy, a nobody. Even I know that someone as high-level as you... why are you taking such good care of a 'Tool Monkey' like me who barely understands GPT-5 logic?"*

**Lily** (her smile fades, her eyes becoming distant and hollow, revealing a transcendental aura that belies her age): *"Because you possess a kind of 'Purity' that even a universe-wide database cannot simulate. Toby, do you know? In this year of 2040, where fake intelligence is everywhere, there are actually three driving forces: the first is Fake Intelligence (AI), the second is Awakened Wisdom (Essence), and the most powerful third one... is called 'Love'."*

**Lily**: *"The power of the latter can even smooth out black holes. I want to see with my own eyes what the world will look like when you finally awaken and master the power of 'Love'."*

**Toby** (completely lost by her words, but thinking Lily looks incredibly cool right now): *"Oh... that sounds like a philosophy final exam question, but it feels super powerful! Lily Teacher, I’ll definitely work hard, I won't let you down!!"*
