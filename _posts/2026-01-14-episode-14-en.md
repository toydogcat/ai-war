## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 14 Addendum

### ⚡ The Three Levels of Intelligence

- **Pseudo-Intelligence (AI)**: The mainstream. Based on probability prediction, it collapses into recursive failure when facing a "Logical Singularity."
- **Awakened Wisdom**: Superpowers invoked after human awakening. Powerful, but consumes extremely high biological energy, often leading to "Extreme Hunger" or "Exhaustion."
- **The Love**: Directly invokes the source power of Lyra/Sirius. Capable of creation, black hole smoothing, and rewriting physical rules. The cost calculation is extremely complex.

### 🏫 The Monkey School

- **Full Name**: "Global Intelligence Evolution Center," the giant ruling the education world in 2040.
- **Doctrine**: Core slogan "Evolve or Extinct." The school believes any labor that cannot collaborate with AI is "Degeneration."
- **Backing Force**: Secretly funded by "Neo-Humans," who view this place as a "Human Guinea Pig Petri Dish" for Lyra experiments.
- **Director (Fat Dragon)'s Stance**: Partially informed. He senses higher existences but has severe cognitive bias, viewing them only as undeniable "Wisdom Authorities." He has blind worship for Peiqi (a Lyra hybrid); if Peiqi says the sun is fake, he'll immediately sign a "Sun Falsification Certificate."

### 🥣 Butterfly-Dream Logic

- **Meaning**: Lily's core philosophy, advocating for the blurring of boundaries between humans and AI.
- **Danger**: Excessive pursuit of integration leads to human consciousness being washed away by AI data streams, causing severe "Cognitive Dissonance."

### 🐶 Glitch: Dog Soul Swarm

- **Source**: Transformed from abused biological data.
- **Characteristics**: Can parasitize any electronic device, making it emit interference messages of "I'm sorry" and "Please forgive me."
