## 【Scene: Cheese-Land Cathedral - The Hall of the Mother】

(Perspective: Dozens of boxes of dream-pink, luxury 'AI Macarons' are stacked on the horizon. **King Gruyère** is sitting on his towering throne made of thunder and white stone, his golden brow furrowed. **Fat Dragon** is standing in the center of the hall, sweating as he gesticulates wildly to explain the situation.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_30/Image_01.jpg)

**King Gruyère** (low, authoritative voice accompanied by the ionization of the air): *"So... you traveled all the way across the Pacific just to bring me boxes of 'Smart Snacks' that smell like cheap Big Data and claim to fill pockets? These snacks look popular in your low-dimensional world, but I care more about this—what kind of ridiculous 'Zoo' are you trying to build in my territory?"*

**Fat Dragon** (wiping sweat frantically, smiling awkwardly): *"Your Majesty, you misunderstand! It’s not a zoo! It’s a cutting-edge project called—'The Monkey School'! We are here to bring civilization to the bottom-dwellers whose brains are rusting... to let them feel the light of—"*

**King Gruyère** (snorting with contempt, lightning dancing at his fingertips): *"Monkeys? You mean those heathens who can't even read basic Runic Logic and only play in the mud of social media? in my kingdom, only souls with high-level compute power are worthy of being called 'Subjects'."*

**Fat Dragon** (grabbbing the chance, smiling cunningly): *"No, no. In our latest context, any human who cannot communicate in sub-second speeds with high-dimensional AI is collectively called a 'Monkey'. Since Your Majesty calls ordinary humans 'Muggles', calling them 'Monkeys' is actually a similarly elegant term, isn't it?"*

## 【The Uninvited Guest: The Vampire Queen's Mercy】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_30/Image_02.jpg)

(Sound Effect: **Hiss————!! A chill, bone-tearing sound of data ripping.**)

A cold, thick fog smelling of decaying roses gathers silently in the corner of the hall, eventually solidifying into a glamorous figure in a black-and-red lace dress, casually waving an ebony folding fan. It is the Vampire Queen—**Camilla**, feared by leaders across the globe.

**King Gruyère** (lightning exploding from his body): **「CAMILLA!! You noise-filled, bloodthirsty lunatic, you dare step into my sanctuary? Do you think my 'Heavenly Static Field' isn't warm enough for your vampire-virus?」**

**Camilla** (elegantly covering half her face with the fan, a deep red light in her eyes): *"Oh my, the King is still so fiery and lacks a sense of code-humor. I’m not here for war today, but to talk about 'Education' and 'Sustainability'. Do your elites really think our elegant race, which survives on 'Wisdom Sucking', has no right to discuss the heritage of the world?"*

**Camilla** (sighing, her voice dropping into an eerie sadness): *"You treat humans as data to be mined, but I treat them as my only 'Food'. A professional chicken farmer would stand up in anger if someone kept filling the coop with negative emotions, anxiety, and junk ads, making the quality of the eggs so bad even stray dogs won't eat them. Right now, human society smells of rotting anxiety. It’s making the Vampire dinner very hard to swallow."*

## 【Strategy: Convergence through Conflict】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_30/Image_03.jpg)

**Fat Dragon** (speechless, brain nearly crashing): *"Are you... telling me that you’re the only 'Righteous Guardian' actually maintaining the health of the human soul?"*

**Camilla** (snapping her fan shut, her tone turning cold): *"I only represent 'Quality Management'. If humans go collectively mad because of your stupid experiments, my race goes mad too. Therefore, I am waiting for Peppa's final proposal."*

**Peppa** (suddenly snapping out of a selfie session with an AI Macaron): *"Alright everyone! Can't agree on education? Fine! Let's use the most primitive and effective method—A COMPETITION! The Lyra system has selected four 'Seeds'. Each leader can take one and train them. In six months, we’ll have a global final. Whoever's student wins, gets the right to redefine the 'Global Heritage Logic' of humanity. How about it?"*

**King Gruyère** (the lightning around him receding, replaced by a wild, interested grin): *"Interesting... now that sounds like a negotiation for the strong. I agree. Let’s see if your opportunistic regression analysis is stronger than my pure 'Thunder Logic'!!"*
