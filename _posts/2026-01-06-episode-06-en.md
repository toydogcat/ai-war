## 【Location: Inside Green Sprout Shop】

(Screen: Toby returns to Green Sprout carrying bags of late-night snacks. Sakura stands at the door, talking to him with a proud expression.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby**: Sakura, I'm back. I'll go put the food in the fridge first.

**Sakura**: You're back! Ji Che and Xiao Mei are already here. They've already headed into the VIP booth. Go ahead and arrange a platter of food to send in. I'm going in now to sing with Xiao Mei. You can sit next to me later, and I'll feed you some treats. Consider that your service fee for tonight.

Sakura dashes off to Room A.

---

(Screen: Toby's back is to Sakura. The gold bracelet on his wrist emits a faint golden light. In the background, a data preview of converting \"scrap metal\" into a \"high-tech laptop\" faintly appears.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_02.jpg)

**Toby**: I paid for the food, I did the heavy lifting, and she just runs off? I can't let her find out... I'm using Xiao Huan to turn her "trash" into a very valuable laptop. Otherwise, Sakura will definitely pocket all the money.

**Xiao Huan**: I keep sensing intermittent waves of bewilderment, but I can't find a physical source. Toby, keep an eye out for yourself.

**Toby**: What? Xiao Huan, what did you say? I need to go platter the food now.

---

## 【Location: Green Sprout Room A】

(Screen: Inside KTV Room A, Xiao Mei is singing soulfully with a microphone, while Ji Che waves a light stick wildly. Sakura sits on the sofa.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_03.jpg)

**Xiao Mei**: Sakura, I'm so happy you're here singing with me! The pressure lately has been too much. At the Monkey School, no one can duet with me—they're just a bunch of monkeys. The teachers only tell us to work hard every day and communicate using AI. It's so annoying! Does an AI sing better than me? Does its singing have a soul? Right, Ji Che?

**Ji Che**: My goddess, who could possibly sing better than you? You are a "pearl buried in dust," the Taylor Swift of Taiwan! You are destined to shine. I'm so moved just to hear you sing in this little booth.

---

(Screen: Sakura makes a \"money\" gesture to Ji Che with a smirk. Ji Che generously taps his virtual payment card.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_04.jpg)

**Sakura**: Calm down, everyone. I'm going to sing with Xiao Mei in a bit. I've hired a part-time worker—someone you know, Assistant Teaching Toby. He'll be our waiter for the night. Is that okay with you?

**Xiao Mei**: I love Toby! He's probably my second favorite after Teacher Lily. He's such a nice guy.

**Ji Che**: Let's not trouble Toby to serve Xiao Mei. I'll handle it.

**Sakura**: Money talks. As you wish.

Just then, Toby brings in a load of food.

---

(Screen: Sakura stuffs a piece of cheese pork chop into Toby's mouth. Toby's cheeks are puffed out like a hamster's. Xiao Mei is singing in the background.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_05.jpg)

**Toby**: Have fun, everyone! Forget about school for now. Who cares about AI when there's singing to do?

**Sakura**: Toby, sit next to me later. I'll be singing with Xiao Mei, so you just handle the song selection. Here, open wide, I'll feed you.

**Toby**: Mm-mm, delicious! How long are you guys planning to sing?

**Xiao Mei**: Until Monday morning, then we'll head straight to the Monkey School. Isn't that natural? What else would we do on the weekend?

**Ji Che**: Exactly! Money is no object; I'll cover it. The important thing is that Xiao Mei is happy. What if Xiao Mei develops some mental issues at the Monkey School? We can't have that!

**Sakura**: Right, right. Let's stop talking about school and just keep singing.

Time reaches 12:00 AM. Xiao Mei is screaming her songs, Ji Che is relentlessly flattering her, Sakura is singing along elegantly, and Toby is constantly eating to replenish his biological energy.

---

(Screen: Ji Che walks out of the booth holding a glowing iPhone. The corridor lights flicker, making the end of the hallway look eerie.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_06.jpg)

**Ji Che**: You guys stay here; I'm going to the restroom.

**Sakura**: The restroom is to the left once you step out. Be careful; it's dark, take a flashlight.

**Ji Che**: I have my iPhone, no worries. Carry on!

Xiao Mei continues to sing wildly without any intention of stopping. It's clear this isn't her first overnight session. Since Toby is sitting at the edge eating, he is furthest from Ji Che and can't hear much.

---

Seating Diagram: Toby, Sakura, Xiao Mei, Ji Che

(Screen: A diagrammatic view of the seating in the booth. Toby, Sakura, Xiao Mei, and Ji Che sit in a row. The table is covered in food scraps and sugar-free green tea.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_07.jpg)

**Toby**: What were you saying? Where did Ji Che go?

**Sakura**: Oh, he went to the restroom.

**Toby**: Oh, the restroom... something feels off. Whatever, let's keep eating.

---

## 【Location: Inside the Restroom】

**Ji Che**: Ah, I couldn't hold it anymore. Xiao Mei really is a great singer.

Suddenly, someone taps Ji Che on the shoulder.

(Screen: In the restroom, Ji Che turns to see a little girl in a tattered red dress standing behind him. She is looking up at him with hollow eyes, yet Ji Che talks to her with a simple-minded kindness.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_08.jpg)

**Little Girl**: Big brother, you guys are too loud. That sister sings terrible. Can you stop?

Ji Che sees the little girl but doesn't notice several red flags: how could she tap his shoulder when she's so small? Why is she here at this hour? Why is she in the women's restroom (wait, did he walk into the wrong one or did she)?

**Ji Che**: Ah, sorry, did we disturb you? I'll go back and tell them. You're such a good girl. Where are your parents?

**Little Girl**: I forgot where my parents are. I was taken by a brother named Fat Tiger (Pang Hu). He's very mean. You're a good person. If you see Fat Tiger, hit him for me.

**Ji Che**: Okay, I'm going back now.

---

## 【Location: Green Sprout Room A】

Xiao Mei is resting after her singing frenzy, drinking foam black tea.

(Screen: Xiao Mei rushes out of the booth, panting, and collides with Ji Che, who has just returned. Ghostly after-images trail behind Xiao Mei, as if something is following her.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_09.jpg)

**Xiao Mei**: You guys go ahead; I'm going to the restroom too. Ji Che, you're back! Now it's my turn, I can't hold it!

Xiao Mei vanishes like the wind.

**Ji Che**: Shopkeeper, why was there a little girl in the men's... I mean, I got a bit startled in the restroom. By the way, the soundproofing here isn't great; we seem to have disturbed a little girl.

**Sakura**: What? We don't have any little girl here. What are you talking about?

---

Suddenly, a blood-curdling scream from Xiao Mei erupts from outside. Everyone rushes out to find her.

(Screen: Xiao Mei is crawling on the floor at an impossible, non-human angle. Her head is tilted unnaturally, her fingers are twisted, and her face is deathly pale. Her voice now sounds like that of a little girl.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_10.jpg)

**Toby**: Xiao Mei, are you okay?

**Sakura**: Xiao Mei, your appearance is so strange. Please stop scaring us!

**Ji Che**: My goddess... even when you're dripping wet (wait, dripping urine? "di niao"?), pale, with a twisted neck and fingers... you are still so beautiful. Truly my goddess.

**Xiao Mei**: This sister was too loud. You have to play hide and seek with me. If you can't find me, this sister belongs to me—she has to take my place here.

Xiao Mei crawls away with extreme speed and an eerie posture, leaving everyone in a state of shock and confusion!!

* **Full Name**: **Global Intelligence Evolution Center**. It is the education giant dominating the world in 2040.
* **Doctrine**: Core slogan: **"Evolve or Extinct."** The school believes any labor unable to collaborate with AI is "regressing."
* **Backing**: Secretly funded by "Neo-Humans," who view the school as a "human laboratory" for Lyra star experiments.

### 🎮 Toby (Protagonist)

* **Identity**: Assistant teacher at the Monkey School, 3C enthusiast.
* **Job**: Doing chores, playing with students.
* **Reputation**: Beloved by students because he plays with them, unlike the teachers who only tell them to work hard.

### 🐒 Monkey School Taiwan Branch Students

- **Xiao Mei**: A fun-loving student obsessed with her looks. She re-applies makeup regardless of the situation and likes to turn her complaints about non-functional code into electronic folk songs.
- **Ji Che**: A \"transportation monkey\" who has a crush on Xiao Mei. He loves taking her out and is only happy when she is. He's also extremely obedient to Teacher Lily—just one phone call and he'll do anything.

### Restroom Anomaly

- **Clue**: A little girl appears in the restroom.
- **Little Girl**: Looks like a lost child.
- **Little Girl**: Claims someone named "Fat Tiger" (Pang Hu) is mean.
