## 【Scene: Green Bean Sprout Game Service Center - Booth A】

(Scene Perspective: Inside the enclosed KTV booth, the air is filled with blindingly bright and chaotic purple-pink neon marquee effects. The screen plays a pixel-style, low-resolution retro MV. **Xiao Mei**'s eyes are wide open, as she clutches a microphone in each hand, screeching out a melody that completely deviates from the big data model's predicted frequency—husky and full of raw power. **Motor** is in the front row, waving a lightstick so frantically he nearly dismantles the sofa.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_01.jpg)

**Xiao Mei** (screaming at the top of her lungs, voice cracking at one point): *"——Did you all hear that! This is the 'flaw' that big data can never fit! I don't need the post-AI-correction perfection! I don't need the precision filtered through the cloud! My pain... has a dynamic resonance with a soul pulse!!"*

**Motor** (with tears in his eyes, howling at Toby and Xiao Ying who were sitting calmly and eating): *"Everyone! Open your eyes and look! This is the last 'Native Human Diva'! This kind of sound field that makes your bones shake, how could those monkey teachers who only know how to feed big models and research call parameters ever understand?! Long live Xiao Mei! Long live soul timbre!!"*

**Xiao Ying** (sitting elegantly on the very edge of the sofa, holding a plate of Master Li Long’s secret cheese pork chops. She slowly picks up a steaming, heavily cheese-pulling pork chop and stuffs it directly into Toby's mouth just as he was about to complain): *"Toby, eat more, replenish your carbs. Don't you see Little Hoop's power indicator is nearly red? If these two keep singing until Monday, you'll need the strength to carry those scrapped databases back to the school. Come on, open your mouth, say ah——"*

**Toby** (chewing the pork chop, speaking unclearly with a face of bliss): *"Mmrph... Master Li Long's recipe truly can repair the soul. But Xiao Ying, don't you feel it? The hallway outside the booth... it seems to be getting quieter and quieter, even the sound of that cheap variable-frequency air conditioner's hum is gone."*

## 【The Debate Between AI and Soul】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_02.jpg)

**Xiao Mei** (putting down the microphone, gulping down a bitter sugar-free green tea like a general who just finished a battle): *"Xiao Ying, you're someone with a technical background. Look at this ghost town now, even the KTV guide vocals are fake voices generated by the latest 'Full-Automatic Emotional Repair Model'. Although it sounds perfect like heaven, listening to it too much makes you want to vomit. That kind of voice without any process errors, without any delay... isn't music at all, it's just the garbage excretion of computing power!"*

**Xiao Ying** (slowly pushing her holographic eye-mask up, her eyes calm): *"Indeed. In the eyes of big data, 'flaws' are just noise packets that need to be optimized. But for our 'Small Tooth', this irregular random shockwave is instead the best bait for detecting certain specific illegal processes. Small Tooth just reminded me that the reality logic nearby... is experiencing a high level of non-linear collapse."*

**Chia Seed** (A mysterious pale purple light emanates from the muzzle of the pink small gun): **「Warning: The emotional index of the subject and playmates has exceeded the 'Gravity Threshold'. Excessive noise will mask 'Collapse S.O.S.' signals from the underlying code. Suggesting suspension of entertainment processes; be alert for surrounding data infiltration.」**

## 【The Harbinger of Death: The Restroom's Call】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_06/Image_03.jpg)

(Scene: Motor suddenly stands up wobbling, clutching his stomach, pointing at the booth door with a grimacing yet embarrassed expression.)

**Motor**: *"Oh... everyone, Xiao Mei Goddess's voice was just too shocking, it shocked my rectum into a data conflict. I'm going to the restroom for a 'Data Cleansing', be right back! Xiao Mei, when I get back, you must sing that classic 'Cloud Soul Love Unfinished' with me!"*

**Xiao Mei** (waving her hand grandly): *"Go and return quickly! Don't slack off in the restroom! By the way, the door lock here seems a bit rusted due to oxidation, don't pull the deadbolt too hard when you go in, lest you get locked inside and fall in love with an AI!"*

(Scene Close-up: The moment Motor pushes open the booth door and steps out, the fluorescent light in the hallway weirdly flickers to a ghostly green with a "clack." His silhouette at the end of the hallway's darkness appears unnaturally thin, and his shadow on the wall actually begins to tremble and stretch abnormally, with several pairs of twisted, prickly virtual arms slowly emerging from the shadow's back.)
