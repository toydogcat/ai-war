## 【Location: Outside Zi Qiang Tunnel】

Sakura, dragged off the bus by Lu Qing, watches the Route 645 bus drive into the tunnel.

**Sakura**: Fine, let go! Can't you speak calmly? You're going to ruin my clothes. Fine, let's go to the police station. I'll see what you have to say to them.

Lu Qing looks completely relaxed.

(Screen: Outside the tunnel at dawn. Lu Qing leans against a wall casually, while Sakura shouts at her, pointing at the receding bus. The tunnel looks deep and bottomless in the background.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_01.jpg)

---

(Screen: Sakura has her hands on her hips, looking skeptical. Lu Qing holds a finger to her lips, mysteriously explaining the "weirdness" on the bus.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_02.jpg)

**Lu Qing**: Why go to a police station? Just go home. Don't go through the Zi Qiang Tunnel; there's something weird inside. You should thank me for saving you. No need to thank me!

**Sakura**: What are you talking about? My BBQ gear is on that bus! This place is in the middle of nowhere. You drop me here and I'm supposed to thank you?!

**Lu Qing**: Didn't you notice? A Jiangshi (hopping vampire) crawled onto the back of the bus. I saved your life! Also, the Zi Qiang Tunnel at night seems to lead to who-knows-where. It's spooky. I need to go back to Guandu Temple to get some sacred treasures, otherwise, it's too dangerous.

---

(Screen: Sakura points toward the tunnel with a "Are you kidding me?" expression. She mockingly poses like a zombie in Qing Dynasty clothes. In the background, a terrifying silhouette of a Jiangshi is shown for contrast.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_03.jpg)

**Sakura**: Now that you mention it, there was someone in Qing Dynasty clothes at the back. I thought they were going to a film set or something. It's late and I'm tired. Wait, why are you steering me toward something weird? Can't we just think normally?

**Lu Qing**: I'm telling you, this world has many unknown, mysterious events. For example, that person you saw in Qing Dynasty clothes is actually a Jiangshi. When people die with unreleased resentment, they become Jiangshis. They are much stronger than your average "monkey."

---

(Screen: Lu Qing explains the concept of "resentment accumulation." Faint blue projections of Taoist runes or ancient structures appear behind her, hinting at her extraordinary nature.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_04.jpg)

**Sakura**: Stop with the ghost stories. Mysterious events? If there were so many, wouldn't everyone have been eaten by now? Stop lying to me.

**Lu Qing**: You don't need to be too afraid. These events follow patterns. As long as you observe and don't touch them, you'll be fine. Plus, there are many skilled professionals and mysterious lineages protecting everyone. The government also has special agencies to handle these things. It's not as scary as it sounds.

---

(Screen: Lu Qing acts like it's no big deal, pointing to the sky or far away, as if suggesting the government and professionals are all around. Sakura looks confused.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_05.jpg)

**Sakura**: So you dragged me off the bus and cost me my sleep just to tell me... "don't be afraid"? And where are these "professionals" you speak of?

**Lu Qing**: Me! Didn't I just save you? I'm on my way to Guandu Temple.

**Sakura**: Stop, stop. I don't want to hear your nonsense. You're a "professional"? Then why didn't you go fight that Jiangshi? Were you scared?! "Professional" my foot. You're just bragging.

---

(Screen: Lu Qing pinches her nose with a disgusted look, explaining she didn't want to touch the "dirty, smelly" Jiangshi. Sakura is speechless.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_06.jpg)

**Lu Qing**: To be honest... I was a bit scared. Jiangshis are dirty, smelly, and unhygienic. You can't touch them with your hands, or you'll get sick.

**Sakura**: You're scared but you make it sound so logical. I'm not playing with you anymore, kid. I'm tired and I'm going home.

**Lu Qing**: I'm going to Guandu Temple first. Later, I'll activate the "Kun-Xian-Suo" (Immortal-Binding Lock) and lock that Jiangshi in the temple's cellar!

---

(Screen: Lu Qing mentions Guandu Temple. The background displays a pitch-black cellar and a glowing projections of the "Immortal-Binding Lock.")

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_07.jpg)

**Sakura**: Sure, keep making up stories to justify your fear. Little girl, let me tell you... there's a job called "actor." They wear costumes and makeup to earn a living. You'll understand when you grow up. Now go home and stop messing around.

**Lu Qing**: Well, I noticed you have a mysterious, weakened artifact. I thought we could exchange some notes.

---

(Screen: Sakura mocks Lu Qing, but Lu Qing stops arguing and stares mysteriously at the pink toy gun in Sakura's pocket. The gun is emitting a weak pink light.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_08.jpg)

**Chia Seed** (weakly): Sakura... are you ready to perform the Deep Soul Binding? I think I'm running out of energy. After fighting that ghost, I'm too weak.

**Sakura**: Uh, let me think about it. I haven't decided yet.

**Lu Qing**: Since we met by chance, I'll leave a "Grand Opportunity" in your pink toy gun.

---

Lu Qing quickly forms a hand seal, sending something into Chia Seed, then she vanishes in a flash.

(Screen: Lu Qing forms a hand seal. A violet-golden light shoots from her fingertip into the pink gun. Sakura is stunned as Lu Qing's figure begins to blur.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_09.jpg)

**Sakura**: Why is everything getting so weird lately? Am I really walking back to Green Sprout? So tired... why is this world so difficult to manage? This is life, I guess... QQ

## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 09 Supplement

### 👧 Anomalies, Ghosts, and Jiangshis
* **Anomaly**: Mysterious events that exist in this world.
* **Ghost**: A type of anomaly; formless energy that requires special handling. Skilled humans can deal with them.
* **Jiangshi**: A type of anomaly; physical monsters that can be dealt with via Maoshan arts.

### 💎 The Grand Opportunity
- **Origin**: A blessing bestowed upon the "Chia Seed" AI by the mysterious girl Lu Qing.
- **Function**: Injects a computing logic that doesn't belong to this era into the pink toy gun (AI host). It seems to significantly increase the AI's "spirituality" and "energy storage capacity."

### 🌸 Sakura (Xiao Ying)
- **Identity**: From Sushi Nation, manager of Green Sprout.
- **Status**: Exhausted, sleepy, carrying leftover BBQ gear.

### 🤖 Chia Seed
- **Status**: Energy depleted, in a "semi-hibernation" state.
- **Transmutation**: After receiving the Grand Opportunity, its logic becomes "leap-frogging," often uttering strange remarks that blend philosophy with sarcastic commentary.

### 👧 Lu Qing
- **Identity**: A mysterious girl from Guandu Temple.
- **Personality**: Lively, loves to joke, but possesses unfathomable strength.
- **Tools**: Possesses Taoist artifacts like the "Kun-Xian-Suo."
