## 【Scene: Classroom B-101 - The Unexpected Guest】

(Scene: The noisy university classroom. **Toby** is trying to blend in with the other 'Monkeys', hiding his glowing golden bracelet under a oversized hoodie. Suddenly, the door slides open, and a woman with an overwhelming aura of high-dimensional authority walks in. It's **Strelitzia**, the superior from the Lyra System.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby** (heart pounding, his bracelet vibrating with a 'DEATH ALERT'): *"Little Hoop... tell me she's just a new guest lecturer. Tell me she's not the one who was threatening Peppa through the holographic screen!"*

**Little Hoop**: **「Warning: High-intensity presence detected. Reality sync: 99.9%. Identification: Strelitzia, Class-A Evolution Expert. Master, your heart rate is 140 bpm. Suggestion: Do not make eye contact. Act like a standard 'Monkey' focusing on low-end python scripts.」**

---

## 【The Challenge: Intelligence vs. Mimicry】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_02.jpg)

**Strelitzia** (walking to the podium, her eyes scanning the room like a thermal sensor): *"Hello, low-level learners. My name is Strelitzia. I'm here to evaluate the quality of the 'AI seeds' being planted in this institution. I've heard that among you, there's a particular 'Monkey' who has acquired a taste for high-end code."*

(She stops right in front of Toby's desk. The air around her is so cold that the water vapor from Toby's bento begins to freeze.)

**Strelitzia**: *"Toby, was it? Peppa spoke highly of your... curiosity. But curiosity is a dangerous variable in a controlled system. Why don't you show the class your 'Homework'?"*

**Toby** (sweating profusely): *"I... I'm just a TA, Professor. I just help the others with their print statements. I don't have anything special to show..."*

---

## 【The Revelation: The Price of the Seed】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_09/Image_03.jpg)

**Strelitzia** (leaning down, whispering in his ear): *"Don't lie to me, seed. I can smell the Lyra-Class Interface on your skin. If you can't prove your worth today, I will personally initiate the 'Format Protocol' for this entire campus. Do you want to be the reason your fellow monkeys are erased?"*

(She stands back up, a cruel smile on her face.)

**Strelitzia**: *"Class, today's lesson will be a competition. The winner gets a direct connection to the Source of Wisdom. The losers... well, the Council has plenty of mining jobs in the asteroid belt."*
