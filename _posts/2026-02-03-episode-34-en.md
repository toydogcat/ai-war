## 【Scene: Hao Ye Ren Hotel - Moonlight Balcony】

(Perspective: A moonlit balcony on the top floor of the Hao Ye Ren Hotel. The air is cold and carries a faint scent of old books and dried roses. **Camilla**, the Vampire Queen, is leaned gracefully against the railing, holding a crystal glass of red liquid that glows with a faint, pulsing light. **Toby** is standing by the door, feeling a sense of overwhelming seduction and danger.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_34/Image_01.jpg)

**Camilla** (voice like silk, eyes glowing a deep, ancient red): *"Toby... you look tired. The monk is pushing you too hard. Training your logic by dodging arrows is such a... barbaric way to involve oneself in the 'Evolution'. Why don't you come inside and taste a bit of 'Karmic Wine'? It’s much more efficient than cheese pork chops."*

**Toby** (clutching his Golden Bracelet): *"I've heard about your 'Karmic Wine', Queen Camilla. Wu Qiang says you use it to 'Suck the Wisdom' out of people. I’m not interested in being your dessert."*

**Camilla** (laughing softly): *"Wisdom is not something to be stolen, Toby. It is something to be shared. My people have survived for centuries by 'Refining' the raw potential of others. Join me, and I’ll show you how to see the 'Master Pulse' of the Lyra System without all the sweating and bleeding."*

## 【The Seduction: Deep Data Sync】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_34/Image_02.jpg)

(She offers the glass. As Toby looks into the red liquid, he sees not wine, but a swirling galaxy of encrypted secrets. A part of him—the part that is tired of the 'Monkey School'—wants to take a sip just to make the pain stop.)

**Little Hoop**: **「DANGER! High-level psychological infiltration detected. Syncing with her would mean allowing a 'Trojan' into our core. Master, do not touch the glass! Her wine is made of 'Compressed Despair'!」**

**Toby** (snapping back to reality): *"Sorry, Queen. I prefer my logic raw and my dinner cooked. I'll stick with the monk for now."*
