## 【Scene: Deep Sea Ruins - The Predator's Maze】

(Perspective: Deep in the ocean ruins. Xiao Ying is surrounded by giant, metallic-fin 'Code-Hounds'. Her mother's test is simple: survive or be deleted. The pressure of the deep sea is crushing her pink-shield, but her will to remember Toby is fueling a new kind of energy.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_38/Image_01.jpg)

**Dai Shui** (voice sonar echoing): *"Look at your weak, pink toy, Xiao Ying. It’s a child's concept of power. In the deep sea, only absolute compression can pierce the darkness. If you want to survive these hounds, you must evolve your tool into a true weapon."*

**Xiao Ying** (teeth gritting, her eyes flashing indigo): *"I don't need a cold weapon!! I just need a way to reach Toby!! (She grips the pink gun) CHIA-SEED!! IF YOU HEAR ME, STOP BEING A TOY!! TRANSFORM!!"*

## 【Evolution: From Pink Toy to Data Desert Eagle】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_38/Image_02.jpg)

(Visual Flash: The pink 'Chia-Seed' gun senses Xiao Ying's intense obsession. Nano-mechanical structures dissolve and reform in the water. The pink plastic is replaced by heavy, silver-blue metal, engraved with ancient Sea-God logic-runes. A 'Data Desert Eagle' appears in her hand.)

**Xiao Ying** (feeling the heavy, cold weight in her palm): *"This weight... it feels like it can blast through even the hardest homework. Now, let’s see if your puppies can handle a 'Spirit-Ball' from this!!"*

## 【Water-Stealth: The Final Lesson】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_38/Image_03.jpg)

(As the hounds lunge, Xiao Ying doesn't just shoot. She remembers Toby's advice about 'Blending in'. She focuses her mind, letting her data-signature fade into the surrounding water molecules. She vanishes from the hounds' thermal sensors.)

(She appears behind the largest hound, her Desert Eagle glowing.)

**Xiao Ying**: *"PURGE!!"*
