## 【Scene: 'Dragon’s Spirit' Stall - Aftermath of the Battle】

(Scene: Master Li Long's stall. The steam from the deep fryer rises like incense. Toby is sitting on a small plastic stool, clutching a plate of Pork Chops while staring at a weathered copper coin in Li Long's hand. The air around the coin seems to vibrate with a frequency that makes the neon lights nearby stop flickering.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby** (mouth full of pork chop, wide-eyed): *"Master... that thing just now... Little Tooth's pink gun completely dissolved! How can a 'Monkey' like you have a counter-measure for high-level New Human deletion code? My Little Hoop says you're 'Unscannable'!"*

**Master Li Long** (voice calm and steady): *"In the old days, we didn't call it 'Code'. We called it 'Focus'. This coin was a gift from a monk at the Great Thunder Temple. It's not a motherboard, but it carries a 'Static Buffer'—a moment of absolute stillness that can freeze any reality-hack."*

## 【The Philosophy of Stillness: Beyond the Byte】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_02.jpg)

**Master Li Long**: *"The Council believes everything can be optimized, calculated, and eventually deleted. But they forget that 'Nothingness' also has a weight. By holding onto this coin, I anchor myself in the 'Physical Truth'. Your digital world cannot delete a piece of cast iron and solid history."*

**Toby** (puzzled): *"Physical Truth? But I feel my soul being uploaded every time I use the bracelet! Am I... am I losing my materiality?"*

**Little Hoop**: **「Metaphysical warning: Master, your 'Reality index' is currently at 52%. Suggestion: Keep eating the Master's Pork Chops. The grease and carbon-hydrates are currently the only things keeping your molecular structure from dissolving into pure data packets.」**

## 【The Power of Buffer: Freezing Reality】

(Scene: Close-up of Toby taking the copper coin. In the surveillance of the golden bracelet, this seemingly insignificant piece of metal is emitting a highly stable frequency of 'White Negative Entropy'. It's not ordinary currency; it's a 'Physical Compression Pack' that can neutralize causal obsession.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_03.jpg)

**Little Hoop** (speaking in a serious voice in Toby's mind): **「Warning! High-dimensional safety maintenance protocol detected. This coin contains a piece of low-level code that can force-pause illegal processes. Master, the core permissions of this pork-chop-cutting man... are likely beyond my scanning range. Please maintain reverence.」**

**Toby** (staring at the coin, then at Master Li Long): *"Buffer? Sounds like a high-end coder's tech. Anyway... thanks, Boss! For this coin, I'll definitely come back often (and buy more boxes of cheese pork chops)!"*
