## 【Scene: Inside Green Sprout Shop】

(Screen: Sakura watches Toby carrying several bags of steaming cheese pork chops. Toby is handing a piece of crispy pork with melting cheese strings to Sakura.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_01.jpg)

**Sakura**: Toby, why do you have so many cheese pork chops? Can you even finish them all?

**Toby**: I was so hungry I bought a ton. Thanks for giving me those old machines; they were a huge help. Do you want some?

**Sakura**: Wow, you moved fast! That mountain of trash was cleared in a flash. I don't have money for a cleaning fee, though. Cheese pork chops are too greasy for me; I'll pass.

---

(Screen: Sakura solemnly shows the stack of yellow joss paper to Toby. Toby leans back in shock, his face turning pale green.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_02.jpg)

**Sakura**: Forget that. I just ran into something weird. Look at this stack of joss paper. I forgot toilet paper earlier and ended up using this... it scared the life out of me.

**Toby** (Shocked): Your butt is that tough? Doesn't it hurt?

**Sakura**: Where is your focus?! Shouldn't you be asking who gave me the joss paper? I still don't know who it was. The restroom was empty when I came out. Isn't that strange?

---

(Screen: Xiao Huan (the gold bracelet) emits a faint pulse of light. The translucent holographic avatar of Xiao Huan looks tired and is yawning.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_03.jpg)

**Toby**: It is indeed mysterious. I'm definitely not using the toilets around here. I'll find another one. Let me ask Xiao Huan if he can solve this.

**Xiao Huan**: I've just exhausted my free usage limit for this month. I need to rest. I can only perform simple routine tasks now, like flattery, singing, or just chatting.

**Sakura**: Aren't you a teaching assistant at the Monkey School? Wait, some of your schoolmates, Xiao Mei and Ji Che, are coming over later for an overnight karaoke session. They even booked the accompanied singing service. we'll be in Green Sprout Room A.

---

(Screen: Sakura excitedly waves a list. The screen shows the reservation record for \"Green Sprout Room A,\" with a blurred photo of Xiao Mei and Ji Che in the background.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_04.jpg)

**Sakura**: Ji Che paid for it. He even added extra to have us join in. I told him we could do overnight karaoke, but he'd have to cover the food list—at a premium. Ji Che said money's no object, as long as we have fun. So Toby, here's the list. Go do the shopping, and you can buy whatever you want to eat for yourself. I want a dozen sugar-free green teas, no ice. I'll pay you back later.

**Toby**: It's fine. You've already given me more than enough \"trash.\" I'd feel bad taking more. Let's just have fun tonight. The main thing is to avoid the toilets around here. I'll go get the food now!

Toby vanishes like a whirlwind to go shopping.

---

(Screen: Toby turns and dashes out of the shop, leaving a swirl of data particles in his wake. Sakura waves from behind, shouting, \"Sugar-free green tea, no ice!\")

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_05.jpg)

**Sakura**: Hmm, do I really send Toby to deal with trash too often? He seems to remember it... Guess I'll have to make sure Toby has a blast tonight. I'll stuff him with food so I can sing happily with Xiao Mei. He'll be happy with all that junk food anyway.

**Chia Seed**: Why is everyone's focus so strange? Are humans always such peculiar creatures?

**Sakura**: Ah, Ya, what did you say?

**Chia Seed**: I said my master has unique logic, rigorous thinking, and a beautiful singing voice. A rare talent indeed.

## 【Location: Food Court Basement level 3】

(Screen: Boss Li Long is deep-frying pork chops over a roaring flame, which reflects off his determined face. In the background, mysterious kitchenware marked \"Da Lei Yin Temple\" can be seen.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_06.jpg)

**Toby**: Boss, I want this, that, and those—all of them. And a dozen sugar-free green teas, no ice.

**Li Long**: Are you throwing a party? Since you're such a handsome guy, I'll tell you a secret—and not just because you're buying so much. Have you heard the rumors? They say the toilets near the back alley of the Sheng Mu Yuan food court are... \"unclean.\"

---

The boss continues packing the food.

(Screen: Li Long pauses his work and whispers to Toby. The air seems to turn cold, and a ghostly silhouette of a little girl in red appears in the restroom doorway.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_07.jpg)

**Toby**: Exactly! Someone's not doing a good job cleaning. Should we find them and give them a talking to?

**Li Long**: Sigh, you don't understand. It's 2040; who uses humans to clean toilets anymore? It's all automated robots. When I say \"unclean,\" I mean several anonymous reports of strange sounds or movements in the restroom—but nobody is ever there when people check. Some suspect it's related to a tragic accident nearby. A couple was window-shopping and lost track of their little girl. She vanished without a trace, rumored to have been abducted. To this day, she hasn't been found.

---

(Screen: Toby looks at a yellowed, curled missing person poster on the wall. The girl's photo matches the silhouette Li Long just described.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_08.jpg)

**Toby**: I've heard of that. But why link the two? Haven't the police solved it yet?

**Li Long**: This part is just my personal observation—no proof. Since we insist on handmade food and refuse mechanization, I'm often here late. Once, while heading to the restroom, I saw the back of a little girl going inside. I went in after her, but she was gone. I couldn't find her anywhere. I thought I was just overworked and seeing things.

**Toby**: Boss, you really are working too hard. Linking those two things is just exhaustion talking.

**Li Long**: Look, they only recently put this missing person poster back up. I didn't notice it before, but once I saw the flyer, I realized... the silhouette, the clothes... they matched the back I saw that night. But again, no proof. Just something to chat about.

---

(Screen: Li Long hands Toby a glowing electronic component amulet engraved with Sanskrit. Toby looks at a $20,000 digital bill with a \"Boss, are you robbing me?\" expression.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_05/Image_09.jpg)

**Toby**: Boss, don't scare me! I'm going karaoke tonight; I won't dare go to the bathroom now, and that'll be a serious problem.

**Li Long**: Oh, don't be afraid. Here, take this amulet from Da Lei Yin Temple. I hear it's very effective. I'm giving it to you because we're destined to meet—not just because you're a big spender and a regular.

**Toby**: Oof... first of all, why does everyone love giving me \"trash\"? Who knows if this charm actually works? But Boss, I get the hint—you want me to come back often. Don't worry, your cheese pork chops and curry rice are delicious. I'll definitely be back.

**Li Long**: Everything's packed. That'll be $13,000... I'll round it up to $20,000 for you as a special price.

**Toby**: Hahaha! Boss, your math is amazing... Is this amulet really that expensive?!

Little did he know, this Da Lei Yin Temple amulet was crafted by Abbot Wukong. It contains secret strategies and residual divine wisdom, a lucky find for Toby that will pave the way for the destiny to come.
