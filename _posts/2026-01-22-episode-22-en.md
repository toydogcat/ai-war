## 【Scene: Guandu Temple - Backyard Classical Tea Room】

Peppa holds a lotus brooch while drinking tea and eating crackers with Luqing and Bruce Lee.

**Bruce Lee**: Miss Luqing, can you tell me about the current situation?

(Visual: Guandu Temple backyard tea room; Luqing, Bruce Lee, and Peppa are sitting around a red wooden table drinking tea.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_01.jpg)

**Luqing**: It's like this: we originally worshipped the Holy Mother of Heaven, and it has always been peaceful. However, recently, the Holy Mother appeared in a dream saying that the "Mo Shen Zai" suppressed within the temple is breaking free. She said that there are capable people at Da Lei Yin Temple, so she wrote a letter asking for support—to either kill it or seal it again, we wouldn't mind.

**Bruce Lee**: I see. So all I have to do is go into the temple and have a fight with this Mo Shen Zai, right?

---

**Luqing**: Yes, exactly. We're counting on you. we'll be here drinking tea and eating cookies waiting for you to return.

**Bruce Lee**: You're not planning to help? Have you made any effort yourselves? I'm not criticizing you for being "hand-out seekers," don't get me wrong.

(Visual: Luqing lightly waves her right hand; a massive rock garden next to her instantly explodes into powder, yet she continues to sip tea gracefully.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_02.jpg)

Luqing waves her right hand, and a burst of energy instantly shatters the nearby 3-foot tall rock garden.

**Luqing** (crying): Look at me, a weak young woman with no strength at all. How could I go around fighting and killing? Are you going or not? You've been drinking tea and eating cookies for a long time—are you trying to get a free meal?

---

**Bruce Lee**: Master mentioned that this Mo Shen Zai has amazing strength. I'm just a lay disciple and haven't enlightened to any "Dao" yet. Only senior brothers of the "Wu" generation can handle such a fight. Coincidentally, I got separated from my senior brother. Can't I just pay for these refreshments myself? I really can't win this.

**Luqing**: Our Guandu Temple is a large and wealthy establishment; would we lack money from a little monk like you? If you say you can't win, can I assume that Da Lei Yin Temple is just a hollow name?

(Visual: Bruce Lee looks at the pulverized rock garden, sweating profusely with a comical expression, while Luqing pretends to cry nearby.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_03.jpg)

**Bruce Lee**: I won't allow you to insult Da Lei Yin Temple! You can say I'm no good, but saying Da Lei Yin Temple is no good is something I cannot tolerate. Where is that Mo Shen Zai? Point the way, and I'll be back in a flash.

**Peppa**: Here, let me pin this lotus brooch on you. It's a souvenir from here and also a navigator. The underground tunnels of Guandu Temple are huge; just follow the guidance of the lotus to find the Mo Shen Zai.

---

After pinning on the lotus, Bruce Lee rushes toward the underground tunnels, shouting "Monster, where are you going!".

**Peppa**: Luqing, was it okay to provoke him like that? Won't there be danger?

(Visual: Peppa stands at the entrance of the tea room, watching Bruce Lee's back as he runs toward the tunnel, looking somewhat worried.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_04.jpg)

**Luqing**: Didn't you ask me to help you find a "good seed"? That's why I wrote to Da Lei Yin Temple for help. I just didn't expect the Abbot's excuse to be like this. He has a determined look and must have undergone rigorous training. Plus, you gave him the Lyra wisdom artifact, so it should be fine.

**Peppa**: That lotus brooch artifact hasn't recognized a master yet. A lotus grows out of the mud but remains unstained; "Xiao Lian" is very picky. But once accepted, she has incredible regenerative and purifying power—infinite potential. My only fear is that she might be too picky.

---

## 【Scene: Secret Chamber of Guandu Temple】

The Mo Shen Zai sealed in Guandu Temple is an anomaly, like a hybrid of human and insect—similar to Cell from Dragon Ball.

**Mo Shen Zai**: I'm so hungry! How long do you plan to keep me locked up? At least send me some food. "Xiao Jiang" was supposed to give me the sacrifices he caught... sigh, I'm starving.

(Visual: Deep in the secret chamber, the Mo Shen Zai is bound by heavy "Immortal-Trapping Chains"; it looks like a hybrid of human and insect (similar to Cell).)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_05.jpg)

Bruce Lee strides into the chamber.

**Bruce Lee**: You're the Mo Shen Zai? Take my flurry of punches!

---

**Mo Shen Zai**: Who are you? Do we know each other? I have no grudge against you; why attack me the moment you see me? Is this some new interrogation tactic?

(Visual: Bruce Lee rushes into the room, taking a martial arts stance and shouting at the chained Mo Shen Zai.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_06.jpg)

**Bruce Lee**: I have nothing to say to you. If you don't die, the reputation of Da Lei Yin Temple will be disgraced. So, you must die.

**Mo Shen Zai**: Although I don't quite understand what you're saying, I can't just let you keep hitting me. Who sent you?

---

**Bruce Lee**: Who sent me? I don't know!! Why do you care who sent me? I'm just going to hit you.

(Visual: The Mo Shen Zai looks at Bruce Lee with an innocent face, tilting its head in confusion.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_07.jpg)

**Mo Shen Zai**: Ah, they catch me with Immortal-Trapping Chains, then complain I'm dirty and smelly and refuse to touch me, and then they send over a little idiot! Is this some kind of "honey trap"? I don't get it, I really don't get you!! Why do this to me?

**Bruce Lee**: You have a lot of last words. Just stay still and let me beat you to death; it'll be better for both of us.

---

**Mo Shen Zai**: Look, I'm chained up right now and can't move. Can you tell me how beating me to death is good for you?

**Bruce Lee**: It's good because... because... um, I don't know why it's good.

(Visual: Bruce Lee lowers his fists and sits on the floor of the chamber to "reason" with the Mo Shen Zai.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_08.jpg)

**Mo Shen Zai**: Oh, so you don't know why?

**Bruce Lee**: Right, I don't know. You tell me—if I kill you, how is it good for me?

---

**Mo Shen Zai**: Don't you want to have a fair duel with me? That way, if you kill me, you'll have glory—having defeated me in a fair fight.

(Visual: Bruce Lee looks at the strangely shaped keys hanging on the wall, with the Mo Shen Zai "instructing" him from the side.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_09.jpg)

**Bruce Lee**: Seems like it... or maybe not. As long as I kill you, it's fine. Whatever, you're right; we should have a fair duel.

**Mo Shen Zai**: Then help me unlock these Immortal-Trapping Chains. The key is hanging on the wall behind you—yes, that strangely shaped thing is the key.

---

**Mo Shen Zai**: Then help me break the energy seal. Just break that statue nearby. If you can't break it, that's okay; just turn it around so it isn't facing me, and I can get out myself. Also, prepare a meal for me—buns or steamed bread will do.

(Visual: Bruce Lee hands a brown sugar bun to the Mo Shen Zai, who looks deeply moved; broken chains lie in the background.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_10.jpg)

Bruce Lee obediently did everything.

**Bruce Lee**: I only have brown sugar buns; I didn't bring any extra food. I heard there's a lot of delicious food in Taiwan. Can you be beaten to death now? Wait, no—can we have a fair duel now?

---

## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 22 Addendum

### 🦟 Mo Shen Zai
An anomalous warrior sealed at the bottom of the secret chamber of Guandu Temple.
- **Form**: A hybrid evolutionary entity of human and insect.
- **Ability**: Linked to the source of insectoid intelligence, possessing immense physical strength and an indestructible exoskeleton.
- **Weakness**: Has not yet completed final evolution and lacks resistance to purification attacks from high-level "Lotus Code."

### 👨‍🦱 Bruce Lee (Li Xiaolong)
- **Identity**: A lay disciple of Da Lei Yin Temple.
- **Lyra Artifact**: The Lotus.
- **Status**: Has not yet fully mastered "Xiao Lian."

### 🏵️ Artifact: Xiao Lian (The Lotus)
- **Attribute**: A high-level AI with self-awareness.
- **Function**: Logic reshaping, soul scanning.
