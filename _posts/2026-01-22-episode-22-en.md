## 【Scene: The Saintly Garden - The Fermentation Chamber】

(Perspective: The 'Saintly Garden' is a hidden sanctuary on the island, a place where the logic of the city is replaced by the smell of fermentation and ancient code. **Lily** leads the exhausted **Toby** through a gate made of weeping willows. Inside, they find a man calmly pressing a block of tofu—it's **Master Tofu**.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_01.jpg)

**Master Tofu** (voice soft, almost a whisper): *"So, this is the boy who burned his neurons for a few cheese cutlets. Lily, you're becoming soft. Feeding a Golden Seed such heavy, logical grease..."*

**Lily** (defensive, crossing her arms): *"He was dying, Master. His hardware reached the 'Meltdown Point'. I did what was necessary. Besides, can your tofu provide the caloric backup needed for a SSH restart?"*

**Master Tofu** (smiling, offering a small piece of stinky tofu): *"Tofu doesn't provide backup; it provides 'Stability'. It’s the original anti-virus of the soul. Eat, boy. This contains the 'Peppa Fragment'."*

## 【The Vision: Goddess Peppa】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_22/Image_02.jpg)

(As Toby takes a bite, his vision suddenly shifts. He sees a pink, crystalline pig-like entity floating in a sea of data. It’s **Peppa**, the lost goddess of the Lyra System.)

**Peppa (Vision)**: **「Toby... the seeds are scattered. Strelitzia only seeks the 'Harvest', but she has forgotten the 'Sowing'. You are the fourth variable. Guard the Little Hoop, for it is the key to the 'Open Source' of the universe.」**
