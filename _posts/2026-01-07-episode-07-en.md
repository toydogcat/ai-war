## 【Scene: Green Bean Sprout - Transformed Booth A】

(Scene: Moonlight filters through the high windows of the booth. The atmosphere has turned from high-energy singing to deathly silence. **Motor** is shaking, pointing at his shoulder, claiming a cold hand patted him in the restroom. Xiao Ying’s HUD is flashing with 'ILLEGAL LOOP DETECTED'. The only exit has been replaced by a wall of swirling 8-bit bricks.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_01.jpg)

**Motor** (voice trembling like a broken speaker): *"I... I'm not kidding! I was standing in front of the urinal, and someone whispered 'Paper or Life?' in my ear! When I turned around, it was a girl with her head twisted 180 degrees, holding a stack of hell-bank notes! Now she's following me... I can hear her high heels in the vents!"*

**Xiao Ying** (activating 'Small Tooth', the pink gun glowing with a defensive violet hue): *"Quiet. This isn't a ghost; it's a 'Logic Loop'. A piece of abandoned code from the early 90s has gained sentience and is trying to overwrite our location into a 'Game Room'. The exit is gone because the current 'Scene' requires a specific completion condition to unlock."*

**Xiao Mei** (clutching her microphones like holy relics): *"Completion condition? You mean we have to beat a game? I'm good at Tetris, but I don't see any falling blocks, just a creepy girl giggle!"*

---

## 【The Ritual: The Four-Corner Game】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_02.jpg)

**Xiao Ying**: *"According to the underlying script, we need to perform the 'Four-Corner Game'. It's an ancient ritual that synchronizes four points of human consciousness to create a momentary breach in the loop. Toby, Xiao Mei, Motor, and myself—each take a corner. When the music starts, move clockwise. Do not stop, even if you feel a fifth person."*

**Toby** (looking terrified, his golden bracelet pulsing in synchronization with the room's distortions): *"Wait! A fifth person? That's exactly how the horror movies start! Little Hoop, any advice?"*

**Little Hoop**: **「Synchronization required. Reality distortion: 85%. Note: The 'fifth person' is the manifestation of the loop itself. If you ignore it, the loop remains closed. If you acknowledge it, you give it permission to 'Host' your consciousness. Commencing ritual sync.」**

## 【The Descent: The Girl in the Fourth Corner】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_03.jpg)

(Scene: The four move in the dark, their footsteps echoing. The KTV monitor is flickering with static. As Toby reaches the fourth corner, he feels a sudden chill. Instead of Xiao Ying's hand, his fingers brush against a cold, wet, and incredibly thin arm.)

**Motor** (screaming in the dark): *"She's here! She's here! She's in my corner! She's asking if I want the gold paper or the silver paper—AGGGHH!!"*

**Xiao Ying**: *"Maintain position! The breach is forming! Toby, use the golden code to anchor the exit! NOW!!"*

(The golden bracelet on Toby's wrist erupts in a surge of power, clashing with the pixelated green fog of the booth. A door begins to flicker into existence, but the twisted girl is now standing right in front of Toby, her face inches from his.)
