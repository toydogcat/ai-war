## 【Location: Corridor Outside the Restroom】

(Screen: In the dimly lit KTV corridor, Xiao Huan (the gold bracelet) glows intensely. Toby, Sakura, and Ji Che are huddled together, the atmosphere tense. Ji Che has tear streaks on his face, while Sakura checks her pink toy gun.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby**: Xiao Huan... are you there? What is happening?

**Xiao Huan**: This is a supernatural event, what you'd call a possession. The ghost is playing hide and seek with you. We have to catch it, or Xiao Mei will be 'substituted' (taken as a replacement). Saving her is very dangerous for all of you. It seems there's no professional exorcist around.

**Ji Che**: We can't just leave Xiao Mei! We have to save her together.

**Sakura**: It's not like we can't... but it'll cost extra. You see, the situation has escalated. Ya, are you there?

---

(Screen: Close-up of Sakura. She is staring at her \"Chia Seed\" toy gun, which is flickering with pink data streams. The Chia Seed AI displays \"READY\" on the screen.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_02.jpg)

**Chia Seed**: I have enough energy to fire several spiritual bullets, but I need to aim directly at the ghost's Spirit Core. Since we haven't performed a Deep Binding yet, I have to rely on your manual aim. My advice... give up.

**Ji Che**: Money is no object! Wealth is just a material possession. You must help me save Xiao Mei!

**Toby**: Xiao Mei is my dear student. we cannot abandon her.

**Ji Che**: Exactly! So how do we play hide and seek with her? How do we find her?

---

(Screen: Toby holds the amulet. A massive, semi-transparent golden dome barrier envelops the group, with the faint silhouette of an ancient Buddhist Master visible above the field.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_03.jpg)

**Xiao Huan**: Toby, since you insist on saving her... because I've used up this month's free data, I can't affect the physical world. However, through my analysis, there's only one solution. That amulet on your chest—it's the key. It can protect you from possession with its defensive barrier. It's the Master's power. To save Xiao Mei, we must summon the Master's Phantom. It will be dangerous for you, Toby. I'll have the Master's Phantom hold Xiao Mei to force the ghost out. Once it's out, Sakura will use Chia Seed to deliver a precision strike to the ghost's Spirit Core.

**Toby**: That sounds like a plan. But can we hit a ghost hidden inside someone? How big is the Spirit Core? And how do we find her? She's playing hide and seek!

**Chia Seed**: It's very difficult. The Spirit Core is tiny. With limited energy, the spiritual bullet must be perfectly precise. Missing by even 1mm could provoke the ghost into taking all of you as replacements.

---

(Screen: A tiny pink digital grid is reflected in Sakura's pupils. She is staring intently at the spatial distortions in the air where the ghost is hiding.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_04.jpg)

**Sakura**: I can't see the Spirit Core, but I've been observing the tiny air ripples. I'll just aim at the 'weird spots.' It's hard to explain.

**Chia Seed**: Do you have an Anomaly Detection talent? That's a rare ability. We have to try.

**Toby**: Wait, wait. All this planning... but how do we draw her out?

**Ji Che**: What are you guys arguing about? Is this 'hide and seek' that Four-Corner Game I've heard of?

---

(Screen: Everyone stands at the four corners of the pitch-black Room B. The gold bracelet trails four streaks of light in the dark, forming an unstable spatial frame.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_05.jpg)

**Sakura**: The final piece of the puzzle! I know how to lure her. We go into Room B, stand at the four corners of the rectangular room, and turn off the lights. I'll start, walking to the next corner, touching the person there, taking their place, then they move to the next. We continue this. If anyone has to stop, they cough. Since there are four of us, we'll summon the little girl to join in. If we find the game continuing without a break, it means there are five players. That's when we turn on the iPhone lights and execute the strike plan.

**Toby**: Sounds good, but let's count... you, me, Ji Che. That's only three of us. Xiao Mei is possessed. Can three people play the Four-Corner Game? Even if Xiao Mei joins, we're short. And you'll have very little time to aim once the phone lights go on.

**Sakura**: I know Room B like the back of my hand. I'll spot any anomaly instantly.

**Xiao Huan**: You really are an outlier. I don't usually believe in such human abilities, but I can use the Master's Phantom as the fourth player. However, after this, this amulet will likely be useless.

---

## 【Location: Room B】

After agreeing, they immediately set the plan in motion. The lights go out, and they start the Four-Corner Hide and Seek. After a while, they realize the chain isn't breaking. Instantly, they turn on their iPhone flashlights.

(Screen: A grand, semi-transparent Master's Phantom emitting a harmonious golden light is seen firmly yet gently hugging the distorted Xiao Mei from behind, forcing the dark shadow of the girl in red to separate from her.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_06.jpg)

**Xiao Huan**: Now! Go! Master, hold her!

The Master's Phantom embraces the eerily contorted Xiao Mei, chanting the Great Compassion Mantra.

**Xiao Mei**: Don't you want to play with me? Why did you stop? You are all mean!

The ghost of the little girl emerges in the void. To normal eyes, there's nothing, but via the Deep Binding, Xiao Huan sees it clearly. Chia Seed can't see it either and must be manually fired by Sakura.

---

(Screen: Ji Che looks at the suffering Xiao Mei with deep concern. Xiao Huan is shouting, \"NOW!\". Sakura completely calms her mind and closes her eyes, as if listening to the wind.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_07.jpg)

**Toby**: What do we do now? Is it out? Do we hit Xiao Mei?!

**Ji Che**: Xiao Mei, you are still so beautiful. Even possessed, you are my goddess!

**Xiao Huan**: Sakura, now! Can you see it? If not, we're finished!

Sakura remains perfectly still with her eyes closed, then suddenly snaps them open.

---

(Screen: Sakura opens her eyes and instantly fires seven thin pink laser beams. The lasers strike the ghost's core with petal-like precision. Space shatters like glass.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_08.jpg)

**Sakura**: I see the anomaly! Ya, rapid fire!

After seven rounds of firing, a crisp sound like shattering glass echoes. The Master's Phantom vanishes, and Toby's amulet loses its energy.

**Xiao Mei** (hoarse voice): What are you guys doing? Why am I here... everything hurts.

**Ji Che**: Xiao Mei, you're awake! Thank god!

---

Ji Che embraces Xiao Mei. Dawn is breaking. As Ji Che takes care of her, Toby and Sakura pack up their things. The party mood is gone. After some rest and tidying up...

(Screen: KTV entrance at dawn. Sunlight peeks through. Ji Che holds a conscious Xiao Mei. Toby and Sakura lean against the wall, exhausted. Toby's amulet has crumbled to ash.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_07/Image_09.jpg)

**Toby**: That's about it. I'm heading out. Sakura... you're a genius. A shooting genius.

**Sakura**: Alright, I'll take it from here. You have school tomorrow, right?

**Chia Seed** (thinking to itself): How can she perform like that without Deep Binding? Sakura's potential is terrifying. Peppa might have found another good seed.
