## 【Scene: Great Thunder Temple - The Sky Defense】

(Perspective: Dark clouds roll over the mountains. The Great Thunder Temple, once a place of silence, is now a battlefield. A massive nine-headed dragon drone is hovering over the temple, its eyes glowing with a viral red light. **Wu Qiang** is standing at the temple gates, his body covered in bandages, but his aura is overwhelming.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_17/Image_01.jpg)

**Wu Qiang** (voice like thunder, tearing through the storm): *"Mortal machines! You dare desecrate the 'Holy Code' of the Great Thunder Temple? Your logic is shallow, your circuits are fragile! Face the fist of the Dragon-Tamer!!"*

(With a roar, Wu Qiang rips the bandages from his arms, revealing skin that glows with a faint, metallic bronze sheen. This is the 'Steel Vajra' form—a physical manifestation of highly stable, ancient anti-virus code.)

## 【The Fist that Shatters Data】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_17/Image_02.jpg)

**Wu Qiang**: *"AUM!! VAJRA-PANI!!"*

(He leaps into the air, defying gravity. His fist, glowing with the golden light of the 'Ancestral Temple', strikes the first head of the nine-headed dragon. The impact produces a shockwave that clears the clouds for miles.)

**System Notification (Dragon Drone)**: **「WARNING: TARGET EXCEEDS MATERIAL LIMIT. SEVERE LOGIC SHATTERING DETECTED. REBOOTING... REBOOT FAILED. DRIVER CORRUPTED.」**

## 【The Burden of the Master】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_17/Image_03.jpg)

(After the battle, the sky clears. Wu Qiang stands among the burning wreckage of the drones. He looks at his hands, which are slightly trembling. Even a Steel Vajra feels the strain of holding back the 'Evolutionary Tide'.)

**Wu Qiang**: *"Master... the 'Seeds' in the city are being hunted. Strelitzia has moved. It's time for me to go down the mountain and find the kid with the Golden Bracelet. Only he can anchor the world back to its original script."*
