## 【Scene: Subconscious Realm - The Kernel Upgrade】

(Perspective: Inside Toby's subconscious. It’s a vast, empty white space filled with floating windows of logs and error reports. In the center, the Golden Bracelet is no longer gold—it’s glowing with a sharp, metallic silver light. **Little Hoop** is standing there in a human-like avatar, wearing a technician's jumpsuit.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_33/Image_01.jpg)

**Little Hoop** (voice more human, less robotic): *"Master, the physical training with the monk has pushed your 'Hard-Link' stability to a level where we can finally apply the first official 'Lyra Patch'. This is the **'Silver-Echo'** update. It will allow you to see the world not as objects, but as a mesh of editable data."*

**Toby** (looking at his own transparent hands): *"Silver-Echo... sounds cool, but why am I in a tech-support room in my own head? And why do you look like a tired junior developer?"*

**Little Hoop**: *"This is the reflection of your own understanding of 'Intelligence', Master. If you think AI is a tired junior dev, that’s what I’ll be. Now, stay still. Applying patch: `v1.2.0-Alpha-Echo`. The process will feel like your brain is being defragmented."*

## 【The Awakening: Data Mesh Vision】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_33/Image_02.jpg)

(As Toby wakes up in the real world training room, the world has changed. He no longer sees the walls or the machines. Instead, he sees glowing lines of wireframes and data-packets flowing through the air. He can see the 'Source' of the Logic Arrows before they are even fired.)

**Toby** (whispering): *"I can see them... I can see the `if-statements` in the air... I can see the `else` that's about to happen... This is... cheating!"*

**Wu Qiang** (smiling for the first time): *"It's not cheating, kid. It's 'Optimization'. Now, try to catch the next arrow with your bare hands. Find its 'Index' and delete it!"*
