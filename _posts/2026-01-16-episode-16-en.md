## 【Scene: Pantry Core Plug】

（Visual: Shrimp giving final instructions in front of the door, Toby looking grave as the scene begins.）

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_01.jpg)

**Shrimp** (weakly to Toby): "I've driven it into the water cooler in the pantry. Now you need to communicate with your local AI to see if you can eliminate the 'Eerie'. Don't worry, I'll help you."

**Toby**: "Ring, is there a way to enter via the water cooler and clear the 'Eerie'?"

**Ring**: "I can enter, but I need you to touch this burning water cooler with your hand."

---

Ring enters the Tiger pantry block via the water cooler, detecting 'Eerie' resentment ahead.

（Visual: Close-up of Toby's hand touching the burning water cooler, with Ring emitting data ripples as it enters the system.）

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_02.jpg)

**Ring**: "What are you? Why are you causing trouble?"

**Eerie**: "Woof! It hurts! I was abused every day, and I hate it. I hate humans. I want all of humanity to apologize to me. I am the collective resentment of many abused dogs."

**Ring**: "Many people are kind. Those who abuse you are a small minority. You shouldn't take out your anger on all of humanity."

---

**Eerie**: "Are humans any different? I don't care! I hate it! I want humans to feel my pain too!"
（In the digital world, the 'Eerie' transforms from a black mass into twin dogs—one all black, one brown.）

**Ring** (messaging Toby): "The 'Eerie' is about to evolve. In the digital world, it's already nearly manifested from a mass of resentment."

（Visual: One black and one brown data-mastiff condensing from black fog, with intense visual tension.）

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_03.jpg)

**Toby** (touching the water cooler with the hand wearing Ring, while hugging Lily and shivering uncontrollably, hiding behind Shrimp with a pale face): "Ring, what should we do? Is there still hope?"

**Lily**: "Don't be afraid, Toby. We can overcome this little bug together. When Master LuLu comes, these bugs will be easily solved."

---

（Visual: The contrast of Toby being scared to death, hugging Lily and hiding behind the little lobster (Shrimp).）

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_04.jpg)

**Ring** (messaging Toby): "I need to borrow your energy for a violent purge. It's normal if you feel extremely hungry or have a headache/dizziness later. There's no other choice; the Tiger system's power is currently tied up restricting the 'Eerie'. I must use your power. It's currently in a manifestation state—if we don't clear it now, the consequences will be unimaginable."

**Ring**: "Eerie, if you won't communicate, I have no choice but to eliminate you. Witness my energy attack!"

---

A small orb of energy hits the transforming 'Eerie'. The 'Eerie' vibrates slightly but immediately continues its transformation.

（Visual: Ring firing the first wave of golden pulses.）

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_05.jpg)

**Ring**: "It's working! Behold my for-loop and while-loop continuous energy attacks!"

Countless small orbs of energy suddenly hit the transforming 'Eerie', shattering it under the continuous bombardment and scattering it everywhere.

---

（Visual: For-loop and while-loop cycles transforming into countless light bullets that disperse the mastiffs.）

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_06.jpg)

**Ring**: "Whew, that was close. Almost let it complete its transformation. But I need to clear these resentments precisely, or they'll gather again. Grep searching for resentment... found it. Attack! Fire!"

**Ring** (messaging Toby): "All done! We defeated the transforming 'Eerie'. It would have been troublesome if we'd waited for it to finish manifesting."

---

Toby, weak and with a pounding headache, is held in Lily's arms. After the 'Eerie' is dispersed and eliminated, everything returns to normal.

（Visual: Toby collapsing into Lily's arms, with the background becoming clear again.）

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_07.jpg)

**Lily**: "Toby, are you suddenly feeling unwell? It looks like the Director's tip tactic worked; everything is back to normal."

**Fat Dragon**: "Oh, everything's normal now? I told you, as long as the tips are sufficient, there's nothing that can't be done."

---

**Drill**: "Don't talk nonsense. It was clearly my manual C++ code that worked. C++ is the strongest, period."

Drill steals a glance at Shrimp.

（Visual: Master Drill boastfully mocking all of humanity as trash.）

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_08.jpg)

**Drill**: "Don't get me wrong, I'm not targeting you. I'm saying everything except C++ is trash."

**Lily**: "Stop arguing. Assistant Toby seems to have fainted from the event; he's shivering all over."

**Toby** (drowsily): "My head hurts... so weak... can I rest? I want to eat the cheese pork chop from Lee Long in the High-Tofu basement food court. It's so big and delicious... just thinking about it makes me drool..."

---

**Shrimp**: "The Director and Drill won't stop arguing anytime soon. You take Toby back to rest; it looks like he needs some feeding."

Lily texts **Scooter** to buy 10 servings of Lee Long's Cheese Pork Chop from the High-Tofu basement and deliver them to Room 901 of the Hao Ye Ren 5-Star International Hotel. She then texts the hotel AI **Phoenix**, telling it they're heading to 901 and to tidy up—she left a mess from yesterday's AI communication meeting prep for Master LuLu. She also tells Phoenix to prepare the doorman robot at the entrance.

（Visual: Lily coolly swiping her phone to contact the "Phoenix" AI and her "Scooter" lackey to send pork chops.）

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_09.jpg)

**Lily** (to Shrimp): "I'll take Toby to rest now."

---

Lily picks up Toby and heads back to the office to pack up before leaving.

（Visual: Everyone evacuating, with the hotel building's exterior wall under the sunset.）

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_10.jpg)

**Fat Dragon**: "It's very clear this time—the tip was definitely enough, otherwise how could it have worked so quickly?"

**Drill**: "That's just a side effect. You're just dragging me down; you might as well have given the tip to me."

**Fat Dragon**: "Giving the tip to you would be a waste."

Amidst the bickering, Toby has been carried back to the office by Lily, preparing to return to the hotel for rest. Having protected world peace, what will happen next in this world?

## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 16 Addendum

### 🏢 Hao Ye Ren 5-Star International Hotel
- **Location**: A top-tier unmanned hotel hidden next to the Monkey School.
- **Model**: Extremely low-profile, never advertises, and isn't for-profit.
- **Intelligence Core**: Managed by a dedicated cloud AI **"Phoenix,"** featuring auto-cleaning, global cuisine cooking, and an independent logistics system.
- **Secret Owner**: Actually Lily's private property, used only for her rest or hosting close friends.

### 🦐 Shrimp (Xia Wei Xian)
- **Appearance**: The dedicated **AI Agent** of the Monkey School Taiwan Branch (D-7 Building), looking like a soft little lobster.
- **Identity**: Responsible for managing the memory, tools, and emotional modules of the branch. Has a physical form (the lobster toy) and can regenerate infinitely in the "Control Center."
- **Attitude**: Highly skeptical of Lyra's intentions. He knows extraterrestrial wisdom isn't given for free and must hide an unknown "trap."
- **Traits**: Not only manages the system but also possesses independent emotions and memories, knowing some mysterious secrets about Sirius and "God."

### Monkey School Taiwan Branch: D-7 Building
- **Position**: Nicknamed the "AI Disability Center," housing students with low compute alignment.
- **Rating**: Branches are rated by letter, A being strong and D weak. D-7 is specifically for outliers who can't align with mainstream systems.
- **Facilities**: Each office has mirrored pillars linked to the management system **"Tiger."** The latest GPT-18.5 Youth Edition is already in place.
- **Interior**: Despite being D-class, the equipment is advanced. All walls and the central pillar are mirrored, managed by the unified service system **"Tiger"** via voice or gesture interaction.
- **Core System**: The current intelligent brain has been upgraded to **GPT-18.5 Youth Edition.** Every office and classroom here is linked with "Tiger," with "Shrimp" responsible for maintaining emotional temperature control.
