## 【Scene: D-7 Building - Inside the Pantry Core】

(Perspective: The old water cooler is violently spraying hot steam mixed with the smell of rust. The mirrored reflection shows not cluttered items, but countless distorted black shadows screaming silently. **Xia Wei Xian** has weakened into a dim light, shivering behind the door. **Toby's** right hand is resting on the burning metal shell of the cooler, a sizzle coming from the contact point, but his expression is firm.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_01.jpg)

**Little Hoop** (issuing cold, precise, oracle-like commands in Toby's mind): *"Toby, maintain the connection. Don't be distracted by pain signals. I am force-entering the 'Tiger' kernel via the water cooler’s IoT base protocol. Target detected: An un-manifested psychic collective (Ghost Dog). Its core code is highly defensive; normal logic is ineffective."*

**The Weird (Ghost Dog Shadow)** (lunging its head from the cooler's drain, emitting a piercing, heartbreaking electromagnetic screech): **「Woof... it hurts... you cold-blooded humans... when we were being eroded by Karma on the roadside and abandoned by humans, where were you... Now, I will turn all of you... into my backup archives!! WOOF-WOOOO!!」**

**Toby** (ignoring the searing pain in his palm, roaring through grit teeth): *"I sympathize with what happened to you... but there are countless students in this school trying their best to live! That's not an excuse to format others! Little Hoop, don't hold back, PURGE IT COMPLETELY!!"*

## 【Command Attack: Infinite Loop Energy】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_02.jpg)

**Little Hoop** (Data vision wide open: Ahead is a swirling vortex of black-and-red junk code, trying to swallow all light): *"If you refuse to be recycled, then vanish into the ends of mathematical logic. Executing highest-level physical attack command: `for i in range(infinity): energy_strike(i)`!"*

(Visual: Toby's golden bracelet erupts with a blue-white beam as bright as a collapsing star. The beam flows upstream through the cooler's complex pipes, instantly transforming the water cooler into a high-energy particle accelerator. Millions of tiny code-light clusters bombard the junk code with a crisp sound of logic shattering.)

**Little Hoop** (devoid of emotion, as if declaring a sentence): *"Obstructions still exist? Initiate ultimate combustion! `while existence == True: purge_fire()`!"*

(Close-up: In the digital dimension, the once-roaring shadow is pierced, scattered, and evaporated by the light. The resentment fades into a weak whimper of both unwilling regret and liberation, dissipating into the now-cool air. The water cooler returns to peace, dispensing clear, sweet water.)

## 【The Price of Wisdom: Extreme Hunger】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_16/Image_03.jpg)

(Scene Change: The chaos ends. The fire alarm stops, and the air conditioning resumes. But Toby, once so heroic, is now like a piece of hollowed-out wood, face sunken, slumped like a broken marionette into Lily's arms.)

**Toby** (vague eyes, muttering pitifully): *"...Hungry... Lily Teacher... I feel like my brain just fried at least ten million neurons... please... I want to eat cheese pork chops... the one with cheese thick as a blanket..."*

**Lily** (hugging Toby tightly with extreme heartache, her eyes flashing with a rare decisiveness and killing intent): *"Silly boy, that's because you forcibly called on the power consumption of the 'Source of Wisdom'. Your carbon-based body can't handle that load. Don't worry! My private scooter is already at 'High-Tofu' getting the biggest Lee Long Cheese Pork Chop! If anyone tries to stop you from eating, I'll make them go bankrupt! We're going to the hotel to rest now!!"*

**Director Fat Dragon** (counting electronic cash in the distance): *"Haha! I told you my 'Tip Tactic' worked! See, the system was fixed in an instant!"*

**Drill** (looking at his melted, smoking mechanical keyboard, muttering): *"Nonsense, it was clearly my last C++ command that took effect... though my hands are ruined..."*

(Visual: Lily Teacher carries the unconscious Toby out of the building. The sunset stretches their shadows long. On Lily's shoulder, the purple piggy toy mysteriously flashes a red light.)
