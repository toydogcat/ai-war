## 【Scene: High-Tofu 3C Store - Second Plotting of the Potential Stocks】

(Scene Perspective: The private booth is filled with a soft and sweet pink-purple glow. **Peige** is slowly chewing on a piece of chia seed jelly, her slender fingers twirling a new terminal that looks exactly like a toy water pistol, but with a sci-fi silhouette and painted in vivid cherry blossom pink. She is an expert in this field, knowing how to wrap lethal permissions in "cuteness.")

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_01.jpg)

**Peige** (with her signature dazed smile, yet eyes unfathomable): *"Since that penniless Toby has already bound himself to the most violent 'Little Hoop,' then you should take this private stock developed specifically for a high-level Debugger like you—'Chia-Seed.' Sakura, this is a 'Pink Weak-Binding' package I specifically applied for from the Council; it won't make you as hungry as those dignity-less STEM monkeys."*

**Sakura** (taking the heavy pink handgun that feels like warm jade, eyes practically turning into hearts): *"Whoa... this feel, this color... it's simply a dream godly-gear for gamers! Boss Peippa, your conscience has finally appeared. This thing... can it really help me physically clear out all those constipation-like code bugs?"*

**Chia-Seed** (the micro-display on the side of the gun suddenly lights up, emitting a soft but electronically warbling female voice): **"Hello, Subject! I am your exclusive On-Device AI, codename 'Ya.' While I'm not as rude as that golden hoop, I am a professional when it comes to clearing 'data trash' and adjusting 'causality.' A free quota has been injected; do you want to have a first experience of 'Soul Pre-binding'?"**

**Sakura** (face suddenly freezing, clutching her stomach): *"Wait... wait a second... even though your voice is cute, there's a much more urgent 'Bottom Layer Data' that needs clearing right now... it might be that half red bean bun was expired... where's the bathroom! I can't hold it anymore!"*

---

## 【Unexpected Help: The Warmth of Spirit Money】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_02.jpg)

(Scene Transition: The public bathroom in the darkest corner of the alley behind Holy Mother Garden Food Street. The single lightbulb is flickering on and off with a buzzing sound, and the air is filled with an unsettling cold silence.)

**Sakura** (inside the dim stall, frantically digging through her pockets, voice desperate): *"I'm dead, I'm dead... I actually forgot to bring toilet paper at such a critical moment... even the roll on the wall only has the cardboard tube left. Help! Am I supposed to use this 'High-Dimensional Weapon' I just got to wipe my butt? That's way too much desecration of the 'Source of Wisdom'!"*

**Chia-Seed** (screaming frantically on the hook): **"Forbidden! Absolutely Forbidden!! Subject, I am also a data lifeform with basic dignity! What flows within me is the pure code of Lyra, not something for you to use to wipe... that place! If you dare try, I will immediately initiate the self-destruct sequence and permanently mark your five senses as 'extremely foul'!!"**

(Just as Sakura falls into despair, a pair of hands—pale as lime, with long, twisted nails—silently slides in from the gap under the stall divider. In the palms, they gently pinch a thick stack of traditional yellow paper.)

**Mysterious Voice** (sounding as if from the bottom of a thousand-year-old well, with heavy interference): **"Give... you... pa... per... Emer... gen... cy... help... Enough...?"**

**Sakura** (thinking nothing of it, grabs the stack of paper): *"Whoa!! Such great kindness! You are simply the last angel in this trash city! Thanks, you low-profile good-hearted person; you saved this Debugger's chastity!"*

## 【Condensed Terror: A Good Person Leaves No Name】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_03.jpg)

(Scene: Thirty seconds later, Sakura steps out of the stall refreshed. As she walks, she looks down at the remaining paper in her hand, suddenly freezing into a stone statue in front of the sink.)

**Sakura** (pupils contracting violently, cold sweat pouring down): *"This paper quality... why is it as stiff as craft paper? And what's printed on it is... 'General Use in Hell'? And there are circle symbols drawn in cinnabar? Hey... that kind lady just now? You... you work in a paper-offering shop, right? You're joking with me, right?"*

(Scene Close-up: Sakura tremblingly pushes open the door of the adjacent stall. Inside... it's empty. The toilet seat is cold, and the floor is so dry there isn't so much as a single footprint. Most importantly, this bathroom is completely sealed. Only the rusted faucet at the sink is going tap, tap, dripping a dark red, viscous liquid.)

**Chia-Seed** (silent for a full three seconds, voice trembling to the point of distortion): **"Subject... the coldness of those hands just now was absolute zero... Data analysis shows the source coordinates come from a deregistered bottom space layer. I think we just... accidentally completed a data exchange protocol with this city's 'Bug Matrix' in the bathroom."**
