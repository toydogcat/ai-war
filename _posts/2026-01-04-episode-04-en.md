## 【Scene: 'Dragon’s Spirit' Deep-Fried Pork Chop Stall】

(Scene: The chaotic night market stands. Toby is backed against a wall of grease-stained barrels. Little Tooth’s pink pulse gun is aimed directly at his forehead. Just as the air begins to crackle with energy, a deep, rhythmic sound of a heavy cleaver striking a wooden board echoes through the alley.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_01.jpg)

**Master Li Long** (voice low and resonant like a temple bell): *"Young lady, this alley is for people who appreciate the 'Dao of the Deep Fry'. Data deletion and soul recovery are prohibited within five meters of my deep fryer. It affects the crispiness of the crust."*

(A massive man with a thick beard and eyes that seem to hold ancient wisdom steps forward. He is **Li Long**, a legendary figure hidden within the night market's labyrinth.)

**Little Tooth** (squinting at her HUD, which is flickering with 'ERROR: UNSCANNABLE ENTITY' messages): *"Impossible... My sensors show you as a mere 'Monkey Class' vendor. But your bio-signature is generating a massive static field. Stand aside, or you will be identified as an obstruction to Council justice!"*

---

## 【The Clash: Cleaver vs. Pink Code】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_02.jpg)

**Master Li Long** (chuckles, slowly wiping his blade): *"Council justice? You kids today rely too much on your glowing glasses. Real power isn't in your code; it's in the 'Silence between the bytes'."*

(Little Tooth fires her pink 'Small Tooth' gun. Master Li Long doesn't move, but the energy pulse seemingly vanishes into thin air as it approaches him, turning into harmless rose-colored petals.)

**Little Tooth** (shocked, hands trembling slightly): *"What... What did you do? My pulse was at 100% capacity! How did you neutralize the deletion protocol with a... a kitchen utensil?"*

**Toby** (cheering from the floor): *"Go, Chef! I knew your pork chops were magical! Little Hoop, see that? That's what I call a real defensive layer!"*

---

## 【The Gift of Safety: A Temporary Truce】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_03.jpg)

**Master Li Long** (pointing his cleaver at Toby): *"Kid, get up. Take this plate of 'Cheese-Core Special'. It has enough 'Static Buffer' to keep your little gold bracelet from overheating for at least 30 minutes."*

**Master Li Long** (to Little Tooth): *"Go home, Little Tooth. Tell 'Strelitzia' that the night market isn't a playground for her toys. If she wants the seed, let her come and try a pork chop herself."*

(Little Tooth, realizing her current technology is completely countered by this man's presence, lowers her weapon. A holographic notification appears on her eye-mask: 'RETREAT SUGGESTED. THREAT LEVEL: UNKNOWN EXTREME'.)
