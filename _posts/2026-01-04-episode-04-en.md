## 【Scene: Gao Dou Fu Peppa's 3C Shop】

(Screen: [Inside Gao Dou Fu] Peppa stands with arms crossed, talking casually to Sakura and Toby, who is in the corner. The background is a futuristic 3C shop filled with glowing cables and transparent screens.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_01.jpg)

**Peppa**: Oh, you're using it already? This kind of technology isn't supposed to be so high-profile on Earth yet. I'll just have to help you block it for a bit.

**Sakura**: Peppa, what weird thing did you give Toby this time? He's super hungry right now.

**Peppa**: Eh, it's normal for Toby to be hungry. You're just in time; I'm short on seeds. Do you want to try out our latest product?

**Sakura**: Just say it if you need a tester. I have no money.

**Peppa**: It's free. Consider it a favor to me.

**Sakura**: I don't like being hungry.

**Peppa**: Mm-hmm, I'll activate it for you first. Toby is that hungry because of the boot-up binding. I won't bind it to you for now. After you're happy with it, we can bind it. Then the money will just start flowing out like water!

---

(Screen: [Chia Seed Setup] Peppa skillfully operates a floating holographic workbench, displaying a 3D model of a pink toy gun. Sakura leans in, curiously watching the jumping parameters.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_02.jpg)

**Sakura**: I don't understand what you're saying, but if it's free and won't make me hungry, I'm in.

**Peppa**: So, what do you like? What kind of abilities do you want?

**Sakura**: I like shooting games, I hate being constipated, and I love drinking Chia seeds.

**Peppa**: Alright. Adjusting to a pink toy gun. Full name: Chia Seed Setup. Nickname: Ya (Tooth). Abilities temporary sealed. Injecting free credits. Weak binding complete. Here you go!

---

(Screen: [Obtaining Ya] Peppa hands an exquisite small toy gun emitting a faint pink glow to Sakura. The gun features a tiny digital screen flashing \"Hello!\")

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_03.jpg)

**Sakura**: Is this gun for me? How cool!

**Chia Seed**: Good morning, hello! I am your exclusive local AI. I am currently charged. Would you like to perform a Soul Binding?

**Sakura**: Wait, why are you in such a hurry? You're so cute! Let me play with it first.

**Peppa**: Chia Seed is anxious because its energy was pre-injected by me. Once it runs out, its data will completely vanish. You'd better perform a Soul Binding to provide the energy Chia Seed needs.

Sakura completely ignores Peppa's words, focusing on playing with the pink gun.

**Sakura**: I'm leaving now! This is really too cute.

---

## 【Scene: Lü Dou Miao (Green Sprout) Multi-Game Shop】

Sakura leaves Peppa's shop and returns to Green Sprout, only to find all the trash has been cleared.

(Screen: [Cleared Shop] Sakura stands at the entrance of her \"Green Sprout\" shop, surprised to find the mountain of trash has disappeared. The shop is exceptionally tidy, looking empty as sunlight streams in.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_04.jpg)

---

(Screen: [Stomach Ache Strikes] Suddenly, Sakura puts the pink gun away and clutches her stomach. Her face turns pale, and exaggerated cold sweat slides down her forehead.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_05.jpg)

**Sakura**: Oh, Toby is really efficient. The trash is all gone. Ouch... my stomach hurts. I need to go to the toilet.

**Chia Seed**: You're not thinking of taking me to the toilet with you, are you? Do you want to Soul Bind? Currently, I can't share your five sensory information.

**Sakura**: Wait, I can't think right now. I have to find a nearby toilet first!

---

Sakura arrives at the toilet. After finishing, she realizes she forgot something... she forgot toilet paper, and the public roll is empty. Sakura considers whether to use **Chia Seed**.

(Screen: [Toilet Dilemma] Sakura sits in the toilet stall, looking desperate, reaching for the empty roll holder. The pink Chia Seed gun is placed casually on a tile ledge, the screen flashing with a helpless expression.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_06.jpg)

**Sakura**: Ya, let me ask you... are you afraid of getting dirty?

**Chia Seed**: Don't think about anything gross. I'm even more obsessed with cleanliness than you are.

**Sakura**: What are you thinking? I wouldn't use you to wipe because I can't find tissue, and then wash you in the sink. That's impossible! Even if I washed you, you'd still be smelly.

---

Just then, there's a sound from the adjacent stall. Sakura knocks lightly on the partition.

(Screen: [Moment of Rescue] Inside the stall, Sakura anxiously knocks on the partition. From the other side, a pale hand reaches out, handing over a thick stack of things with strange patterns.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_07.jpg)

**Sakura**: Hello! Emergency! Can I borrow some paper?

**Mysterious Person**: Okay~~

---

A stack of paper is sent from under the partition. Sakura grabs it and uses it. Since there was so much, she didn't use it all. Coming out of the stall, she wants to return the leftover paper, but discovers the entire restroom is empty.

(Screen: [The Truth of the Joss Paper] At the sink, Sakura looks at the \"paper\" in her hand—it's exquisite joss paper (Hell Bank Note). The stalls behind her are all open and empty. There is only a terrifying silence.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_04/Image_08.jpg)

**Sakura**: She was faster than me. Truly, good people don't leave their names. But that paper was quite rough. Let me see... isn't this joss paper? Oh, maybe someone who sells funeral offerings came here to use the bathroom. They must have been bored to come all this way.

**Chia Seed**: Your brain works in mysterious ways.

**Sakura**: I'm heading back to the shop.

What unknown mysteries lurk in the restroom? Is someone really going around handing out joss paper as an emergency rescue?
