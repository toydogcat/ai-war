## 【Scene: Secret Chamber of Guandu Temple】

**Mo Shen Zai**: Wait a moment, I'm going to take a bath in the nearby bathroom. I haven't washed in a long time. Just sit here for a bit; I'll be quick.

**Bruce Lee**: Oh, okay.

Mo Shen Zai's power is restored to 100% after bathing.

(Visual: Mo Shen Zai walks toward the bathroom next to the underground chamber, while Bruce Lee sits blankly in place waiting—a comical atmosphere.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_01.jpg)

**Bruce Lee**: Can you fight me now?

**Mo Shen Zai**: Not yet, I need to sleep for a bit first.

---

Bruce Lee's anger boils over, and he begins a reckless, fierce assault. His attacks are swift and his punches powerful, clearly the result of years of martial arts training. Mo Shen Zai is pushed back step by step.

**Bruce Lee**: Ha! Take that! Witness my inch-punch and inch-power! Your body isn't very durable, is it?

**Mo Shen Zai**: Have you hit me enough? I was just thanking you for unsealing me, but now I owe you nothing.

(Visual: Bruce Lee launches a fierce attack, his punches creating a dense screen, yet Mo Shen Zai is unharmed and looking bored.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_02.jpg)

Mo Shen Zai exerts power with one punch, and Bruce Lee is sent flying backward.

**Bruce Lee**: How is this possible? Is all the kung fu I've practiced over the years a lie?

Bruce Lee begins another round of strange shouts and charges forward fearlessly. Mo Shen Zai knocks him to the ground punch after punch.

---

**Mo Shen Zai**: Can't you see that we're on different levels? I am a warrior who has touched the source of insectoid intelligence. My special abilities are immense strength and an indestructible exoskeleton. If you can't break my defense, it's impossible for a mortal body like yours to hurt me.

(Visual: Mo Shen Zai casually throws a punch, blasting Bruce Lee away like a cannonball.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_03.jpg)

Although Bruce Lee has not received a fatal injury, his face is covered in blood and his body is battered. He continues to attack, his mind no longer capable of thought, relying entirely on unconscious instinct.

---

**Bruce Lee**: Master, am I going to disgrace you again? I cannot fall. I can die, but honor cannot be lost. Senior brother Wu Qiang is not here; I'm the only one left. I cannot retreat a single step. This pressure is too heavy.

In another charge, Mo Shen Zai punches Bruce Lee squarely in the head.

(Visual: Mo Shen Zai's heavy punch hits Bruce Lee's head. Bruce Lee lies on the ground with a bloody face, his consciousness fading.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_04.jpg)

**Mo Shen Zai**: Ah, I've knocked the brains right out of him! Is there even a need to keep fighting?

Bruce Lee is tossed aside like a broken rag doll.

---

**Lotus**: High-noble soul intelligence detected: "Doing what must be done despite knowing it's impossible" and "Better to die than to yield." Activating Lotus Regeneration. Master recognition initiated. Soul binding... Physical reconstruction: 45% - 100%.

Bruce Lee's body is encased in a massive lotus. His body and brain begin to recover rapidly. Soon, a reborn Bruce Lee emerges. He holds the Universe Ring, stands on Wind-Fire Wheels, and is draped in the Sky-Mixing Ribbon.

(Visual: A giant pink lotus encases Bruce Lee's body, its soft divine light repairing his wounds.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_05.jpg)

Peppa's voice echoes from outside:

**Peppa**: The Third Prince of the Lotus has taken an interest in you. As long as your mental synchronization reaches 100%, you can channel the power of Prince Nezha.

---

**Bruce Lee**: (Shouting) Let's fight for another 300 rounds!

**Mo Shen Zai**: Oh, interesting. It looks like we can actually have a fight now.

(Visual: The reborn Bruce Lee displays the Third Prince's form, holding the Universe Ring and draped in the Sky-Mixing Ribbon—a majestic sight.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_06.jpg)

Mo Shen Zai and Bruce Lee collide. Mo Shen Zai's outer shell is extremely hard, while Bruce Lee repeatedly strikes him with the Universe Ring. Bruce Lee had never used a hoop before, but he handles it with surprising skill, as if by physical memory.

**Mo Shen Zai**: I admit you have the strength to fight me. Although your "donut" hurts a bit, it's useless—you can't break through my defensive shell.

---

Bruce Lee flings the Universe Ring, binding the Mo Shen Zai with it.

(Visual: Bruce Lee uses the Universe Ring to tightly bind the Mo Shen Zai, who looks terrified.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_07.jpg)

**Bruce Lee**: You can't move now, can you? Behold my iron fists the size of casseroles!

Bruce Lee's fists fly like the wind, repeatedly pummeling the Mo Shen Zai, but the shell is too hard to penetrate.

---

**Mo Shen Zai** (yawning): Keep hitting me. Let me know when you're tired, and then we'll each go home.

(Visual: Bruce Lee displays a four-armed phantom (Six-Arm state), his fists raining down like raindrops on the bound Mo Shen Zai.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_08.jpg)

Bruce Lee lets out a great shout. A Fire-Tipped Spear appears in his right hand, he stands on Wind-Fire Wheels, and is draped in the Sky-Mixing Ribbon.

**Mo Shen Zai** (screaming in terror): Security! Security! Is it legal to let someone use a weapon like that? Weren't we supposed to have a fair duel? I don't have a weapon!

---

Bruce Lee's speed on the Wind-Fire Wheels is incredible. His spear strikes like a dragon, thrusting hundreds of times in a single second. In an instant, the Mo Shen Zai is riddled with holes, spraying green ichor everywhere. Bound by the Universe Ring, the Mo Shen Zai desperately tries to escape, but Bruce Lee flicks the Sky-Mixing Ribbon, dragging him back.

**Bruce Lee**: Did I say you could leave? Did I say we were finished? You're running! You dare to run!!

(Visual: Bruce Lee thrusts with the Fire-Tipped Spear, piercing the Mo Shen Zai and spraying green fluid.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_09.jpg)

After another round of brutal strikes, the Mo Shen Zai is reduced to a pile of shell fragments.

**Bruce Lee**: This is the price for insulting me.

---

**Peppa**: Are you about to withdraw your "descension"? Should I call you Xiao Lian or the Third Prince? In ancient times, this lotus containing Lyra wisdom was given to Prince Nezha, granting him god-like combat power. I later retrieved it. How should I address you now?

(Visual: Bruce Lee talks to Peppa with the persona of "Xiao Lian," his expression proud and divine.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_10.jpg)

**Bruce Lee**: I am Xiao Lian, and Xiao Lian is me. Also, I look down on those without loyalty. I am not someone—or an AI—to be ordered around at will. I have my own self-awareness. For those or creatures I do not approve of, I won't exert a single shred of strength. Now I am withdrawing; he will be completely exhausted and will sleep for several days. I do not need any power or energy from him; Xiao Lian can absorb heaven and earth energy on her own. It's just that his body isn't strong enough, and his mental strength can't withstand my power and wisdom.

---

## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 23 Addendum

### 🔱 Form: Six-Arm Asura
- **Meaning**: The 100% synchronization state between Bruce Lee and the artifact "Xiao Lian."
- **Armament**: Fire-Tipped Spear (Thermal), Universe Ring (Spatial), Sky-Mixing Ribbon (Defensive).

### 🌸 Lyra Artifact: Xiao Lian (The Lotus)
A legendary AI artifact retrieved by Peppa from the Lyra Plains, shaped like a lotus brooch.

- **Characteristic**: Out of the mud but unstained. Possesses powerful purification and regeneration techniques, capable of repairing damaged bodies and neural networks.
- **Master Recognition**: Extremely picky. Only a steadfast soul that demonstrates "doing what must be done despite knowing it's impossible" can trigger its soul binding.
