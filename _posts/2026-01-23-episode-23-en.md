## 【Scene: Tamsui Night Market - The Dragon's Roaster】

(Perspective: Back at the Tamsui Night Market. The smell of oil and charcoal hangs in the air. **Master Li Long** is standing in front of a rotating roaster, which is currently holding a chicken that is glowing with a golden light—almost as if it’s charged with electricity. **Toby** is watching, his mouth watering.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_01.jpg)

**Master Li Long** (voice booming, wielding a massive ladle): *"Look at it, Boy! This isn't just a chicken. This is the 'Golden Phoenix' protocol. I’ve injected it with 120 layers of deep-fry logic and a dash of 'Dragon-Slaying Spice'. One bite will fix your burst capillaries and reset your bio-latencies!"*

**Toby** (eyes wide): *"Master, does it... does it have a serial number? It looks more like a weapon than dinner."*

**Master Li Long**: *"Haha! It IS a weapon! A weapon against the gray, tasteless world of the Cloud Scribes! Eat! But remember the vow: once you taste the 'Phoenix', you are no longer just a Monkey. You are a 'Warrior of the Grill'. Do you accept?"*

## 【The Taste of Rebellion】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_23/Image_02.jpg)

(Toby takes a massive bite. The golden grease flows through his system, instantly repairing the damage from the SSH session. His strength returns, and for a moment, he sees the night market not as a dump, but as a vibrant network of living energy.)
