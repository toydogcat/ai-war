## 【Scene: Tamsui Night Market - The Blue Inferno】

(Perspective: The night market is in chaos. Thick black smoke billows from the 'High-Tofu' stall. But this isn't normal fire—the flames are flickering with a static-electric blue light, consuming not just wood but also the local Wi-Fi signals. **Master Li Long** is fighting back with his 'Dragon-Slaying Ladle', but the fire is spreading too fast.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_01.jpg)

**Master Li Long** (shielding his eyes from the blue heat): *"This isn't a kitchen accident! It's a 'Data-Burn'! Someone has overclocked the local grid to incinerate my secret recipes!! TOBY!! Don't let the 'Phoenix Cutlets' catch fire!! That's your only power source!!"*

**Toby** (running through the smoke, his Golden Bracelet vibrating with alarm): *"I'm trying, Master! But the fire is... it’s eating my code! Every time I get close, Little Hoop reports 'Connection Timed Out'!"*

**Little Hoop**: **「Warning: High-density data-overload detected. The fire is a physical manifestation of a 'DDoS Attack'. Suggestion: Use the 'Archivist Vision' to find the packet-source!」**

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_02.jpg)

(Suddenly, several mechanical figures wearing the X-Corp logo emerge from the shadows of the night market. They carry 'Void Strippers'—devices designed to pull energy from both machines and humans.)
