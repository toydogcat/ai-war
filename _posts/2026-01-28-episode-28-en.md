### 【Scene: Rich Rich Hotel — Luxury Living Room of Suite 901】

Inside the hotel, Toby is eating a cheese pork cutlet, holding a small pig while watching a news flash. A female host named Tracy is interviewing Don-wang, the leader of Eagle Nation.

(Visual: On the holographic TV screen, Tracy is interviewing Don-wang, who has iconic blonde hair and makes exaggerated gestures.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_01.jpg)

**Tracy**: Mr. Don-wang, this is terrifying. Those "monkeys" who refuse to evolve have launched a terrorist attack on Mark's research center near Phoenix City.

**Don-wang**: It's pure jealousy. They just love to attack smart researchers.

---

**Tracy**: It seems many researchers were injured, many of whom are from "Curry Country."

**Don-wang** (shedding tears): These people from Curry Country traveled thousands of miles to Eagle Nation just to develop the latest AI technology. They don't even take a salary at Mark's company; all they ask for is curry, singing, and dancing.

(Visual: A split screen showing Don-wang crying crocodile tears on one side, and happy researchers dancing while eating curry on the other.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_02.jpg)

**Tracy**: That's wonderful. Thank God, thank science.

---

**Don-wang** (banging the table and shouting): Mark and the others have done so much for Eagle Nation! I'm going to impose heavy taxes on those monkeys who refuse to evolve—a 100% IQ tax! No, that's not enough—1000%!

(Visual: Don-wang bangs his fist on the desk, with exaggerated slogans of "1000% IQ Tax" in the background.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_03.jpg)

**Tracy** (startled): But they just want to "lie flat"... is it really that serious?

**Don-wang**: It's extremely serious! There's 100% proof that the brains of monkeys who don't evolve will get sick. Eventually, our hospitals will be overrun by monkeys who just want to lie flat. Are you willing to use your income to support them?

**Tracy**: That's terrifying! How can we avoid this horrible disease?

**Don-wang**: That's exactly why I'm here today. I've known for a long time that their brains would get sick, so I ordered the Monkey School to develop a "miracle cure."

---

**Tracy**: Mr. Don-wang, you truly understand everything! Is there anyone in the world who knows more than you?

**Don-wang**: Exactly. This miracle cure is called "Smart AI Macarons"—the savior of humanity. We're launching a trial sale at $10,000 per macaron. I suggest eating them whenever you want, but they're so effective that we might have to limit purchases in a month.

(Visual: Don-wang displays a box of colorful "Smart AI Macarons" emitting a divine glow.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_04.jpg)

Don-wang stares intensely at Tracy, who looks a bit flustered.

**Don-wang**: I'm not just talking to you (turns to the camera). I'm talking to everyone watching—you all need to buy a year's supply of Smart AI Macarons.

---

Don-wang is also a powered individual. His ability is the "Don-wang Domain": anyone who makes eye contact with him is hit by a special logic wave, making them highly likely to believe whatever nonsense he says. "Monkeys" have zero resistance.

(Visual: Don-wang's eyes emit golden spiral energy waves. Tracy and the viewers at home fall into a trance.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_05.jpg)

Just then, the door to Suite 901 opens. Lily enters, wearing an oversized coat that is filthy and scorched, smelling like burnt wires.

**Toby**: Lily, did you hear? "Smart AI Macarons" seem really effective! Have you tried them? Are they really that amazing?

(Visual: Lily enters in her scorched, dirty coat, contrasting sharply with Toby eating pork cutlets on the sofa.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_06.jpg)

---

**Lily**: Oh, Toby, you're awake. I'm going to shower and change first; I'm covered in filth.

**Toby**: What happened?

(Visual: Lily points toward the door, tiredly explaining her ordeal in the warehouse to Toby.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_07.jpg)

**Lily**: It's all the Director's fault. He said we're leaving for "Cheese Kingdom" tomorrow for an exchange visit. Cheese Kingdom has been resisting the Monkey School's expansion, so the Director wants me to prepare. You and I are flying there tomorrow. To make sure Cheese Kingdom is sufficiently impressed, the Director sent me to the old warehouse to find some "Legendary Teaching Tools." But Drill never organizes the warehouse; it was a total mess, and I got caught in a mess myself.

---

Lily talks to Toby from the bathroom while showering.

**Toby**: What? A sudden trip to Cheese Kingdom? Isn't the Director going?

**Lily**: He's going too, but he got a call saying the Monkey School HQ has a new miracle product—some kind of macaron thing. He's flying to Eagle Nation first to get the latest product before heading to Cheese Kingdom.

**Toby**: But I've never been to Cheese Kingdom! What if I get lost? What if I can't eat the food?

(Visual: Toby looks at Xiao Huan, which projects a magnificent holographic map of the Cheese Kingdom's temples and sacred flames.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_08.jpg)

---

**Lily**: Don't worry. The Director said I'll be with you. Master LuLu also instructed me that my main task is just to look after you. If you're tired, go to sleep. Jet lag in Cheese Kingdom is no joke. Just show up; I'll handle the rest.

**Toby**: Hey Xiao Huan, is Cheese Kingdom fun? I'm going on another trip. What if I get lost?

(Visual: Toby clenches his fist with anticipation, his eyes shining with a mix of excitement and nervousness about going abroad.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_09.jpg)

---

**Xiao Huan**: Aren't you just supposed to be a mascot? Why are you whining? However, Cheese Kingdom has a very powerful heritage. You'll definitely experience many "Awakenings" there. But given your job, promoting the Monkey School will be nearly impossible. They are far too wise to be brainwashed—even Don-wang's ability wouldn't work on them.

**Toby**: Now that you've said that, I'm even more excited!

**Toby** (yelling toward the bathroom): Lily, I'm counting on you tomorrow! I can't wait!

(Visual: The bathroom is filled with a thick black mist, with Lily's silhouette appearing eerie and mysterious through the steam.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_28/Image_10.jpg)

The bathroom is filled with black mist, but Toby doesn't notice at all.
