## 【Scene: Afternoon Park - The Sand Trap】

(Perspective: A tranquil afternoon in a park on Taiwan Island. A man dressed in an exotic, sand-colored robe—**Aladdin**—is sitting on a bench, holding an ancient-looking bronze oil lamp. Opposite him is a beautiful woman with silver hair, looking for her lost Persian cat.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_41/Image_01.jpg)

**Aladdin** (voice calm with a dangerous undertone): *"Are you looking for this, Miss? (He rubs the lamp, and a puff of smoke reveals a small, glowing Persian cat floating in the air) Your cat has excellent 'Wisdom Potential'. It’s a shame to let such a rare data-creature wander around in a place filled with so many 'Monkeys'."*

**Silver-Haired Woman** (stepping back, her eyes narrowing): *"Who are you? My cat isn't 'data'. It’s a living being. Give it back, or I’ll have to call the park authorities."*

**Aladdin** (laughing): *"The authorities? My lamp is currently 'Overriding' the local reality. To them, we don't even exist. I am Aladdin, the collector of the desert. And I think your cat would look much better as a 'Genie' in my service."*

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_41/Image_02.jpg)
