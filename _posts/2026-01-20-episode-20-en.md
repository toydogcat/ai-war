## 【Scene: Apartment Hallway — Absolute Dark Realm】

Toby left Glasses' room and came outside to find a 7-foot giant, his body wrapped in bandages with traces of blood seeping out. Suddenly, dark shadows darted before Toby's eyes. Wu Qiang moved instantly, lightly touching one with his pinky, and Xiao Guo was shattered into black mist.

(Visual: Toby in a dark hallway filled with black slime, encountering Wu Qiang, a giant wrapped in bandages slowly seeping blood.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_20/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby**: Who are you? Are you some kind of monster? Can you communicate? Can you stop your supernatural powers?

**Wu Qiang**: Poor soul, you shouldn't be here. There are countless, unknown numbers of "anomalies" here. I've already killed I don't know how many, but they never end.

**Toby**: Oh, so you're human.

---

During the conversation, Xiao Ping kept launching charges in turns. Wu Qiang easily crushed Xiao Ping by just moving his ten fingers. To Xiao Ping, this was an absolute provocation. Its animalistic nature was triggered, and it began to attack wildly.

(Visual: In the narrow hallway, Wu Qiang easily crushes the pouncing shadow of the vicious dog using only his fingers, moving as fast as teleportation.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_20/Image_02.jpg)

**Toby**: In this short time, have you counted how many you've killed, and what their characteristics are?

**Wu Qiang**: What are you talking about? Is there a number in this world higher than my fingers? Aren't they countless? I don't know how to count.

**Toby**: You've killed 234 black ones and 329 brown ones. My Little Hoop told me there are only two of them, and they need to be killed almost simultaneously.

---

**Wu Qiang**: Is that so? Are you a math genius? But they move so fast, how can I kill them together? Even if I catch one, the other runs away. How can I kill them at the same time? Even if you're great at math, it doesn't help with the problem we're facing now.

**Toby**: Well, even though you're very strong, it seems you're also useless for the current situation.

(Visual: Toby huddles in the corner of the hallway, communicating urgently with the floating Little Hoop. Little Hoop's display is filled with a complex data matrix.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_20/Image_03.jpg)

**Little Hoop**: What should we do? There seems to be no way. I told you you're physically incompetent. Even though I've given you all the information, you're still useless.

**Toby**: Little Hoop, let me ask you, why is he so strong? Can I reach his strength in a short time?

---

**Little Hoop**: Impossible. He has attained a very high level of Buddhist affinity and has continuously practiced combat meditation. But let's get back to the point. This double-bodied anomaly itself isn't strong. It's not impossible for you to reach the strength to kill them in a short time. Theoretically, if you relinquish your control and let me take over your body entirely, it's possible. However, you must fully understand my programmed operational commands without a single hesitation. Only then can we barely meet the speed and power requirements to kill them.

(Visual: Toby decides to activate "Physical Programming." Blue coding symbols flash in his pupils, and violent digital interference appears around his body.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_20/Image_04.jpg)

**Toby**: No choice. At Glasses' speed of memorization, he's almost done with the Labor Standards Act. If he comes out, it'll be dangerous. Just use me.

**Toby** (shouting at Wu Qiang): I'm about to perform at a superhuman level and kill one of them. I don't know which one. You need to coordinate your attack with my timing.

**Wu Qiang**: Coordinate with your attack? Is there anything simpler than that? I've won a duel against a nine-headed dragon.

---

**Toby + Little Hoop**: ssh toby@local, Init, left leg move to xx yy, right leg move to zz yy, body up, right hand move to zz xx.

(Visual: Toby's body twists at an extremely unnatural high-frequency angle, striking Xiao Ping like blue lightning. The movement is stiff yet perfectly precise.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_20/Image_05.jpg)

**Wu Qiang** (eyes narrowing): I see which one you're attacking. Great Majestic Dragon, take this punch!

---

Both black flags shattered simultaneously. The flags dissolved into black threads in the air. Xiao Ming watched quietly from the dark corner. Wu Qiang and Xiao Ming's levels seemed to be worlds apart, as Wu Qiang didn't seem to notice Xiao Ming's presence.

(Visual: At the moment the black flags shatter, the two giant dogs dissipate in the air. In the distant shadows, Xiao Ming watches coldly.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_20/Image_06.jpg)

**Little Hoop**: Detected a Super-God level anomaly. It's currently unmanageable, but fortunately, the other party has no ill intent.

---

Glasses rushed out of the room.

**Glasses**: Assistant, I've finished memorizing the Labor Standards Act! Ah, Toby Assistant, what happened to you? Why is your whole body so weirdly twisted and bleeding? Toby, what's wrong!!

(Visual: Glasses rushes out of the door in panic, seeing Toby lying in a pool of blood with his limbs twisted unnaturally.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_20/Image_07.jpg)

Lily's voice came from the stairwell.

**Lily**: Glasses, did you get the piggy doll? The Director told me to bring it to you; he said it's the most important thing right now. Ah, what happened to Toby!!

---

Lily held the limp Toby, hugging him tightly in worry.

**Toby** (weakly): I'm fine, just exhausted from playing. So this is what it feels like to hand over your body... everything feels like it's not mine, I can't move at all, and I'm starving.

(Visual: Lily cries as she tightly holds the limp Toby. Moonlight streams through a broken window, illuminating the aftermath of this brutal battle.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_20/Image_08.jpg)

Toby fainted.

**Lily**: Glasses, take this piggy doll. I'm going to take Toby back to rest.

**Glasses**: Okay, Teacher Lily. I really wouldn't know what to do if you didn't show up. Toby Assistant just ate all the food in my fridge, I was at my wit's end.

**Lily**: It's okay, I'll handle it. You go back to your room.

Toby has saved the world once again. Will anomalous events continue to occur? Let's find out in the next episode.
