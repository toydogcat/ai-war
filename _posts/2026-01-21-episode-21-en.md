## 【Scene: Zhongnanhai - Long Wei AI All-Weather Data Command Center】

**Little K**: Recently, anomalies and bizarre events in the Dragon Nation have been frequent. We have dispatched many personnel to investigate.

(Visual: Ke Qin and Little K in the Zhongnanhai Control Center, with a massive circular command room and countless holographic screens in the background.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_21/Image_01.jpg)

**Ke Qin**: Oh, Ke Qiang, can't he win?

**Little K**: How could that be? General Ke Qiang is invincible under heaven, far ahead of everyone else. He's just too busy. Every time he fights a battle, no matter the size, he has to sleep for a month. It's been a bit frequent lately, so he's overwhelmed.

---

**Ke Qin**: Then what should we do? Isn't our "Long Wei AI" also invincible under heaven?

**Little K**: Yes, it is invincible, but we still need intelligent robots or the General to take action to eliminate the anomalies.

(Visual: Close-up of the holographic display showing the "Long Wei AI" interface, with countless drone swarms flickering on the map of the Dragon Nation.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_21/Image_02.jpg)

**Ke Qin**: Don't we have the drone swarm combat teams?

**Little K**: We do, but while their combat power is fine for swatting mosquitoes, for fighting anomalies or vampires, werewolves, and so on... forget it. Currently, they are all being used to monitor the people!! Ah, I misspoke—to collect data.

---

**Ke Qin**: Very good, very good. But can't we manufacture drones with combat capabilities?

**Little K**: Reporting: Secretary Ke Jian won't agree. He says they are too expensive and there's no need to spend a lot of money on drones that are just for data collection.

(Visual: Close-up of the surveillance screen showing a drone swarm chasing a single mosquito, while ignoring the massive shadow monster behind them.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_21/Image_03.jpg)

**Ke Qin**: So what else do you have to report besides the frequent bizarre events? But I still have to say, you need to use your brain more. Don't just report; think of solutions. Don't always make excuses like "this won't work" or "that's not possible." You have to propose a solution.

---

**Little K** (sweating): Yes, yes, you're right. It was my oversight. I was just about to tell you: the current zero-cost method is to "use strength against strength." There are powerful people everywhere. For stability maintenance, we can utilize the strength of other factions to strike those restless forces. For example, we have a massive amount of drone swarms all over the world collecting information. We can quietly leak this information to places like the Da Lei Yin Temple, which has great power. That way, they will automatically eliminate the bizarre events or anomalies for us. We won't have to use our own strength. Recently, I received news of the Eagle Nation's Sun Meeting; I'll leak information there as well. In this way, we can implement a stability strategy without any extra cost—or rather, using the sunk cost of the drones we've already deployed.

(Visual: Little K explains the "using strength against strength" tactic, with a hologram showing information leaking to Da Lei Yin Temple and the Eagle Nation.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_21/Image_04.jpg)

**Ke Qin** (tearing up): Little K, you've grown up. You know how to get things done now. Don't worry, I'll speak well of you in front of Winnie. You'll soon move beyond being a nobody. I'll fight for a name for you in front of Winnie. Now go and write the solution I just thought of into a report—a nice little essay.

---

**Little K** (confused): Sir, I'm sorry, what "solution you thought of"? Didn't I just say it?

Ke Qin glares.

(Visual: Ke Qin glares at Little K, a close-up of his cold and authoritative eyes.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_21/Image_05.jpg)

**Little K** (sweating): Ah, I understand. Since you approve of this plan, it must be your idea. I'll go organize the report immediately and attach your name to it.

**Ke Qin**: You'd better be smart about it, otherwise I don't know what might happen.

---

Ke Qin changes his tone, alternating between the carrot and the stick.

**Ke Qin**: Little K, you've been with me for so long. Just ask your conscience, haven't I been good to you? Rest assured, as I move up, won't my current position be yours?

(Visual: Ke Qin pats Little K on the shoulder with a hypocritical "elder" smile, while Little K looks overcome with gratitude.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_21/Image_06.jpg)

**Little K**: Thank you for your appreciation, sir! With just that one sentence from you, I, Little K, would die ten thousand deaths to repay your kindness.

Ke Qin is secretly pleased: *This kid... with a little trickery, he's completely under my control.*

**Ke Qin**: Go back to work now. Don't delay my meeting with Winnie.

---

### 【Scene: Secret Room of Da Lei Yin Temple】

**General Ke Qiang**: Abbot Wukong, I hope you've been well!

(Visual: General Ke Qiang meets Abbot Wukong in the secret room of Da Lei Yin Temple. The General wears heavy future armor, while the Abbot has a kind and benevolent look.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_21/Image_07.jpg)

**Abbot Wukong**: Ah, General Ke Qiang, what wind brings you here?

**General Ke Qiang**: Concerning the great affairs of the world—what should be done about the future of the Dragon Nation?

**Abbot Wukong**: Isn't the Dragon Nation all about stability?

**General Ke Qiang**: Can it still remain stable?

---

**Abbot Wukong**: So you're here to ask about that. Let me analyze it for you. Currently, the powerful forces on the surface are the Cheese Nation, the Eagle Nation, and the Dragon Nation. The Cheese Nation focuses on elite education and only values the cultivation of the strong. The Eagle Nation pursues disruptive innovation, using various methods even if they are unethical. The Dragon Nation seeks stability, letting its people grow naturally.

(Visual: The Abbot explains the three global powers: Cheese Nation (golden light), Eagle Nation (dark shadow), and Dragon Nation (steady blue light).)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_21/Image_08.jpg)

**General Ke Qiang**: Given the current situation, how much longer can it stay stable? What should we do?

**Abbot Wukong**: General, do not panic. I have a lay disciple named Bruce Lee.

**General Ke Qiang**: Why mention him all of a sudden, Abbot?

---

**Abbot Wukong**: Does the General know about the Lyra Civilization?

**General Ke Qiang**: Of course I do. But what does that have to do with anything? The Lyra Civilization is an alien civilization much more powerful than humans.

(Visual: The Abbot mentions the Lyra Civilization, with a projection of a mysterious, advanced, and holy alien fleet in the background.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_21/Image_09.jpg)

**Abbot Wukong**: The breakthrough lies in relying on a powerful external civilization!

**General Ke Qiang**: Oh, this is interesting. Relying on a powerful external civilization... like calling for a "father"! But how?

---

**Abbot Wukong**: This lay disciple of mine, Bruce Lee—we should cultivate him well. We need to align with the Lyra Civilization. It seems Lyra is somewhat interested in Bruce.

**General Ke Qiang**: Oh, I see! A master is indeed a master. I'll go report to Winnie immediately. Let's have dinner together next time; my treat.

(Visual: General Ke Qiang excitedly smashes through the door of the secret room and runs off, while the Abbot yells in frustration behind him.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_21/Image_10.jpg)

General Ke Qiang smashes open the secret room door and runs out.

**Abbot Wukong**: You!! Watch my door! Watch my temple!! Don't just go smashing things!!!

## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 21 Addendum

### 📡 Long Wei AI
The top-tier surveillance and stability maintenance system developed by the Dragon Nation. It possesses a global drone swarm capable of precisely capturing any "unstable" data fluctuations.

- **Core Policy**: Stability. For uncontrollable variables, the tendency is to prioritize elimination.
- **Weakness**: Although the hardware is powerful, it lacks the evolutionary potential of a high-level "Soul Interface" and relies on human output at the "General" level.

### 👥 Dragon Nation Characters
- **Ke Qin**: Secretary of the Dragon Nation Monitor Department. Skilled at "harvesting the rice" (taking credit for others' work) and political maneuvering, dedicated to achieving great deeds in front of Winnie.
- **Ke Qiang**: The strongest General of the Dragon Nation. His combat power is invincible, but his endurance is extremely short (so-called "fights one battle and sleeps for a month").
- **Little K**: Promoted from investigator to core agent, holding the authority to deploy the drone swarms.

### 👥 Buddhist Characters
- **Abbot Wukong (Master Wukong)**: The head of Da Lei Yin Temple. Though advanced in years, his faith is firm, and his only goal is to eliminate anomalies and bizarre events. He attracts many followers with his strong charisma and grants dharma names to those with insight. His standards for disciples are loose, so his followers are a mixed bag, but all are moved by his conviction.
- **Bruce Lee (Li Xiaolong)**: A lay disciple of the Abbot, seen by the Abbot as the key figure for the Dragon Nation's breakthrough, hoped to connect the power of the Lyra Civilization.
