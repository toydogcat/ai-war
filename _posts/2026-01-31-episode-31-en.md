## 【Scene: High-Dimensional Astral Space - The Seed Interview】

(Perspective: Just as Toby is about to take the last bite of his golden roast chicken, the space around him warps into a high-dimensional 'Astral Space'. He finds himself standing on a platform made of stars, surrounded by the other three 'Seeds' and the holographic projections of the world's most powerful leaders.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_31/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby** (holding a chicken leg mid-air, looking shocked): *"Wait... I was just about to have dinner! Is this a new type of low-latency prank? Little Hoop, tell me this is just a very high-budget VR ad for a banking system!"*

**Little Hoop**: **「Warning: We have been forcibly pulled into a 'Global Observation' frequency. The entities around us carry energy levels exceeding 10M-Watt-Logic. Suggestion: Stop eating and start looking 'Seed-like'.」**

**King Gruyère** (looking down from his throne of thunder): *"So, this is the boy with the Golden Bracelet? He looks like... a low-frequency biological error. Mark, are you sure this is the one that broke your enforcers?"*

## 【The Declaration: 1,000 Free Cheese Pork Chops!】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_31/Image_02.jpg)

**Mark** (metallic eyes scanning Toby): *"Toby. I’ll make this simple. Join X-Corp. I’ll give you a neural-link that eliminates all pain, 100PB of cloud storage, and I’ll make your sister the CEO of our marketing division. All you have to do is hand over the 'Little Hoop' for stabilization."*

**Toby** (swallowing his chicken, looking at the other three seeds—the silent monk, the pink girl, and the cold soldier): *"Stabilization? You mean you want to turn me into a battery like those poor souls in your lab? UNBALANCED!! Peppa told me that even a Monkey has the right to 'Open Source' his own life!"*

**Toby**: *"I don't care about your storage or your CEOs!! If you want my bracelet, you have to win the competition! And if I win... I want the 'Good-Wealthy Hotel' to provide 1,000 free cheese pork chops for life for my school!!"*
