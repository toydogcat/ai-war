## 【Scene: Sand-Tower Summit - The Final Wish】

(Perspective: The top of the Sand-Tower. **Aladdin** is holding the oil lamp high, his robes torn and flapping in the data-storm. Faced with the four Seeds, he prepares his most desperate move—the third and final wish. A shimmering bubble of 'Time-Erasure' logic begins to expand from the lamp, aiming to reset the local timeline to zero.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_46/Image_01.jpg)

**Aladdin** (face twitching with a mixture of fear and triumph): *"You win the battle, but I win the 'History'! With this wish, I will erase the very moment Toby found the bracelet! You will all be back to being Muggles, and the Middle-Eastern Coalition will rewrite the 'Golden Era' in its own image!! I WISH FOR THE GREAT RESET!!"*

**Toby** (running towards him, hand outstretched): *"NO!! You can't just delete the growth we've been through!! The 'Evolution' can't be UNDONE!!"*

**Little Hoop**: **「Temporal instability detected. The lamp is trying to find the `Initial-State` of the timeline. Warning: If he succeeds, all 'Love-Bonds' and 'Wisdom-Levels' will be wiped! Suggestion: Disrupt the 'Index' of his wish!」**

## 【The Sacrifice: Lily's Intervention】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_46/Image_02.jpg)

(Suddenly, a ghostly projection of **Lily Teacher** appears from within the Golden Bracelet. She looks transparent, her data-signature flickering with an intense white light.)

**Lily** (calmly): *"Toby... move aside. To stop a 'Global Reset', we need a 'Universal Constant' that even the lamp cannot override. I will use the 'Sacrificial Algorithm' to anchor this moment in the eternal server. But it will cost... my current existence."*

**Toby** (screaming): *"LILY!! NO!! DON'T DO IT!!"*
