## 【Scene: Alley behind Lyudoumiao Food Street】

(Scene Perspective: Deep in the shadows filled with the smell of pungent oily smoke and rust. **Toby** is slumped against a cold, moss-covered red brick wall, his stomach letting out a thunderous roar. That so-called "Soul Certification" not only drained his mental energy but also took away all the calories maintaining his physical existence, making him look like a data-dehydrated mummy.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby** (hoarse voice, pleading with the ring on his wrist): *"L-Little Hoop... you vampire... can't you first solve this quantized hunger that's about to vanish my existence? I feel like I'm seeing the Madonna of Heaven waving at me, even holding fried chicken and milk tea..."*

**Little Hoop** (letting out a cold and merciless "ding" in his mind): **"Hint: Subject energy level is below 15%. Detecting rapid degradation of subject's physiological functions. Advise executing emergency protocol: 'Pain Data Removal.' I can temporarily shield all your neural transmission data, allowing you to walk toward physical collapse in an elegant, sensation-free state. For a Lyran, this is currently the most 'aesthetic' way to take a final bow."**

**Toby** (rolling his eyes): *"Aesthetic my foot! Then wouldn't I just become like those 'monkeys' who brainlessly feed in prompts, dying for no reason? I want food! Carbohydrates!"*

---

## 【Sakura's Helping Hand: Red Bean Bun and Data Junk】

(Scene Transition: The rusted small door of the Lyudoumiao Electronic Games Shop is pushed open, and **Sakura**, wearing a hoodie with a large "GAME OVER" logo, steps out. She carries a yellow camping lantern, curiosity flashing under her eyepiece.)

**Sakura** (kicking Toby's sneaker): *"Yo, Toby? Are you playing some 'Roadside Corpse' immersive LARP here? You look even worse than those STEM monkeys who collapse because of network lag. Here, it's half a red bean bun I just finished, emergency aid for the world, swallow it."*

**Toby** (madly grabbing the bun): *"Phew... thanks for saving my life! Sakura, you are simply the only light in this data cage! What are you doing out here in this place filled with scrap metal instead of grinding monsters at the counter in the middle of the night?"*

**Sakura** (pointing at the mountainous pile of PS1s, black Xboxes, and scrapped motherboards behind her): *"The boss said these were all outdated junk and asked me to clear them tonight. Otherwise, did you think that half-bun was for free? Toby, if you can get rid of this pile of 'data residue,' our debt from just now will be wiped clean."*

---

## 【Atomic Restructuring: The Power of the Hoop】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_02.jpg)

(Scene Close-up: Toby's hands tremble as they slowly press down on the cold, discarded parts. The golden bracelet on his wrist, "Little Hoop," senses the outdated processing units and lets out a violent resonance.)

**Little Hoop**: **"Detecting outdated magnetic sector units. Executing Lyra's Highest Correction Directive: 'Atomic Restructuring.' Subject authorization in progress... Warning: This operation will consume the subject's last glycogen reserves. Please ensure large intake of sugar after execution, otherwise it may lead to intelligence drop to 'STEM Monkey' level."**

**Toby** (gritting his teeth): *"Do it! As long as I don't turn back into one of those stubborn monkeys, I'll pay any price!"*

(Full-panel Spread: Under the perspective of the "Soul Eye," the pile of rotting electronic equipment disintegrates into hundreds of billions of grey data dust particles, then reconstructs under the guidance of golden code. In just three seconds, all the scrap iron condenses into a "Mythological Grade Laptop" with a cold exterior and flowing silver mist.)

## 【Guanghua Digital Plaza: The Jaw-dropping Appraiser】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_03.jpg)

(Next Scene: A second-hand repair shop deep in the Guanghua Digital Plaza. A professional appraiser wearing thick AR glasses casually connects a test cable.)

**Appraiser** (initially nonchalant, then his voice abruptly stops): *"Y-Young man... what kind of ghost thing are you bringing here to test? (The red warning label on the screen flashes madly) Read speed 400 TB/s?! How can this old SATA slot possibly run at such superfluid transmission rates? Is this some kind of cold fusion black-tech you're using?!"*

**Toby** (touching his hot bracelet, forcing a smile): *"Oh, that's just me helpin this old laptop... run a powerful 'Function Call,' that's all. Boss, how much are you offering?"*

**Appraiser** (cold sweat trickling down, hands shaking): *"I-I only have 200,000 USD in cash on hand... no, deal! I'll take the protocol authorization for this machine!"*
