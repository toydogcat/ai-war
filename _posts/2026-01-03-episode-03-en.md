## 【Scene: Shilin Night Market - Deep Alleyway】

(Scene: Shilin Night Market, the air thick with the smell of stinky tofu and roasting meat. **Toby** is hunched over a plate of fried squid, his face glowing from the bracelet on his wrist. Little does he know, a pair of sharp, digitized eyes is fixed on his back from the cover of a nearby bubble tea stall.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby** (stuffing food in his mouth, blissfully unaware): *"Mmm... this is the life! Little Hoop, see? This squid ball has just the right amount of 'Love' frequency. I can feel my energy reserves climbing to 40%! Who knew being a cosmic chosen one would taste so good?"*

**Little Hoop**: **「Warning: High-intensity data scan detected. Target: 10 o'clock position, behind the 'Frog Hits Milk' sign. Subject profile: High-level 'New Human' combatant. Code Name: 'Little Tooth'. Suggestion: Stop chewing and prepare to run.」**

---

## 【Encounter: Little Tooth and the Pink Small Fang】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_02.jpg)

(A girl with silver hair and a high-tech holographic eyepiece steps out from the shadows. She carries a sleek, pink weapon that looks like a futuristic toy gun but radiates an ominous energy. This is **Xiao Ying**, known in the underworld as **'Little Tooth'**.)

**Little Tooth** (voice cold and precise): *"Subject Toby. Detection: Unauthorized possession of a Lyra-Class Interface. You are carrying a 'Seed' that your monkey-level soul cannot possibly handle. Hand over the bracelet, and I might leave you with enough brain cells to remember your own name."*

**Toby** (choking on a squid ball, pointing at the pink gun): *"Wait! Is that a... a high-tech water gun? Look, kid, I just won this in a raffle! I'm just a TA! Peppa said this is for 'Love and Awakening'!"*

---

## 【Clash: The First Spark of War】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_03.jpg)

**Little Tooth** (ignoring him, raising her weapon): *"Protocol: Asset Recovery. Target: The Golden Hoop. Commencing data deletion."*

(The pink gun, nicknamed **'Small Tooth'**, emits a blast of rose-colored energy. The market stalls around them flicker as the reality code is temporarily overwritten. Toby's bracelet flares with a protective golden barrier.)

**Little Hoop**: **「Deploying 'Love-Shield' LV.01. Energy consumption: 15%. Master, stop acting like a bystander! Your survival is currently being computed at 12%. I suggest you use your 'Monkey Agility' to evade while I find a vulnerability in her encryption!」**

**Toby** (diving behind a cart of stinky tofu): *"Monkey agility?! I'm a human! Help!! Peppa, you didn't say the 'gift' came with a pink-gun assassin!!"*
