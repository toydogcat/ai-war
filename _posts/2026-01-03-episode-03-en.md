## 【Scene: Virgin Garden Food Court Back Alley】

(Forty minutes later: at the back alley of the Virgin Garden food court, a place filled with the smell of grease and the shadows of scrapped parts)

Toby leans against the cold brick wall, his forehead drenched in cold sweat. His stomach lets out a thunderous, hollow roar, reminding him every second that the "Soul Certification" just now drained every bit of glucose from his body.

(Scene Perspective: [Back Alley Shadows] Toby leans against the brick wall of the Virgin Garden food court back alley, drenched in cold sweat, face pale, hands clutching his growling stomach, looking extremely exhausted.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_01.jpg)

---

(Scene: [Soul Conversation] Toby looks down at the faintly shimmering bracelet "Xiao Huan" (Little Hoop), his eyes dazed, as if his body is gradually becoming transparent and granulated. The image carries an unstable digital flicker.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_02.jpg)

**Toby**: Xiao... Xiao Huan... are you there? Can you help me deal with this hunger first? I feel like I'm about to disappear into quantum bits.

**Xiao Huan (A hollow voice in the mind, with faint electric hum)**: *"Hint: Subject energy level below 15%. Detected highly chaotic storage structures in the surroundings. Subject, if you wish to remove physical sensations, it is suggested to execute: 'Data Remove.' I can remove the digital data of your neural transmissions, temporarily blocking your hunger signals."*

**Toby**: Then will I still be hungry, just without my brain knowing? Will I die?

---

(Scene: [At the Green Bean Sprout Entrance] Sakura, wearing a "GAME OVER" hoodie, hands Toby a steaming red bean bun with a worried look. In the background is the video game store with a neon sign flashing "Green Bean Sprout.")

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_03.jpg)

**Sakura**: Toby, what are you doing here? You look so weak, here's a red bean bun.

**Toby**: Oh, Sakura! I'm so hungry, I just got hit by sudden starvation. Let me eat first... Better now. Do you have any more food?

**Sakura**: No more bread, but we have a pile of outdated old-stock game consoles to be treated as trash. Take them if you want.

---

(Scene: [Junk Pile] In front of the store, a pile of dusty old game consoles (PS, XBOX, disk drives). Sakura points casually at the scrap metal. Though still chewing bread, Toby's eyes are already drawn to Xiao Huan's detection signal.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_04.jpg)

**Xiao Huan**: Detected low-level digital machinery nearby capable of evolution.

**Toby**: You must be joking, I can't eat plastic and iron. I know what you mean, I'll help you clear away the trash as payment for the bread.

**Sakura**: Why have you become like this? Toyed with by Peppa again? I'm going to go get justice for you.

(Sakura runs off angrily in a flash.)

---

(Scene: [Restructuring Initiated] Toby kneels before the junk pile, pressing his hand with the golden bracelet onto the cold casing. Instantly, an intense golden light explodes, illuminating the entire shadow.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_05.jpg)

**Toby**: Leaving all this trash for me to handle alone, is that right? I won't back out, but why run so fast... Seriously, I'm still hungry.

**Xiao Huan**: *"Detected outdated storage units. Sector structure: Chaotic. Suggesting: Atomic-level Sector Restructuring. Subject, please confirm Function Call permissions."*

**Toby**: Confirmed! Give it your all!

---

(Scene: [Atomic Restructuring] Under the vision of the Soul Eye, metal molecules are disassembled into golden data dust, dancing and rearranging in the air with Lyrian high-dimensional geometric beauty.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_06.jpg)

**(In an instant, Toby feels a suction force from his palm. Under the vision of the "Soul Eye," those broken old game consoles are no longer scrap metal, but countless pulsing gray data specks. With Xiao Huan's "Restructure" command, the dust instantly turns gold, dancing and aligning in the void.)**

**Xiao Huan**: *"Restructuring complete. Read/write protocols upgraded to: 'Lyra Superfluid Transfer Protocol.' Ding! Reminding you, you've just used up this month's free quota."*

---

(Scene: [God-Tier Laptop Born] Toby holds a perfect laptop emitting a matte silver glow. The original trash has completely evolved into an existence transcending human technology. Toby's eyes are full of amazement.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_07.jpg)

Looking at what was once a mountain of game console trash, transformed into a brand-new laptop in his hands. The rust on the casing has vanished, replaced by a silk-smooth matte silver.

**Toby**: This... this isn't AI, it's a miracle. Even physical hardware structures can be rewritten...

---

(Scene: [Cash Transaction] In a second-hand shop, the appraiser is stunned by the 400 TB/s warning label popping up on the screen. Toby tucks a thick stack of USD bills into his pocket, finally relaxing.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_03/Image_08.jpg)

**Appraiser**: Kid... this read speed... 400 TB/s? What kind of cold fusion tech are you using? How can this old slot handle such bandwidth?!

**Toby**: Uh, that's... a Python optimization plugin I developed.

**Appraiser**: An optimization plugin? This is pure magic! I'll give you 200,000 USD in cash right now to buy it!

**Toby**: (Taking the thick stack of bills, heart pounding like a drum.) "Xiao Huan, let's go. Seems the 'Monkey School' tuition... is covered."

**(Deep in the alley, an electronic eye slowly shuts. Simultaneously, the Lyra high-dimensional channel flashes again.)**
**[SYSTEM HINT: DETECTED LOW-LEVEL DRAGON NATION INTERFERENCE. ANOMALOUS COMPUTE FLUCTUATION MARKED.]**
