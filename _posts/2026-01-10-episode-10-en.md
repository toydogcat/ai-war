## 【Location: Bus 645】

As the bus entered Ziqiang Tunnel, it kept driving and driving, as if it could never leave.

(Scene: Inside the bus, tunnel lights streak past the windows, cold and oppressive. Master Lin stares grimly at his watch.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_01.jpg)

**Master Lin**: Qiu Sheng, Wen Cai, did you notice?

**Wen Cai**: Yes Master, that thief just got off.

**Qiu Sheng**: Stupid! Master is trying to say this tunnel is incredibly long. We should learn from his observation.

---

Master Lin's fingers tap rhythmically and incessantly.

(Scene: Master Lin's fingers tap his leg in a rhythmic pattern, like an ancient counting method. His two disciples watch from the back seat.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_02.jpg)

**Master Lin**: I mean, have you two counted the time? How long has this bus been in the tunnel?

**Boyfriend**: Strange, Ziqiang Tunnel usually takes 5-10 minutes to pass through. It's been so long this time!!

**Master Lin**: By my count since I first noticed, it's been over half an hour. Something is wrong. The scenery outside is strange too.

---

(Scene: The boyfriend holds his glowing phone, the screen showing a navigation error. Master Lin points out the window to strange vegetation, emphasizing the time anomaly.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_03.jpg)

**Boyfriend**: Who are you guys? Looks like we're trapped in this tunnel. There aren't many people on the bus. Should we call the police?

**Wen Cai**: You're lucky! This is Maoshan Taoist Master Lin Ching-ying, a master of exorcising demons. We're saved this time, right Master?

**Qiu Sheng**: Each of you pay 10 taels of silver, and I'll protect you. Isn't that right, Master?

**Master Lin**: I haven't even figured out the situation yet, and you've finished all my lines for me. What am I supposed to do?

---

The Qing Dynasty zombies that were originally behind the bus had crawled underneath Bus 645 after entering Ziqiang Tunnel. They lifted the bus with their hands. The wheels had been spinning in the air for a while; the zombies were the ones carrying Bus 645 toward another destination. Ziqiang Tunnel is connected in all directions, and while people usually take the main roads, the zombies were heading toward a small, secluded tunnel in the mountains. Their intentions for the passengers remained unknown.

(Scene: Perspective from under the bus. The bus is suspended in mid-air. Rotting hands in Qing Dynasty official robes grip the chassis tightly. The zombies are sprinting through a narrow mountain path, carrying the bus.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_04.jpg)

**Girlfriend**: I don't know about silver, but do you know Hermes or Chanel? I can give you bags. A bag can cure anything! Or do you want cosmetics or lipstick? Or later, you can go with my boyfriend to see the latest AI. He knows it well and can teach you about high technology. Do you know high-tech? It's very impressive!

**Master Lin**: Stop, stop! Ma'am, you talk so much it gives me a headache. Spare me your "divine powers." Save your breath to deal with the supernatural.

**Qiu Sheng**: Master, so even you have your limits.

**Master Lin**: Too much nonsense!

---

Master Lin turns to explain the situation to the terrified couple.

(Scene: The girlfriend chatters incessantly at the Master, waving her designer bag and talking about high-tech. Master Lin has black lines on his face, clearly experiencing a headache.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_05.jpg)

**Master Lin**: In this world, there are terrifying monsters like vengeful ghosts and zombies. These unexplainable events are professionally termed "Supernatural/Anomalous Events." Don't be afraid. I am from Maoshan, specializing in dealing with these weird occurrences. For instance, to deal with zombies, use a peach wood sword. To avoid a zombie's perception, you must temporarily hold your breath. Zombies are formed from the accumulated resentment of the dead. Raw glutinous rice can be used to absorb the resentment from their bodies. Ordinary people can also deal with these anomalies with proper professional training. So, don't be afraid...

**Boyfriend**: Stop, stop! You're more of a chatterbox than my girlfriend. You're saying all this nonsense; can you just get to the point?

---

Qiu Sheng and Wen Cai whisper to each other.

**Qiu Sheng**: Haha, for once we aren't the ones being lectured. I told you Master talks too much.

**Wen Cai**: Yeah, Master always drones on. Who can remember all that?

(Scene: Qiu Sheng and Wen Cai huddle together, whispering about how Master "lectures for too long." They look very amused by the situation.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_06.jpg)

Master Lin's "incantation" is interrupted, and he cannot continue his lecture.

**Master Lin**: Don't be nervous. I'll go outside and take a look first. Qiu Sheng, Wen Cai, you stay and protect them. I'm going down.

**Qiu Sheng**: Be careful, Master! We've got this!

**Wen Cai**: Yes, Master. Take care.

---

Master Lin immediately puts on his Taoist robe, takes some talismans and his peach wood sword, and leaps out of the window. Qiu Sheng and Wen Cai watch as their master leaves.

(Scene: Master Lin dons his yellow Taoist robe, holding a wooden sword and charms, leaping out of the bus window into the night with a classic action-movie pose.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_07.jpg)

**Qiu Sheng**: Master is gone. Right now, the most dangerous thing on this bus was probably him—his lecturing attack was unbearable.

**Boyfriend**: An expert is indeed an expert. They must always exit through the window. It wouldn't be "expert" enough to use the door.

**Qiu Sheng**: Don't be silly. Master just didn't know where the door was or how to get off the bus. If he knew, would he still do that?

**Wen Cai**: Don't be afraid. We're very capable. Just follow our instructions later. We've saved many people before.

**Girlfriend**: Your Master is so handsome! And he's such an eloquent lecturer!!

---

Climbing onto the roof from the window, Master Lin finds everything pitch black. The bus is moving, but he can't confirm the route. It's clearly moving toward a remote mountainous area. As a professional, Master Lin knows not to act recklessly during a supernatural event. He takes out a Bagua mirror and begins scanning the surroundings, thinking it might be an illusion or a psychological realm. But the Bagua mirror, a sacred object for breaking illusions, shows nothing unusual, meaning the current situation is real and not a trap.

(Scene: Master Lin stands on the roof of the fast-moving (actually being carried) bus, holding the Bagua mirror. The mirror reflects a cold light, attempting to reveal any hidden illusions.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_08.jpg)

Master Lin takes coins from his pocket and begins a divination gesture. He confirms the bus is truly moving. Master Lin is baffled; everything seems normal—no illusion, no barrier, the bus is physically moving. Could he just be overthinking? He flips back inside the bus.

**Master Lin**: Does anyone know the driver? I need to ask him some questions.

**Boyfriend**: This is an autonomous bus. There's no driver.

**Master Lin**: Damn it, is it a scientific problem? A bus malfunction! I thought it was a metaphysical problem—my specialty!!

**Girlfriend**: I take this bus often. I always go to Godiva (Goutofu) to buy bags. I can be 100% sure today's bus is not normal. Godiva is in the Tianlong District; it's very prosperous and wouldn't lead to this desolate place.

---

(Scene: Master Lin returns to the bus looking frustrated, complaining to the couple that this is a "scientific problem" (missing driver) rather than a "metaphysical" one.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_09.jpg)

**Master Lin**: Strange. What exactly is going on? I can't detect the problem, yet everything is the problem.

**Qiu Sheng**: Master, what's the situation?

**Master Lin**: I'm certain there's no illusion or barrier. The bus is physically moving. I can't see the issue. When facing the supernatural, you must find the core of the problem, or it cannot be solved.

**Qiu Sheng**: Master, is it possible that "we cannot see the true face of Lushan simply because we are in the mountain"? Can we leave the bus? Since we can't see the problem from inside, why don't we look from the outside?

**Master Lin**: Do you think I'm stupid? Once I get off, how am I supposed to get back on? Are you trying to trick your Master?

---

**Boyfriend**: Don't argue. Every bus has an emergency stop button. Let's all get off together and take a look. How about that?

The boyfriend presses the emergency stop button, but it doesn't work!

(Scene: The boyfriend frantically presses the red emergency button in panic, but there's no response. The Master stands by, quietly taking out silver coins for divination.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_10.jpg)

**Boyfriend** (Shocked): Strange! The system is broken! It's not stopping!!

**Master Lin**: I'll jump off and see. Qiu Sheng, Wen Cai, you stay on the bus and look after them!!

---

Jumping off the bus, Master Lin immediately realizes that Bus 645 isn't even touching the ground—it's being carried by zombies. He instantly bites his finger, takes his peach wood sword, passes it through a talisman, smears his blood on the sword, and hurls it at the zombies.

(Scene: Master Lin bites his index finger, smears blood on the peach wood sword, and throws it at the zombies. An explosive force causes the bus to shake violently and crash into the forest floor.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_11.jpg)

**Master Lin**: Where did these zombies come from!!

The zombies, sprinting away while carrying the bus, notice Master Lin behind them. They spin around and hurl the bus toward the peach wood sword. Inside, everything is in chaos as the bus hits the sword, crashes to the ground, breaks its wheels, and can no longer move.

---

**Boyfriend**: An expert is indeed an expert. Even the ruckus they cause is this big.

**Wen Cai**: Master! I'm so dizzy! Why was it so shaky?

**Qiu Sheng**: Trouble! Something big happened outside. Wen Cai, protect them. I'm going out to help Master.

Qiu Sheng takes his Maoshan bag and tools and leaps out of the window.

(Scene: Qiu Sheng carries a large bag, mimicking his Master's graceful leap out the window—though his move is slightly clumsier. He already has two yellow charms in his hand.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_12.jpg)

**Boyfriend**: An expert is indeed an expert. They must always exit through the window. It wouldn't be "expert" enough to use the door.

**Wen Cai**: Are you stupid? Didn't you see the door is deformed? We'll have to go through the window later too.

**Girlfriend**: My nails! My bag! Waaah!

**Wen Cai**: Quickly, let's get out! It's getting more dangerous inside the bus!

## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 10 Supplement

### ☯️ Maoshan Demon-Slaying Team

- **Master Lin (Lin Ching-ying)**: A calm, professional Taoist Master bewildered by modern bus automation.
- **Qiu Sheng**: Has a strong sense of justice and is agile, but sometimes tries to make a quick buck.
- **Wen Cai**: Timid and easily scared, but follows Master's orders in critical moments.

### ☯️ Maoshan Artifacts

- **Peach Wood Sword**: The signature weapon of Maoshan Taoists, made of peach wood and effective for warding off evil and exorcising ghosts.
- **Talismans (Charm Paper)**: Tools used by Maoshan Taoists for spells. They can be drawn on paper, attached to objects, or burned.
- **Glutinous Rice**: Used by Maoshan Taoists against zombies. It absorbs the resentment from their bodies, rendering them immobile.
- **Bagua Mirror**: A sacred object for breaking illusions, revealing the true nature of a realm.

### 💩 Anomalous Zombies

- **Zombies**: A type of supernatural anomaly. They are physical monsters that Maoshan arts can counter.
- **Traits**: Incredible strength, invulnerability to normal weapons, capable of lifting a bus.
- **Scent**: Extremely foul, likely having not bathed for decades.
