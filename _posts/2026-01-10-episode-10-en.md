## 【Scene: Classroom B-101 - The Logic Lock】

(Perspective: The screen in the classroom flickers violently. Strelitzia has locked the door to the classroom, her expression icy. She has initiated a 'Root-Level Math Test'. Toby's golden bracelet is heating up, and the air around him is humming with a frequency that smells of ozone.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_01.jpg)

**Strelitzia** (voice resonating like a frequency modulator): *"The test is simple, Monkey. Solve the 'Quantum-Decay' equation displayed on the main board. If your brain's biological latency is higher than 0.001ms, the classroom's firewall will interpret you as a 'Bad Sector' and initiate an automatic purge. Start now."*

**Toby** (staring at the board, sweat dripping onto his desktop): *"Little Hoop! I don't even know what a Quantum Decay is! I thought we were just learning basic Python today! Help me out or we're both going to be recycled!"*

**Little Hoop**: **「Warning: High-dimensional math detected. Analyzing... Error: Calculation capacity insufficient in 'Idle Mode'. Suggestion: Activate 'Kernel Sync'. Note: Doing so will reveal our presence to everyone in the room. Permission?」**

## 【The Revelation: The Golden Aura】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_02.jpg)

**Toby** (looking around at his terrified classmates, seeing the classroom walls begin to pixelate into a void): *"Whatever! Just do it! If we don't pass, there won't be anyone left to see anyway! EXECUTE!!"*

(Visual Explosion: A blindingly pure golden light erupts from Toby's wrist, forming a sphere of energy that instantly stabilizes the room's distortions. On the main board, millions of lines of code are being rewritten at a speed that makes Strelitzia's eyes widen.)

**Strelitzia** (smiling coldly): *"There you are... the Golden Seed. You're not a user; you're a walking backdoor. And you've just handed me the keys to your soul."*

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_10/Image_03.jpg)
