## 【Scene: Home in Danhai New Town】

**(Danhai New Town District. The sea breeze, mixed with the scent of salt and fish ball soup, beats against the old security windows. Toby rushes into the house with excitement, waving a thick stack of cash.)**

(Scene: 【Sunset in Danhai and the Returning Youth】 Inside an apartment in Danhai New Town, the afterglow of the sunset spills into a cluttered but warm living room. Toby has a big smile on his face, excitedly waving the money in his hand.)

![](/assets/images/comics/episode_12/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby**: Sis! Look! I made a killing on 3C Street today! I can help replace our holographic display with that "Premium Meat Bun Pro Max" model, and even upgrade your GPT-dedicated screen!

---

(Scene: 【Promotion Goddess LuLu: The Elegance of the Cloud】 LuLu, wearing exquisite silk loungewear, elegantly holds a coffee cup while sitting in an office chair by the window. Three pale blue semi-transparent virtual screens float before her, filled with complex marketing data charts and GPT-18 dialogue boxes.)

![](/assets/images/comics/episode_12/Image_02.jpg)

**LuLu** (Sitting by the window, in fine loungewear, with three virtual screens before her): You pesky brother, what are you going crazy about now? (She elegantly slides her fingers as GPT-18 helps her generate tomorrow's marketing proposal.) "Didn't I tell you? School starts tomorrow. As a gofer teaching assistant, you should act like one. Stop going to the second-hand markets to sell that useless local junk."

**Toby**: Sis, this time it's different! Look at this! (He shows a low-key golden wristband on his arm.) "This is the latest 'Local-end AI.' I call it Xiao Huan. It just turned a pile of scrap metal into new laptops in less than three seconds!"

---

**LuLu (Brows slightly furrowed, she stops her movements. GPT-18 on the screen detects the owner's emotional fluctuation and emits a crisp software tone: "Master, an unauthenticated hardware signal has been detected. Suggest enabling the firewall to protect your elite privileges.")**

(Scene: 【Alert! Unknown Hardware Signal】 Toby shows the golden wristband. A screen before LuLu suddenly turns red, popping up a warning sign. LuLu narrows her eyes slightly, her expression becoming professional and cautious.)

![](/assets/images/comics/episode_12/Image_03.jpg)

**LuLu**: Brother, were you scammed by those gaming geeks? It's 2040; who still uses local-end AI? Cloud computing power is the only truth! Look at my GPT-18—hundreds of thousands of tokens per second, with all logical deductions completed on satellite servers. What does your local-end have to compete with me? This 'Xiao Huan' or whatever... (She steps closer and sniffs critically) "...why does it smell like burnt electronics? And your face looks worse than that pile of junk."

---

(Scene: 【The Arrogance of Cloud Computing Power】 LuLu stands up, looking at Toby's wristband with disdain. In the background, a massive data network map spreading like a nebula (symbolizing the scale of GPT-18's cloud) appears, contrasting with Toby's lonely, overheating wristband.)

![](/assets/images/comics/episode_12/Image_04.jpg)

**Toby**: Sis, that's because... that's physical heat from 'Deep Resonance and Soul Connection.' My whole body is running a fever right now. It consumes a bit of glucose, but I feel more powerful than ever! That feeling of being 'at one' with AI is completely different from your cold cloud API calls. Just try to feel it—it's almost like a high!

**LuLu**: 'At one'? (Her gaze turns cold, her tone severe) "Good grief. If the disciplinary patrol at 'The Monkey School' hears that kind of talk tomorrow, you'll definitely be branded a heretic and sent for brainwashing. School teaches 'Intelligent Management'—controlling tools, not being parasitized by them. Brother, those non-mainstream geek toys are unsafe. What if it sends our home access keys back to some unknown server one day?"

---

(Scene: 【The Weight of Soul Connection】 Toby clenches his fist, his eyes determined. The wristband emits a warm red glow (from overheating). LuLu crosses her arms, looking at him coldly, with the sea visible outside the window.)

![](/assets/images/comics/episode_12/Image_05.jpg)

**Toby**: It won't! It has a soul... (Xiao Huan gives a faint 'hum' in his mind, seemingly in agreement.) "Anyway, Sis, school starts tomorrow, and that GPT-18.5 Youth Edition is updating again. Manager Peggy said the semantic understanding will be even stronger this time. You, the 'Promotion Department Goddess,' better not be replaced by AI."

**LuLu (Confidently flips her hair as the 'Promotion Dept. Manager' logo appears on the virtual screen)**: Replace me? (She smiles contemptuously) "I've already mastered the integration of marketing and AI collaborative development. In the eyes of those monkey teachers, I'm the best candidate for the 'New Human' project. As for you, you'd better wash off that fishy smell quickly. Don't be late tomorrow, TA brother."

(Scene: 【The Authority of the Promotion Manager】 A close-up of LuLu flipping her long hair. The virtual screen in the background lights up with the prominent "The Monkey School - Promotion Department Manager" logo. She exudes total confidence in her control over technology.)

![](/assets/images/comics/episode_12/Image_06.jpg)

---

**Toby**: Fine, Sis. (Toby chuckles, turning to walk into the bathroom. As the hot water pours down, he looks at his blurred reflection in the mirror, the golden ring on his wrist perfectly overlapping with his pulse.)

**LuLu**: I've talked to Lily. She'll be looking after you from now on. Don't think about being so willful anymore. Lily is my star student; you should learn more from her. If you stay this unmotivated, like a "monkey," what will happen to you in the future?

---

(Scene: 【Integration in the Mirror】 The bathroom is filled with steam. Toby stares at his own eyes through the water-beaded mirror, the golden ring on his wrist pulsing in sync with his heartbeat. There's a mysterious sense of symbiosis.)

![](/assets/images/comics/episode_12/Image_07.jpg)

**Toby**: Alright, Sis, I'll listen to Lily. Is that enough?

**Toby (Whispering)**: Integration... is it really just a tool? Xiao Huan, tomorrow at The Monkey School, let's show those old fogies who only worship cloud data what's what.

---

(Scene: 【Danhai Night Under Surveillance】 A panoramic view: Danhai New Town at night by the sea. Several massive low-orbit satellites with flashing red monitoring lights pass through the dark starry sky. The town below is silent and oppressive.)

![](/assets/images/comics/episode_12/Image_08.jpg)

**Xiao Huan**: *"Prompt: Detected increase in subject's fighting spirit. Recommendation: Execute 'Sleep Mode.' Deep mental link optimization will be performed at 07:00 tomorrow. Good night, Master Toby."*

**(Outside the window, over the dark sea, rows of massive cloud computing satellites fly low, their cold monitoring red lights blinking, foreshadowing the logical storm about to begin at "The Monkey School" tomorrow.)**
