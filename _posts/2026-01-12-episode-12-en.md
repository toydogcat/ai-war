## 【Scene: Tamsui New Town - Toby's Apartment】

(Perspective: Outside the massive floor-to-ceiling windows, the Tamsui River glimmers with a cold blue metallic sheen. Several giant calculation satellites marked with the Cloud Legion insignia hum low over the horizon. Inside, the space is a minimalist white and silver. **Lulu** (Toby's older sister) is elegantly floating in front of several pale-blue virtual screens, her hands moving like a pianist's.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_12/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby** (pushing open the heavy biometric door, excitedly waving a stack of mysterious 2040-numbered bills he won at an underground auction): *"Sis!! We hit the jackpot! You won't believe what I flipped on the black market today! With this, we can finally upgrade the home hologram to the 'Niku-Bun Pro Max'! I promise your eyes won't be blinded by flashing data when you're reading GPT reports anymore!"*

**Lulu** (not even turning around, her voice light but cold as an ice pick): *"Little brother, what madness are you up to now? (closes a window) Don't think for a second that earning a bit of data-money means you can act big in school. Tomorrow is the start of the new semester; you're a TA, so get your act together. Stop messing around with that abandoned local scrap in the second-hand markets."*

**Toby** (defiantly showing off the golden-light-pulsing bracelet on his wrist): *"It's not scrap! It's a singularity from the Lyra System—'Little Hoop'! Sis, did you know it fixed 1PB of bad sectors in less than three seconds? Can your cloud backend do that?"*

## 【Cloud Supremacy: The Arrogance of GPT-18】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_12/Image_02.jpg)

(Scene: The main screen in front of Lulu suddenly lights up with a sharp blood-red warning. The built-in high-level management AI, **GPT-18**, has detected an unauthenticated low-level hardware signal, emitting a condescending chime.)

**Lulu** (finally turning around, her gaze sharp enough to see the code in Toby's veins): *"Brother, don't be fooled by cheap psychological suggestions. In the Big Data era, only 'Access Rights' and 'Cloud Bandwidth' are the truth. My GPT-18 can perform hundreds of thousands of logic deductions in a second—all intelligence contributions are done on the satellite. (She walks closer, sniffing the air with disgust) ...You smell like burnt, cheap electronic components. Keep messing with that bracelet, and your brain will be cooked into a blank slate."*

**Toby** (face flushing red, arguing stubbornly): *"That's just calling a cold API! That's just being a heartless mover! This feeling of being synced with 'Little Hoop', of your soul being filled with data... a 'New Human' like you who only looks at reports will never understand! Sis, if one day your satellite goes offline or your access expires, how are you going to maintain your 'refined' New Human life?"*

Lily is my elite student; if you dare run around there with that heretical bracelet and get caught by the school's Logic Purge Team... I can't guarantee I can bring you back."*
