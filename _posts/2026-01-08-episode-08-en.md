## 【Scene: Route 645 Bus】

In 2040, buses on Taiwan island are fully autonomous and operate 24 hours a day. Sakura, having just finished a BBQ mission, boards the Route 645 bus with her remaining supplies, heading back to Green Sprout for some rest. It's late, and she's so exhausted she immediately begins to doze off. Her last impressions before falling asleep: three people to her front-left (a middle-aged man and two young men), a teenage girl next to her, and a young couple to her front-right. Before long, she's snoring away.

(Screen: Inside the bus, a fashionable couple is excitedly chatting. The man is looking at a high-tech AI on a holographic phone interface, while the woman points at luxury handbags on a shopping site, her face full of materialistic desire.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_08/Image_01.jpg)

**Boyfriend**: Sigh, we have to keep learning. Later, we'll go to Gao Dou Fu and get the latest AI. We must work hard every day. We need to leave being "monkeys" behind and evolve into true humans!

**Girlfriend**: You're so hardworking, baby, that's why I love you. You look at the 3C AI, and I'll get the latest bag and some new lipstick. I'm bored with all my current colors.

---

(Screen: In the front row sit Master Lin (in dark green traditional attire, looking solemn) and his two disciples, Qiu Sheng and Wen Cai. Qiu Sheng is shivering, while Wen Cai looks dazed. The driver's seat is visibly empty.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_08/Image_02.jpg)

**Master Lin**: Qiu Sheng, Wen Cai, have you noticed?

**Wen Cai**: Master, what are you talking about? I didn't notice anything.

**Qiu Sheng**: Stupid! Master means it's really cold in here.

---

(Screen: The Master is sternly lecturing his disciples, looking strict but a bit tired. Qiu Sheng and Wen Cai hang their heads, the scene having a touch of exaggerated Hong Kong comedy style.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_08/Image_03.jpg)

**Master Lin**: I mean, have you noticed? Where is the driver hiding? His skill is quite high. You two should learn from him. When you face terrifying Jiangshis or malevolent spirits with me later, you'd better hide. Every time you just shout "Master, save us! Master, save us!" Give me some peace, will you? Master is getting old; my back keeps going out!

**Qiu Sheng + Wen Cai**: Yes, Master!

---

Qiu Sheng and Wen Cai whisper to each other.

(Screen: Qiu Sheng and Wen Cai huddle close; Qiu Sheng looks suspicious while pointing at the Master's back, and Wen Cai secretly rolls his eyes. The cabin is dimly lit.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_08/Image_04.jpg)

**Qiu Sheng**: Master's getting more and more talkative. Lately, every time we catch a Jiangshi, his back "goes out." I wonder if he's faking it.

**Wen Cai**: Master wants to train us. How can you doubt his intentions? Hurry up and memorize the Maoshan Ancestral Precepts, or he'll lecture us again until our ears grow callouses. I must say, Master's "Monk Tripitaka" lecturing style is getting powerful.

---

Toward the back of the bus, something is slowly crawling up and entering through a rear window. Sakura is still sound asleep. The girl next to her, Lu Qing, clearly notices something is wrong. The couple in front is still planning their shopping spree, and the other three men are observing the driverless bus with curiosity. Just as the bus is about to enter the Zi Qiang Tunnel, the girl next to Sakura, Lu Qing, makes her move. She grabs Sakura's clothes and starts shouting.

(Screen: A figure in tattered Qing Dynasty official robes is crawling into the bus through the rear window in a bizarre posture. Sakura is still dozing in the back row, while Lu Qing snaps her eyes open, looking sharp and cold.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_08/Image_05.jpg)

**Lu Qing**: Harassment! You girl, how could you be so bad? You stole my wallet!

Sakura is jolted awake by the sudden grab.

**Sakura**: Who are you?! What did you say I stole? I didn't! I was just sleeping!

---

A commotion erupts in the middle of the bus. The thing in the Qing Dynasty robes that crawled in sits at the very back, motionless. Qiu Sheng, being full of justice, immediately shouts out.

(Screen: Lu Qing grabs Sakura's collar and pulls her awake, loudly accusing her of theft. All passengers, including the couple and Qiu Sheng, turn their heads in surprise.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_08/Image_06.jpg)

**Qiu Sheng**: What scoundrel is this? Surrender now!

**Sakura**: Little girl, stop it. I'm exhausted and just want to go home and rest. I really didn't steal anything; I was asleep.

**Lu Qing**: What thief would admit they're a thief? Come to the front with me so everyone can judge!

---

The boyfriend slams the emergency stop button, and the Route 645 bus screeches to a halt just before the Zi Qing Tunnel.

(Screen: The boyfriend irritably hits the red "EMERGENCY STOP" button. Smoke rises from the tires as the bus shakes violently.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_08/Image_07.jpg)

**Boyfriend**: If you're going to argue, take it outside! We have important things to do; we can't play games with you.

**Girlfriend**: Exactly! My bags, lipstick, and makeup are waiting for me! Go play outside, be good!

---

Ignoring Sakura's protests, Lu Qing grabs Sakura's arm and drags her off the bus. Sakura realizes Lu Qing's strength is immense; she can't resist at all.

(Screen: The bus doors open, and a cold-faced Lu Qing drags a struggling Sakura off the vehicle. In the background is the eerie tunnel entrance on a rainy night. A dark Jiangshi silhouette watches them from behind the bus window.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_08/Image_08.jpg)

**Lu Qing**: Come on, let's go find the police. We'll see what you have to say then.

**Sakura**: Little girl, you've really misunderstood! I didn't steal your things!
