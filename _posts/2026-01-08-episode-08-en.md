## 【Scene: Green Bean Sprout - The Shattered Mirror】

(Scene: The KTV booth is saturated with a toxic green glow. The 8-Bit Girl has her hands around Toby's throat. Suddenly, a massive cleaver embedded with a weathered copper coin shatters the pixelated wall. The air is instantly purified by the overwhelming aroma of scallion oil and deep-fried goodness.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_08/Image_01.jpg)

**Master Li Long** (stepping through the rift in reality, his face illuminated by the fryer's fire he brought with him): *"I told you kids, don't sing too late. The '業 (Karma)' of legacy code loves the vibration of out-of-tune high notes. Little girl, your MV era is over. Go back to the recycler."*

(With a single, thunderous swipe of his cleaver, the 8-Bit Girl is cleaved into millions of harmless green particles. The 'Four-Corner Game' loop collapses instantly, and the normal lights of the hallway return.)

**Xiao Mei** (staring at the Chef in awe): *"Chef... you're an exorcist too? And you use a... kitchen knife?"*

**Master Li Long** (shrugging, re-pocketing his copper coin): *"A knife is a knife. Whether it's cutting pork or cutting malfunctioning code, it's all about finding the 'Joint' where reality meets the illusion. Toby, you owe me for two boxes of bento now. This was a house call."*

## 【The Escape: Neon and Silence】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_08/Image_02.jpg)

**Little Hoop**: **「Synchronization successful. Reality restored to 100%. Master, your hunger levels have reached critical. Suggesting immediate ingestion of 'Li Long Bento' before your soul becomes as transparent as that KTV pixel girl.」**

**Toby** (collapsing onto a stool outside the booth, trembling): *"I'm... I'm really becoming a 'Monkey' who fights ghosts now? Chef, how did you even know we were in trouble?"*

**Master Li Long** (lighting a cigarette, eyes distant): *"I felt the 'Static Buffer' I gave you flicker. When the silence of the night market is broken by that kind of digital screeching, it's hard to miss. Now, get home. The Council won't be happy that Xiao Ying failed to retrieve the seed."*

## 【The Warning: A Larger War】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_08/Image_03.jpg)

**Xiao Ying** (emerging from the shadows, her holographic eye-mask cracked but her expression even colder): *"Master Li Long is right. This wasn't a game. It was a probe. The 'Strelitzia' unit now knows there's a wild master protecting the bridge. Toby, consider this your last peaceful night. Next time, the 'Goddess Garden' won't send an operative—they'll send a patch."*

(She vanishes in a flurry of pink pixels, leaving the group in the quiet, neon-lit hallway.)
