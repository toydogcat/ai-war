## 【Location: Deep in the Mountains】

(Scene: Pitch black and silent deep mountains. Master Lin (wearing a dark green traditional suit) stands with his sword, facing a tall Qing Dynasty zombie. The zombie is surrounded by an ominous green mist.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_11/Image_01.jpg)

**Master Lin**: What kind of monster are you? Why did you bring us here?

**Zombie**: Roar~ You are the sacrifices I will offer to Lord Mo-sin-a.

**Master Lin**: I don't know any Mo-sin-a, but you're finished. I am Maoshan Taoist Master Lin Ching-ying. If you kneel and kowtow now, I might spare you.

---

(Scene: The zombie makes exaggerated gestures as if complaining, revealing its terrifying face and fangs. Master Lin frowns, with twisted trees visible in the background.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_11/Image_02.jpg)

**Zombie**: Don't you already have no peach wood sword? What are you going to fight me with?

**Master Lin**: What do you want then? What will it take for you to leave?

**Zombie**: I was also a victim, later captured by Lord Mo-sin-a to do hard labor. Now I need to find substitutes to take my place.

Qiu Sheng leaps from the bus window, delivering the Maoshan bag to his master. Master Lin immediately grabs a bag of raw glutinous rice and hurls it at the zombie. Crackling explosions erupt upon impact.

---

**Zombie**: None of you shall escape!

**Master Lin**: So you're not afraid of glutinous rice? Qiu Sheng, quickly, give me more! It's very effective against zombies!

Qiu Sheng is sitting nearby, eating a glutinous rice cake while watching his master.

(Scene: Master Lin is desperately searching for raw rice, only to find Qiu Sheng sitting aside with his mouth stuffed with white rice cakes, looking innocent. The scene is full of Hong Kong-style comedy.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_11/Image_03.jpg)

**Qiu Sheng**: Master, is there none left in your bag? Then there's probably none left.

**Master Lin**: How can there be none? I prepared several bags! Qiu Sheng, what are you eating?

**Qiu Sheng**: Glutinous rice cake. Master, do you want some?

**Master Lin**: Why did you cook my rice?!

**Qiu Sheng**: Well, I was hungry. I saw the rice and made cakes.

---

**Zombie**: Are you guys making fun of me?!

(Scene: Master Lin swings a white horsetail whisk (Fuchen), which transforms into a white beam of light, striking the zombie heavily. The zombie's body twists under the impact, accompanied by intense hit effects.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_11/Image_04.jpg)

The zombie roars and charges toward Master Lin.

Master Lin pulls out the Fuchen from his Maoshan bag.

**Master Lin**: Where do you think you're going, zombie!

Master Lin uses the Fuchen to ruthlessly lash the zombie, each strike producing a loud crackling sound.

---

**Zombie**: Lord Mo-sin-a! If you don't save me now, I can't hold on much longer!!

Suddenly, a giant insectoid creature bursts from the ground, standing between the zombie and Master Lin. Master Lin lashes the creature with his Fuchen, but it seems to have no effect.

(Scene: A tall insect-like monster (Mo-sin-a), with a mix of transparent and solid parts, suddenly emerges from the ground or tree shadows. it has countless pairs of glowing compound eyes.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_11/Image_05.jpg)

**Master Lin**: What kind of thing are you?

**Mo-sin-a**: I am not a "thing." But all of you are my food.

---

Master Lin begins attacking with talismans, punches, and kicks, with Qiu Sheng joining in, but it's completely ineffective.

**Master Lin**: I'm willing to let you go. Just leave.

(Scene: Master Lin and Qiu Sheng both leap to attack the Mo-sin-a. Their fists glow with the light of fire talismans, but the monster is unscathed, responding with a mocking vibration.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_11/Image_06.jpg)

The Mo-sin-a knocks Qiu Sheng away with one punch and sends Master Lin flying with another.

**Mo-sin-a**: You seem to have a misunderstanding. You are all my food.

---

Suddenly, countless chains emerge from the sky, binding the Mo-sin-a and pulling it back into the air. Only an awkward silence remains.

(Scene: A rift opens in the night sky, and countless silver-blue electrical chains descend, tightly binding the giant Mo-sin-a and dragging it upward. Master Lin lies on the ground, watching in astonishment.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_11/Image_07.jpg)

Master Lin lies on the ground, his whole body in pain.

---

**Master Lin**: Qiu Sheng, Wen Cai, come and save your Master!

Wen Cai and the couple have just crawled out of the wreckage of Bus 645, only to see the Qing Dynasty zombie right in front of them.

(Scene: Near the bus wreckage, Wen Cai and the couple have just emerged, and they find the Qing Dynasty zombie snarling and lunging at them. The three are scared out of their wits.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_11/Image_08.jpg)

**Wen Cai**: Master, I'm coming! But there's a zombie here!

The zombie begins chasing Wen Cai and the couple. Qiu Sheng runs to check on Master Lin.

**Qiu Sheng**: Master, are you okay? What about the zombie!

---

**Master Lin**: How do you still not know how to deal with zombies? Ouch, my back... You'll have to handle it yourselves. To hide from a zombie, hold your breath so it can't see you. Then, slap a talisman on its head to freeze it. Go, save them!

Qiu Sheng immediately runs to Wen Cai and the zombie.

(Scene: Wen Cai and the couple all pinch their noses tightly, their faces turning beet red. The zombie hovers just inches from them, sniffing the air in confusion.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_11/Image_09.jpg)

**Qiu Sheng**: Quickly! Hold your breath! Everyone, hold your breath!

Qiu Sheng, Wen Cai, and the couple all hold their breath.

---

**Zombie**: Hmm? What happened? Where did everyone go?

Qiu Sheng slaps a talisman on its forehead, and the zombie freezes instantly.

(Scene: Qiu Sheng flashes in from the side, precisely slapping a glowing yellow talisman onto the zombie's forehead. The zombie freezes instantly, with the others in the background looking vastly relieved.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_11/Image_10.jpg)

**Qiu Sheng**: Wen Cai, I'll leave this to you. I'm going to check on Master.

Wen Cai deals with the zombie while comforting the couple, finally ending this terrifying ordeal.

## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 11 Supplement

### Mo-sin-a Territory

- **Location**: Unnamed Deep Mountains
- **Description**: Filled with rotting branches, dead leaves, and the smell of mucus from some giant insect. It is a junction where ancient legends meet modern mutated biological entities.
- **The "Delivery Man"**: The zombie, which brings lonely people or vehicles here in the dead of night.

### ☯️ Maoshan Demon-Slaying Team

- **Master Lin (Lin Ching-ying)**: A calm, professional Taoist Master bewildered by modern bus automation.
- **Qiu Sheng**: Has a strong sense of justice and is agile, but sometimes tries to make a quick buck.
- **Wen Cai**: Timid and easily scared, but follows Master's orders in critical moments.

### ☯️ Maoshan Artifacts

- **Peach Wood Sword**: The signature weapon of Maoshan Taoists, made of peach wood and effective for warding off evil and exorcising ghosts.
- **Talismans (Charm Paper)**: Tools used by Maoshan Taoists for spells. They can be drawn on paper, attached to objects, or burned.
- **Glutinous Rice**: Used by Maoshan Taoists against zombies. It absorbs the resentment from their bodies, rendering them immobile.
- **Bagua Mirror**: A sacred object for breaking illusions, revealing the true nature of a realm.
