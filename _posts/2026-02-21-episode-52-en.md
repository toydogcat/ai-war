## 【The Ultimate Choice: The Promise to Lily】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_40/Image_03.jpg)

(In a silent, high-tech room, Toby is brought before **Bawanghua** (Rafflesia), the mysterious Architect of the Lyra System.)

**Bawanghua**: *"Toby... you have nothing left. Your bracelet is broken, your teacher is decentralized, and your brain is a mess of conflicting versions. Why do you still ask for more?"*

**Toby** (looking up, his eyes burning with a golden fire): *"I don't care about the championship! I care about HER. Can you bring her back? Can you fix Lily Teacher?!"*

**Bawanghua** (smiling mysteriously): *"In our dimension, 'Death' is just a failure of synchronization. I can bring her back from the backup servers... but the 'Price' is an eternal training contract in the Lyra Underworld. Are you willing to be dismantled a thousand times for her?"*

**Toby** (roaring): *"I’D BE DISMANTLED A MILLION TIMES!! START THE CONTRACT!! I’M READY!!"*
