## 【Scene: Saintly Garden - Inner Sanctum】

(Perspective: Inside the crystal-white halls of the 'Saintly Garden's' inner sanctum. **Dai Shui**, the mother of Xiao Ying and a high-level delegate of the Lyra System, is looking down at her daughter with a mixture of divine love and cold logic. **Xiao Ying** is holding her pink pistol, her hands trembling.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_24/Image_01.jpg)

**Dai Shui** (voice echoing like a chime): *"My child, you have played for long enough. The 'Mortal World' is a sandbox of errors and low-level biological noise. Your talent for 'Space-Time Debugging' belongs to the Council. It’s time to format your worldly memories and return to the Source."*

**Xiao Ying** (hugging her pink gun, tears in her eyes): *"NO!! I won't go back! I like the 'Monkeys' here! I like Toby, I like Master Tofu's stinky food! Why do you have to erase everything that makes me... me?!"*

---

## 【The Soul Bargain: One Last Month】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_24/Image_02.jpg)

**Dai Shui** (condensing a droplet of holy blue-light water on her fingertip): *"I can give you one more month of 'Freedom'. One month where no one will interfere with your operations. But the price is high: after thirty days, you must accept the 'Deep Soul Link' and become the Council's Debugger. Do you accept the trade?"*

**Xiao Ying** (wiping her tears, a gamble's glint in her eyes): *"Deal! Don't you dare go back on it! If you do, you're an NPC! Chia-Seed!! Do it!! Merge my soul, link the code, I don't care anymore as long as I can keep playing for thirty more days!!"*

(Visual: The pink pistol dissolves into glowing particles, forcibly entering Xiao Ying's body through her skin. She lets out a short, pained groan before collapsing into her mother's arms, entering a deep 'Code Reconstruction' sleep.)
