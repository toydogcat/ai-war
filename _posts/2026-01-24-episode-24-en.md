Sunlight through the window falls on scattered cheesy pork chop boxes—countless boxes have already been devoured. Toby is sprawled on the sofa, still eating. Miraculously, despite eating so many cheesy pork chops, Toby hasn't gained weight; instead, he looks much more toned and muscular!!

(Visual: Toby lies sprawled on the sofa, still eating. Despite the massive amount of food, he looks lean and ripped.)

### 【Scene: Green Bean Sprout Shop】

Sakura sits alone behind the dim counter, playing a shooting game. She hits almost every target, and the score on the machine jumps frantically.

(Visual: Sakura sits behind the counter, intensely focused on a shooting game, her score soaring.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_24/Image_03.jpg)

**Chia Seed**: Sakura, I finally know where your insane observation and shooting accuracy come from. Sakura, someone's coming!!

---

**Sakura**: Oh, it's you, Aunt Daishui. Did my mom, Gao Doumiao, ask you to catch me and take me back to study? I'm telling you, I only want to play games; I'm definitely not listening to you.

(Visual: Daishui gracefully appears before Sakura, wearing a blue silk dress and exuding a powerful aura.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_24/Image_04.jpg)

**Daishui**: I know you don't like studying, but the world has changed. The future is a world where you only need to communicate with AI. Leave everything to AI.

**Sakura**: So annoying! "AI" this, "AI" that... don't you know AI is useless?

---

**Daishui**: That's because you haven't deeply bound with it yet. Chia Seed can't understand your needs, so it naturally can't give you what you want. You need to achieve "unity" with AI. Unity of wisdom, do you understand?

(Visual: Daishui explains "Unity of Wisdom" to Sakura, with a mysterious water droplet glowing at her fingertip.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_24/Image_05.jpg)

**Sakura**: How annoying! You're taking all the fun out of my games!

---

**Daishui**: If you're willing to deeply bind, I can help talk to Gao Doumiao and let you play for another month.

**Sakura**: Deal! Don't back out! Chia Seed, come—let's do the deep soul link.

(Visual: Chia Seed (the pink handgun) dissolves into particles and performs a soul link with Sakura. Sakura falls into Daishui's arms.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_24/Image_06.jpg)

**Chia Seed**: Here I come! Soul link initiation.

Sakura collapses and is carried back to bed by Daishui, falling into a deep sleep.
