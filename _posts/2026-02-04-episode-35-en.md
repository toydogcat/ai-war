## 【Scene: Hao Ye Ren Hotel - The Solar Descent】

(Perspective: The night sky above the Hao Ye Ren Hotel is suddenly torn apart by a beam of golden light as hot as the surface of the sun. The air literally begins to boil. A giant golden chariot, pulled by mechanical horses that are nothing more than concentrated fusion energy, descends from the clouds. **Sol**, the representative of the Sun-God Tribe, stands atop the chariot.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_35/Image_01.jpg)

**Sol** (voice like the crackling of a solar flare): *"Humans! Your 'Monkey School' is too slow! The solar cycle is approaching its peak, and we need the 'Golden Seed' to stabilize the biosphere's data-shield now! Hand over the boy Toby, or we will 'Overclock' this entire island until it turns into glass!!"*

**Lily** (standing on the roof, her eyes reflecting the heat): *"Sol! You’re overstepping! This is a neutral zone! You can't just arrive and heat the atmosphere to 500 degrees because you’re in a hurry!"*

**Sol** (raising a golden spear): *"In the face of the Sun, there is no neutrality. Only those who can withstand the heat are worthy of existence. Let's see if the 'Dragon-Tamer' and the 'Goddess Teacher' can protect their little monkey from the Truth of Light!!"*
