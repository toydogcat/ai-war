## 【Scene: Hao Ye Ren Hotel - Training Zone Level 1】

(Perspective: Back in the real world. The 'Hao Ye Ren' Hotel training room. The training is far from luxurious. **Wu Qiang** has set up a massive hurdle course of physical and digital obstacles. **Toby** is panting, sweat soaking his shirt, as he tries to climb a wall while dodging 'Logic Arrows' fired from a nearby drone.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_32/Image_01.jpg)

**Wu Qiang** (sitting on a bench, calmly drinking tea while holding a remote control): *"Speed up, kid! These 'Logic Arrows' are programmed with 'Delay Junk'. If you get hit, your reaction time will drop by 200ms. In a real fight, that's the difference between being a Hero and being a Pile of Data Scrap!"*

**Toby** (dodging a glowing arrow by a hair's breadth): *"BUT MASTER!! I’m a TA, not a ninja!! Why do I have to dodge physical arrows to learn how to code?! Can't we just... I don't know... read the documentation?!"*

**Wu Qiang**: *"Documentation is a lie written by the weak! True code is written with your blood and your breath! Your Golden Bracelet reacts to your physiological state. If you don't push your body to the limit, 'Little Hoop' will never unlock the 'Kernel Access'!"*
