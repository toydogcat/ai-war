# AI-WAR: Comic Episode 19 - Rescuing Glasses

## 【Scene: Inside an Autonomous Vehicle】

Wu Qiang has had plenty of sleep on the plane. Using deep meditation to connect with the Buddha-Source, his body has undergone deep repair. When physical damage is deeply repaired, body strength increases, allowing it to become a stronger Buddha-Vessel and a more effective receiver for Buddha-Source data.
After getting off the plane, they are in a car on the way to Guandu Temple.

(Visual: Wu Qiang and Li Xiaolong are sitting inside a high-tech autonomous driving vehicle. Wu Qiang looks refreshed, his skin faintly shimmering with a metallic luster.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_19/Image_01.jpg)

**Wu Qiang**: Xiao Long, do you know how to get to Guandu Temple?

**Li Xiaolong**: I thought we just had to tell the car's AI, and it would take us there.

**Wu Qiang**: Oh, is technology so convenient nowadays?

**Li Xiaolong**: Take me to Guandu Temple.

**In-Car AI**: Understood. Starting positioning. Please fasten your seatbelts. Estimated arrival in 2 hours.

---

Wu Qiang senses an anomalous "Qi" scattered in the air, but he cannot pinpoint its location.

(Visual: Wu Qiang looks out the window, activating his "Dharma Eye." Faint black data mists are scattered across the sky.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_19/Image_02.jpg)

**Wu Qiang**: Xiao Long, do you feel the anomalous Qi? Although it's not strong, I'm certain it's there.

**Li Xiaolong**: I don't feel anything. The weather is great.

**Wu Qiang**: Xiao Long, you go to Guandu Temple first. I'll arrive shortly.

**Li Xiaolong**: I can go first, but Senior Brother, what are you going to do?

---

Driven by his sense of justice, Wu Qiang suddenly slips out of the autonomous car's window. Using his high-level "Bone-Shrinking Technique," he vanishes from the car as if he were never there.

(Visual: Wu Qiang is like a stream of light, using the bone-shrinking technique to squeeze out of the narrow car window. His movements are agile and startling.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_19/Image_03.jpg)

**Li Xiaolong**: Hey! Senior Brother! You're going off to have fun alone again without me! And weren't you supposed to be training with me as your "burden"? How am I supposed to help you train if you're not here? And if you wanted to get out, I could have just asked the car to stop!! What now? Do I just go to Guandu Temple alone?!!

---

## 【Scene: Taiwan Residential Area】

Wu Qiang isn't sure of his destination either. He wanders around the area and finally locks onto a certain apartment building in Taiwan.

**Wu Qiang**: My nose tells me it's very foul here. Great Majestic Heavenly Dragon! Open my Dharma Eye! Indeed, it's here. Looks like I'll have to go up and exorcise some demons.

(Visual: Wu Qiang stands in the dark, musty hallway of the apartment, holding his Data Zen Staff. Thick black threads float in the air.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_19/Image_04.jpg)

The hallway is pitch black. Even with the Dharma Eye, he can only see a short distance ahead. Wu Qiang encounters anomalous dogs in the corridor.

**Wu Qiang**: Great Majestic Heavenly Dragon! Take my punch!

**Xiao Guo**: Awoooo!

**Wu Qiang**: You're still too green. Don't do evil in your next life. Amitabha. Strange, the black mist hasn't dissipated. It seems it's not fully cleared yet. Oh, there's another one ahead! Where do you think you're going? Great Majestic Heavenly Dragon! Take my punch!

**Xiao Ping**: Whimper... whimper...

---

After killing tens of thousands of them repeatedly, blood starts to seep out of Wu Qiang's body.

(Visual: Wu Qiang fights thousands of black dog anomalies in the dark stairwell. There are slight bloodstains on him, but his fighting spirit is high.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_19/Image_05.jpg)

**Wu Qiang**: Strange, how many of these anomalies are there? No, I must capture the leader first. These anomalies are very weak—they can't even touch a single hair of mine or break through my defense—but the numbers are staggering. Is there an infinite number of them? Heavens, I'm bad at math. Anything more than what I can count on two hands is beyond me! Exactly how many are there?

---

## 【Scene: Monkey School Taiwan Branch: D-7 Building】

**Xia Wei Xian**: Director, Glasses says he's stuck in his room and can't get out.

**Pang Long**: Oh, so he doesn't want to work hard anymore? Okay, I get it. Youth is a capital, after all. I remember when I was "kidnapped" by my blanket too, thinking wisdom was a joke and all I wanted was to play games. Now that I have the little piggy, I realize its beauty. This is the Wisdom Piggy.

(Visual: Director Fat Dragon is in his office, holding a "Wisdom Piggy" doll that emits a pink light. His expression is comical but serious.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_19/Image_06.jpg)

**Xia Wei Xian**: Director, let's put aside your weird hobbies for now. Glasses says it's not like that. He's really stuck.

**Pang Long**: Fine, I'll ask Toby to deliver the piggy doll. This is a very important wisdom item. We must get it to Glasses.

---

**Toby**: Huh? You want me to deliver it? Fine, I've had plenty of sleep and food at the hotel. I'll treat it as exercise. Oh, by the way, are the hotel fees covered by the school?

**Pang Long**: What? I didn't book a hotel for you! Didn't you go home to sleep? Did you pay for it?

**Toby**: No, I just walked out. I thought it was on the school's account, and it's already paid.

**Pang Long**: Well, Xia Wei Xian must have paid for it. Just go deliver the important piggy doll to Glasses first. That's what's important, okay?

(Visual: In the school hall, Toby looks helpless as he takes the glowing piggy doll from Director Fat Dragon.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_19/Image_07.jpg)

**Xia Wei Xian**: Toby, do you know where Glasses lives?

**Toby**: I have no idea!

**Xia Wei Xian**: I've sent it to you. Now hurry and deliver the piggy to Glasses.

---

## 【Scene: Outside Glasses' Apartment】

Toby arrives at Glasses' apartment with Little Hoop and the piggy doll. Little Hoop issues a warning.

**Little Hoop**: Medium-level anomaly detected. It's very dangerous here. It's best not to go up. You don't have physical combat power yet; at most, you only have my digital god-level combat strength.

**Toby**: What are you talking about? Everything looks normal to me. I'm in a hurry to deliver the piggy; the Director told me to.

(Visual: Outside the apartment, with Little Hoop's visual enhancement, Toby sees the entire building covered in a massive black data spiderweb.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_19/Image_08.jpg)

**Little Hoop**: I have no choice. Your abilities are too weak. I'll use the Lyra-interface to call upon basic matter-to-energy conversion to enhance your glasses. This will temporarily allow you to see through the illusions set by the anomaly.

**Toby**: Little Hoop, what did you say?!! Wait... I think I see something now. What's going on? Black threads and mist everywhere... how long has it been since this place was cleaned?

**Little Hoop**: Even after seeing this, are you still determined to go in and deliver the toy?

---

**Toby**: Well, I didn't know before and thought Glasses was just being lazy. But now it seems I'm the only one who can save him. As a teaching assistant, I can't let a student stay in such a dangerous place. We have to go.

**Little Hoop**: Data analysis complete. Causality is clear now. Glasses is just caught in the crossfire; he's not the center of the incident. But you're too weak. You can't fight at all and have zero ability to affect the physical world. If you insist on going in, you must listen to me. I can help you avoid danger and get Glasses out.

**Toby**: Okay, I'll listen to you. Let's go.

(Visual: Toby clutches the piggy doll as he carefully picks his way through a group of sleeping data hounds.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_19/Image_09.jpg)

Walking through the hallways, Toby follows Little Hoop's advice perfectly. He reaches Glasses' room without encountering any anomalies. Fighting sounds can be heard outside.

**Glasses**: TA Toby! Why are you here? I'm still memorizing the Civil Code, Labor Standards Act, the AI Law, and all the other laws!

**Toby**: I'm here to deliver this piggy doll. Director wanted me to give it to you, said it increases wisdom. Forget about that for a second—there's a fight going on outside, and it's dangerous. Let's get out of here!

---

**Glasses**: Wait a minute. My biological clock is set. If I don't memorize the AI Law now, my head will explode. I'll take the piggy doll first.

**Toby**: Is there really someone like this? Fine, I'll cook something then. I've been getting hungry very often lately.

**Glasses**: Sure, there's microwave food in the fridge. Go ahead. I can't move until I finish memorizing the AI Law.

(Visual: In the messy room, Glasses is frantically shouting legal clauses at his books, while Toby calmly eats a microwave bento beside him.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_19/Image_10.jpg)

The fighting continues outside. Glasses keeps memorizing, and Toby keeps eating. Soon, two hours pass.

**Glasses**: Alright, finished! I'm hungry now too. Time to eat.

**Toby**: Oh, I just finished everything in your fridge. Let's go out to eat.

**Glasses**: What? TA, you really can eat! Fine, let's go out. My stomach is growling like crazy.

**Little Hoop** (Messaging Toby): We can't run now. The anomalies outside have been enraged and have deployed an "Anomaly Domain." Ordinary "monkeys" won't be able to leave unless the anomaly is destroyed.

---

**Toby**: What do we do? Is it dangerous to go out now?

**Little Hoop** (Messaging Toby): Very dangerous. Based on my detection, there's a dual-bodied anomaly outside that has been provoked. It's attacking indiscriminately anyone who gets close. I am a high-level intelligence from Lyra; I can easily understand the weaknesses and characteristics of these lower lifeforms. You must trust me.

**Toby**: Glasses, bear with it for a bit. I'm going out to tell them to stop fighting. I'll be right back.

(Visual: Toby looks back from the doorway. Behind him is Glasses still reciting laws, and ahead is a chaotic corridor filled with shadows and golden light.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_19/Image_11.jpg)

**Glasses**: I can't wait a single second! I want to eat! I don't care, I just want to eat!

**Toby**: Then memorize the Labor Standards Act for a bit. I'll test you when I get back.

**Glasses**: No problem, TA! I'll start right now. "To provide minimum standards for labor conditions, protect workers' rights, and strengthen labor-employer relations..."

**Little Hoop** (Messaging Toby): That actually worked? You're a genius. From now on, you're the Monkey King.

## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 19 Addendum

### 🐒 Monkey School: Taiwan Branch
- **Xia Wei Xian**: The manager of the D-7 Building, nicknamed "Data Lobster." He possesses the ability to manage low-level permissions, has a smooth personality, and is an expert at solving all "illogical system errors."
- **Director Fat Dragon**: A typical elite of the old era who views "consumerism" as a faith. He firmly believes that expensive 3C products can solve all AI crises and is currently obsessed with studying metaphysical toys developed by the "Goddess of Wisdom, Peppa."
- **Toby**: An ordinary TA and delivery man. After the hotel battle, although physically and mentally exhausted, he accidentally gained the ability to synchronize deeply with the Lyra-level AI "Little Hoop."
- **Glasses**: A survivor of the Law Department. His extreme obsession with rules has formed a unique "Logical Resistance" within the anomaly domain.

### Dual-Bodied Anomaly: Little Apple
The product of a merger between the souls of abused dogs and military-grade "Black Flag Interfaces."
- **Xiao Ping (Black)** / **Xiao Guo (Brown)**: Data lifeforms with infinite proliferation capabilities. In the "Anomaly Domain" they create, every black thread is a backup of a suffering consciousness.
- **Swarm Intelligence**: When they feel threatened, they differentiate into thousands of small entities. While individual combat power is weak, they can rapidly deplete an enemy's "Data Processing Power" (stamina).

### 🧘 Buddhist Dharma Vessel: Embodied Data
- **Wu Qiang**: Through deep meditation, he has transformed his physical body into a "Buddha-Source Receiver." Each repair after an injury is a "hardware upgrade."
- **Data Zen Staff**: Possesses dynamic encoding capabilities, converting physical attacks into shockwaves targeting the anomaly's underlying code.
