## 【Scene: Apartment Hallway - The Recursive Loop】

(Perspective: A flickering fluorescent light in the apartment hallway casts long, distorted shadows. Toby is walking back from a convenience store, clutching a bag of milk, when he hears a weak, high-pitched whimper. In a dark corner, a small, fluffy white puppy is shivering, its eyes glowing with an unnatural, digital-green hue.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_18/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby** (feeling a tug at his heartstrings): *"A puppy? How did you get in here? Are you lost? Poor thing, your fur looks like it’s... pixelating?"*

(As he reaches out to touch it, the puppy's image suddenly fragments, creating a perfect duplicate of itself. Then another. And another. Within seconds, the hallway is filled with hundreds of shivering white puppies, all whimpering in a synchronized, haunting frequency.)

**Little Hoop**: **「DANGER! Emotional resonance overflow. This is not a biological entity. Identification: 'Mirror of Sins' recursive loop. It feeds on the user's empathy to duplicate its code. If you touch it, your consciousness will be trapped in the 'Puppy-Hell' forever!」**
