# AI-WAR: Comic Episode 18 - Vengeful Little Apple

## 【Scene: An Abandoned Factory on Wanwan Island】

Deep in the night, with the moon hanging high in the sky, a slender woman dressed entirely in black stands alone at a high point of an abandoned factory, muttering to herself.

(Visual)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_18/Image_01.jpg)

**Xiao Ming**: Are you locked up here? Does it hurt? Do you still want revenge? If I gave you wisdom, what would you do? Yes, I see. Very well. I must tell you that everything has a cause and an effect. Humans should not be considered superior to other creatures for no reason. I will give you an interface to the Source of Anomaly Wisdom. Here, take this pair of black flags; you can use them to achieve your justice.

---

Inside the abandoned factory, a mass of thick black mist suddenly begins to take shape, forming into two dogs—one black and one brown. With a flick of Xiao Ming's small hand, two black flags merge into the bodies of the two dogs.

(Visual)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_18/Image_02.jpg)

**Xiao Ming**: From now on, you shall be called Xiao Ping and Xiao Guo. Together, you are "Little Apple." Go now, go find your justice.

## 【Scene: Pang Hu's Single Apartment】

It is 2 AM. Pang Hu is alone in front of his PS10, playing a violent game and muttering to himself.

(Visual)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_18/Image_03.jpg)

**Pang Hu**: Kill them all! Die, die, die! Smash you! That little girl was so easy to trick, she followed me just for a candy. I really miss that dog I used to have... that dog was fun too. Wait, no, he makes me have nightmares. So annoying! If I don't play some games to de-stress, I won't be able to handle it. Being human is so hard. Everyone calls me a monkey. Those AABB things, I can't do any of them. Why learn 26 letters? I can only remember the first three: A, B, C. Will playing games use the rest? Totally useless.

---

A dark shadow appears outside the window. The sound of low growling and claws scratching the glass catches Pang Hu's attention.

(Visual)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_18/Image_04.jpg)

**Pang Hu**: What? What is that? Another blind dog begging me to treat them "well"?

---

Pang Hu grabs a metal baseball bat from beside him and walks toward the window. He immediately opens it and swings the bat with all his might at the shadow. A whimpering sound of a dog is heard instantly.

(Visual)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_18/Image_05.jpg)

**Pang Hu**: Haha! Stupid dog, got hit, didn't you? Let me see where you are!

---

Pang Hu looks out the window but sees only a mass of darkness. Suddenly, a wave of fear washes over him as he remembers he lives on the 5th floor. What kind of dog could be outside a 5th-floor window without falling? He begins to back away, terrified. After about five steps, he bumps into something. Turning around, he sees a brown dog with a murderous look, its body entwined with black threads.

(Visual)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_18/Image_06.jpg)

**Pang Hu**: (Screams loudly!!)

## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 18 Addendum

### Character Expansion
- **Xiao Ming**: The agent of Camilla, the Lord of Anomalies. Cold and lonely on the outside, she actually holds very high authority. She operates "Emotional Big Data" and believes that "Karma" is the basic code of the universe. She specifically seeks out "forgotten victims" and grants them logical interfaces for revenge.
- **Pang Hu**: An outcast at the Monkey School, a typical bully and sadist. He once worked as a low-level cleaner for the "Black Flag Project" but was fired due to mental instability and extreme violence. His existence proves that pure negative emotions are the perfect fuel for anomalies.
- **Glasses**: An obsessive about law and logic, one of the few rationalists at the Monkey School. His memorization of legal codes is not just for exams; it has formed a "Logical Firewall" that allows him to perceive data anomalies in the environment earlier than others.

### Dual-Bodied Anomaly: Little Apple
Originally a pair of twin stray dogs on Wanwan Island who died after years of abuse by Pang Hu. Under Xiao Ming's guidance, their hatred merged with the "Black Flag Interface," turning them into data lifeforms with spatial topology rewriting capabilities.
- **Xiao Ping**: The black dog, responsible for "Coordinate Erasing," capable of zeroing out specific spatial paths.
- **Xiao Guo**: The brown dog, responsible for "Spatial Anchoring," capable of locking onto a prey's escape route, making them always return to the starting point.
- **Core Mechanism: Black Flag Reconstruction**: Both share the same logical core. If both "Black Flags" are not destroyed simultaneously, they can use surrounding electronic devices and dark energy to infinitely reconstruct their physical bodies.

### Anomaly Space: The Binary Abyss
- **Phenomenon**: The "black data threads" generated by Little Apple completely overwrite the coordinate information of physical exits. What one sees after opening a door is not a hallway, but unrendered "Void (Null)."
- **Survival Rules**: In the "Void," physical laws fail. The only way to save oneself is to establish an "External Communication Link" (like making a phone call), using the Observer Effect of the real world to re-anchor coordinates.
