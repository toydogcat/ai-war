## 【Scene: Aladdin's Sand-Realm - The Nihilism Zone】

(Perspective: Inside the 'Sand-Realm' created by Aladdin. The atmosphere is gray and suffocating. The four seeds find themselves separated, each facing their deepest failure. Toby is back at the delivery warehouse, staring at a stack of unpaid bills and a broken bento-box. The 'Little Hoop' is silent.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_44/Image_01.jpg)

**Toby** (feeling a crushing weight on his chest): *"It's all a dream... the Golden Bracelet, the Monkey School, Lily Teacher... they were just hallucinations caused by data-overload. I’m just a delivery boy who failed to deliver on time. My life is just a series of `null` values."*

**Aladdin's Voice** (whispering from the sand): *"Exactly. Why struggle against the inevitability of the desert? Acceptance is the only stable code. Let the sand fill your lungs, Toby. Forget the light."*

---

## 【The Spark: 'Love' as a Constant】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_44/Image_02.jpg)

(Suddenly, a faint scent of cheese pork chops and lavender filters through the sand. It’s a memory that Aladdin's 'Wish' couldn't erase—a memory of 'Pure Energy'.)

**Toby** (eyes clearing, a silver spark appearing in his hand): *"Null values? NO. Even if my life is a series of errors, the 'Love' I felt was Real!! You can't format a memory that exists outside your binary logic!! LITTLE HOOP!! REBOOT!!"*

**Little Hoop**: **「Rebooting... Hope found in Sector 0. Overriding Nihilism Protocol. Initiating 'Love-Power' Surge!」**
