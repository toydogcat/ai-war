### 【Scene: Phoenix City — Inside a Private Room at the Monkey School】

Peppa just arrived from the high-end Solar Meeting, her mind still lingering on the sweetness of macarons. Before her sit the ruling brothers of the "Monkey School." This is an internal teaching meeting in a small room—a gathering for old-fashioned humans—but Peppa's mind is a bit foggy.

(Visual: Peppa sits in a luxurious private room at the Monkey School, looking dazed while eating a macaron. Opposite her sit two titans dressed in gold and silver.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_26/Image_01.jpg)

**Jin Ren**: Our revenue grew by 50% this year, all thanks to everyone's hard work! Everyone is a hero. Have you all thought of a strategy for this year's "Progress Anxiety" marketing?

**Yin Ren**: You are absolutely right; it's all thanks to your excellent leadership.

**Jin Ren**: I'm talking about strategy! Let's ask our star promoter, LuLu. LuLu, how is your collaboration with Fat Dragon going? Is he doing well?

**Yin Ren**: You are absolutely right; it's all thanks to your excellent leadership.

---

**LuLu**: What? I'm just technically assigned to D-7; I haven't actually gone there. I have a very obedient apprentice named Lili, and I'll delegate things to her. So Fat Dragon should be growing quite fat and well. As for this year's strategy, I think we should pivot the theme to "mysterious events." There have been many reports of strange occurrences lately. We can use this—there are rumors that most of these events happen to "monkeys." We can promote it as a "brain illness" related to inactivity. By using AI and learning AI, they're "curing" themselves. People will pay to stay healthy by joining the Monkey School. By this time next year, we can grow by another 50%.

(Visual: LuLu stands before a screen, presenting the marketing strategy that "mysterious events are brain illnesses." Her gaze is sharp.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_26/Image_02.jpg)

**Peppa**: Oh, right, must work hard... macarons are delicious... haha, woohoo.

---

**Jin Ren**: Good, very good! As expected of our Minister of Promotion. It doesn't matter if you come to the office or not; I know you're at D-7 because you can't bear to leave home.

**LuLu**: You know me well. I hope you can give me all of Lili's time; I've already instructed her to just take care of Toby.

(Visual: LuLu chats casually with Jin Ren, showing him a photo of Lili and Toby on her phone.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_26/Image_03.jpg)

**Jin Ren**: No problem, LuLu. I trust your judgment. Peppa, you're our partner vendor—what AI teaching tools are you selling this year?

**Peppa**: Oh, what to sell... how about macarons? Eat them and you won't get sick; your wisdom will soar.

**Brown**: Goddess Peppa, you're amazing! Let's call them "Smart AI-carons." They're actually just macarons, but if we promote them right, students can learn and progress without any pain.

---

**Jin Ren**: Brown, you're a true hero! "Smart AI-carons" will be our main focus this year. With all the mysterious events happening, the monkeys will be terrified into paying up.

(Visual: Brown excitedly shows off the packaging for "Smart AI-carons" while Jin Ren nods in satisfaction.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_26/Image_04.jpg)

**Yin Ren**: You are absolutely right; it's all thanks to your excellent leadership.

**Jin Ren**: My dear brother, I must remind you—be careful on your way home today. Phoenix City hasn't been peaceful lately; someone reported seeing vagrants gathering.

---

**Peppa**: Is the meeting over? Brown, you really understand me. Would you like to join a project I'm hosting? it's a "Growth Exchange." If you're willing, take my makeup mirror.

**Brown**: Me? How am I so lucky to be invited? And how did you know I really needed a makeup mirror?

(Visual: Peppa hands a black makeup mirror emitting a mysterious faint light to Brown. Her smile is unfathomable.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_26/Image_05.jpg)

**Peppa**: Brown, take this mirror. I must tell you, it's not just a mirror—it's a "Local AI." Remember to bind it when you get back to your hotel. Also, prepare some energy drinks or coffee; it's going to be exhausting.

**Brown**: Goddess Peppa, you're such a joker! Who uses Local AI anymore? Isn't everyone on the cloud? But since you said so, I'll definitely use it when I get back.

**Jin Ren**: You two keep chatting. I'm going to see the Great Know-It-All of Eagle Country to hand over this year's profits and report on the new plan.

### 【Scene: Outside Phoenix City — Abandoned Area of the Rare Earth Base】

Under a dark and windy sky, amidst the roar of automated mining machines, **Xiao Ming** is leading several desperate souls. This is X Company's experimental factory base. Massive "Optimus Prime" robots harvest rare earths to refine the special crystal elements used to solidify "human ore" into non-attribute crystals. Inside, there are biochemical labs for scientists and cells for "anti-human" elements, cult leaders, or prisoners of war waiting to be processed.

(Visual: The rare earth base at night. Massive automated mining robots are at work while Xiao Ming stands in the shadows.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_26/Image_06.jpg)

In a desolate area outside the lab, Xiao Ming is dressed in sleek black. Beside her are a few disheveled men led by Yannick, and Aladdin, whose clothing differs greatly from that of Eagle Country. Their eyes burn with firm conviction.

---

**Yannick**: Why do they call us "black monkeys" and make us do all this work? This is discrimination; this is abuse.

**Aladdin**: We must fight for our faith. Eagle Country's technological faith is wrong.

(Visual: Yannick and Aladdin plead for power from Xiao Ming in the ruins. A green glow illuminates their determined and fanatic faces.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_26/Image_07.jpg)

**Xiao Ming**: So you all want to borrow power from me? I've already said that the price for borrowing from the Lord of the Weird is very high.

**Yannick**: No problem, we're willing to accept it. We have the strength of animals in our blood; we want to go a step further and "go wild"—become werewolves, were-bears—to trigger our primal power.

---

**Aladdin**: We have ancient traditions—Genies of the Lamp—but controlling them isn't easy. I want to borrow the power to control Genies. In the Land of the Lamp, a man can have many wives; I can pay with as many wives as needed. Their lives are yours to take and use as you please. If it's not enough, I'll just marry more.

(Visual: Xiao Ming enters a contract with the rebels. In the background, flames erupt from the rare earth base as Yannick's arm begins to transform into a beastly limb.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_26/Image_08.jpg)

**Xiao Ming**: Fine. Since you want to rebel against Eagle Country, I can come for Yannick or Aladdin at any time. You must provide human sacrifices. We might drain their blood, discard them like trash, or toy with them until death. That is our contract. Do you agree?

**Yannick + Aladdin**: We agree.

The rare earth base comes under heavy attack. Once the staff inside realize what's happening, they send an emergency message to X Company Headquarters.

## ⚙️ System Settings: Episode 26 Addendum

### Monkey School
- **Jin Ren (Golden Man)**: Founder and actual controller of the Monkey School.
- **Yin Ren (Silver Man)**: Co-founder and Jin Ren's younger brother.
- **LuLu**: Rising star of the Monkey School, actually assigned to the D-7 building, Minister of Promotion.
- **Brown**: The second rising star, located at the A-1 Eagle Country HQ. Taken by Peppa to be an evolution seed for Rafflesia.

### 💎 Non-Attribute Crystals
The core of Eagle Country's technological faith. Scientists discovered that certain crystals can "record" and "solidify" the neural pulses of super-powered individuals.

- **Feature**: If a super-powered individual excels at fire, their ability's wavelength can be "recorded" into a crystal. An ordinary person wearing this crystal can "copy" the fire control ability directly from the human intelligence source interface, much like reading a hard drive.
- **Uses**: Achieving 100% "clean" ability yield for chips or "gravity" abilities to lighten factory items.

### ⛏️ Human Mining & Rare Earths
The darkest secret behind this technology. To produce "non-attribute crystals," rare earth minerals must be combined with "human ore."

- **Principle**: Super-powers are essentially human access to the "source of intelligence." Eagle Country uses special rare earths to maintain signal communication and "strips" the communication interfaces from living humans in an extremely cruel manner, solidifying them into crystals.
- **Consequence**: The birth of every high-quality crystal means a "gate opener" has been completely drained. In Mark's eyes, this is a "necessary price of science."
