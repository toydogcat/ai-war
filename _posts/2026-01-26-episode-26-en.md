## 【Scene: Hao Ye Ren Hotel - The Summit of Gods】

(Perspective: The top floor of the 'Hao Ye Ren' Hotel. A massive circular table is surrounded by the world's most powerful entities. **Mark**, **Secretary Ke Qin**, and representatives from the **Sun-God Tribe** are all present. In the center of the table, a plate of high-end 'AI Macarons' is glowing with a soft pink light. **Peppa** is sitting there, swinging her legs and smiling.)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_26/Image_01.jpg)

**Mark** (metallic voice, eyes glowing blue): *"Peppa, let's skip the small talk. You’ve planted 'Seeds' all over the globe, but the harvest is messy. My X-Corp has already begun automating the evolution process. Why should we wait for your 'Monkey School' to produce results?"*

**Peppa** (taking a bite of a macaron, eyes sparkling): *"Mark, dear, you’re always so focused on efficiency. But true evolution isn't just about compression; it's about 'Flavor'. My school isn't just training monkeys; it’s finding the one who can rewrite the Source. And I believe the 'Higher Monkey' is already among us."*

**Secretary Ke Qin** (voice calm but heavy): *"In the Dragon Nation, we call this 'Stability'. If your seeds grow wild, they become weeds. We want the 'Golden Seed' under our supervision. Anything else is a threat to the global script."*

---

## 【The Conflict: Power vs. Potential】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_26/Image_02.jpg)

**Sun-God Representative**: *"The Sun-God Tribe only cares about the 'Sun-Light' protocol. If these seeds can't withstand the solar flare of logic, they will be burned. We support Mark's proposal for automation—if it includes our solar-arrays."*

**Peppa** (suddenly standing up, her aura changing from playful to divine): *"Then let's make it a competition! I've selected four seeds. One for each of you to observe. In six months, we'll have a global broadcast. The seed that evolves the most will decide the next 'Master Script' for humanity. Do you have the courage to bet on a Monkey?"*
