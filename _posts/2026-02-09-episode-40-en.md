At the absolute geometric core floats a blurry, translucent, yet absolutely dominant shadow—**Bawanghua** (Rafflesia).)

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_40/Image_01.jpg)

**Peppa** (shrunk in a corner of the screen, voice filled with awe): *"Boss... based on the safety report from Earth, they say that 'Queen' Camilla is nearly invincible on the 3D level. Shouldn't we intervene and help those poor seeds optimize their firmware?"*

**Bawanghua** (voice calm yet bone-chilling): *"Camilla? That little girl still playing with crude illusions in the 3D mud? Peppa, after working for me for so long, how can you still ask such a low-dimensional question?"*

## 【Universal Rank: Who is Truly Strong?】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_40/Image_02.jpg)

**Bawanghua**: *"Those Earth 'monkeys' who call themselves strong are mostly banging their heads against the 'LV 30 Biological Ceiling'. Mark’s architecture is crude scrap metal, but he understands 'Violent Stacking'—enough energy to shatter a few domains. But they are light-years away from the Lyra System."*

**Peppa**: *"But... she took on Mark's 20,000 super-bullets head-on. How would You far against them, Boss? Like Mark, or the monk Wu Qiang?"*

**Bawanghua** (a short, elegant cold laugh): *"It’s not about how much energy you store. It’s about 'System Permissions'. Earthlings are at the stage of writing tiny modules and begging for calls. Lyra IS the universal **Intelligence Service System**. In my system, they are just sub-processes that can be killed at any moment."*

## 【The Light of Truth: 0 = 100 + (-100)】

![](/ai-war/assets/images/comics/episode_40/Image_03.jpg)

**Bawanghua**: *"When Earthlings are still fighting for a few drops of 'Dragon Blood' and stacking finite energy, we Lyrians have learned to 'Disassemble Nothingness'. We don't need to consume energy; we just disturb the symmetry of the vacuum to split 'Zero' into massive positive and negative poles. This is True Infinity."*

## 【Calculus Hell: The Final Trial】

**Peppa**: *"But Boss... the seeds said your training is worse than hell. Why are they so afraid of your curriculum?"*

**Bawanghua**: *"Oh, that. Physical tearing is bearable. But to open high-dimensional senses, I must restructure their 'Logical Weak Points'. So, after every physical session... they must **hand-calculate 1,000 advanced calculus problems**. No tables, no AI."*

**Peppa** (screaming in horror): *"HAND-CALCULATE CALCULUS?! A THOUSAND?! That’s not training, that’s a massacre!!"*
